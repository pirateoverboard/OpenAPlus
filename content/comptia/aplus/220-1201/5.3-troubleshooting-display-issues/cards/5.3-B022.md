## Question

A projector shuts down after several minutes and its fan continues running after
the image turns off. What behavior should you allow before restarting it?

## Answer

**Allow the projector to complete its cooldown period.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Projectors may need cooldown time to protect the bulb and internal components.
Repeated immediate restarts can work against the cooling process.
