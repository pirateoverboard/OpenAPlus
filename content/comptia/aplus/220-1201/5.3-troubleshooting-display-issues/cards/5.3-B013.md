## Question

A display shows distorted geometry and intermittent image problems after a new
monitor was installed. What OS settings should you verify first?

## Hint

Make sure the computer output matches what the display supports.

## Answer

**Verify the resolution and refresh rate match the monitor's supported
specifications.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

A new monitor changes the supported display modes. Checking resolution and
refresh rate is a targeted, reversible test before replacing hardware.
