## Prompt

What display symptom category is represented by this pattern, and what should a
technician avoid assuming?

## Answer

**Display artifacts or distortion. Do not assume only the monitor failed; test
the cable, display, video adapter, and driver path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Lines, blocks, and flashing color patterns can come from several parts of the
display path. The visual clue should trigger isolation, not instant monitor
replacement.
