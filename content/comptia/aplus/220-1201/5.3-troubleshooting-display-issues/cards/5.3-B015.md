## Question

A projector shows the full desktop, but the image is stretched and does not fit
the screen correctly. What should you check?

## Hint

The display may be resizing the computer output.

## Answer

**Check the display or projector scaling/aspect setting and the computer's
display resolution.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Sizing problems can come from how the output is scaled, not from a broken
projector. Match the output and scaling behavior before swapping hardware.
