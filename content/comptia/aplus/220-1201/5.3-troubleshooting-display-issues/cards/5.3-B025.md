## Question

A monitor has built-in speakers, but its manual says audio requires a separate
input for the current connection. What should you verify?

## Hint

Not every display path carries audio in the same way.

## Answer

**Verify that the required separate audio cable or input is connected.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Some display setups do not use the video cable for audio. Checking the expected
audio path avoids replacing a working monitor.
