## Question

A conference-room projector runs for a few minutes, becomes very hot, then shuts
down. What projector-specific condition should you inspect before blaming the
laptop?

## Hint

Projectors add heat variables that normal monitors do not.

## Answer

**Inspect projector airflow.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The symptom follows projector operation time and heat, not the laptop signal.
Airflow is a specific, noninvasive check before replacing or troubleshooting
the computer.
