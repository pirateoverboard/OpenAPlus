## Question

A projector overheats during meetings. What maintenance item can restrict
airflow and should be checked?

## Hint

Consider what can restrict cooling.

## Answer

**The projector filter.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Projector filters and vents can restrict airflow. That makes cleaning or service
checks more targeted than blaming the laptop video output.
