## Question

A user's screen flashes off and on when the desk is bumped. What should you
check first?

## Hint

Look for the simplest signal-path problem suggested by movement.

## Answer

**Check and reseat the video cable connections, then try a known-good cable if
the symptom continues.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Movement-sensitive flicker strongly suggests an intermittent signal path. Cable
checks are faster and less disruptive than driver changes or hardware
replacement.
