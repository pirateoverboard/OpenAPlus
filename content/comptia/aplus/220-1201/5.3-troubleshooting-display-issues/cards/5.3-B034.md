## Question

A display issue started immediately after a video driver update. What driver
action is more evidence-based than replacing the monitor?

## Hint

Use the recent change to choose the next theory.

## Answer

**Roll back or correct the video driver.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The timing links the symptom to the driver change. That makes the driver path a
better next target than replacing a monitor that worked before the update.
