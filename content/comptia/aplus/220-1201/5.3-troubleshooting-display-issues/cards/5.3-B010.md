## Question

A monitor displays video over HDMI, but the user hears no sound from its built-in
speakers. What operating-system setting should you verify before replacing the
monitor?

## Hint

The working video path does not guarantee that audio is routed there.

## Answer

**Confirm that the OS audio output is set to the display connection.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

HDMI, DisplayPort, or Thunderbolt can carry audio, but the operating system may
still send sound to a different output device.
