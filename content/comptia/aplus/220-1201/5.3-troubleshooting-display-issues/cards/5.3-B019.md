## Question

A desktop was moved, the monitor has power, and the monitor reports "No
signal." What physical display-path check should you make next?

## Hint

Movement makes a simple signal-path issue more likely.

## Answer

**Check that the video cable is seated securely.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A moved desktop can leave the monitor powered but disconnected from video. Cable
seating is a specific check before replacing the display.
