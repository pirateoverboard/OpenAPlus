## Question

You need to escalate a display issue after testing with a known-good cable and a
second monitor. What tested-path evidence should your ticket include?

## Hint

Preserve what you already isolated.

## Answer

**Document which cable, port, and display were tested and the result.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The next technician needs to know what was already isolated so they do not
repeat the same known-good tests.
