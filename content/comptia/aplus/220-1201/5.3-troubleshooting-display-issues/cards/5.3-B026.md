## Question

A laptop's built-in display is dim only when running on battery. What setting
category should you check?

## Hint

Compare what changes when the power source changes.

## Answer

**Check battery or auto-dimming power settings.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A symptom tied to battery operation points to power-management settings before
panel repair. This is separate from a display that remains faint on all power
sources.
