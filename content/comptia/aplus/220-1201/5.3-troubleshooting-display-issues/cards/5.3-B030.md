## Question

Before escalating a display issue, what recent-change detail should you include
if the symptom began after a docking or monitor change?

## Answer

**Document the docking or monitor change and when the symptom started.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Recent changes help the next technician focus on the affected display path. This
card keeps the retrieval target to one documentation detail.
