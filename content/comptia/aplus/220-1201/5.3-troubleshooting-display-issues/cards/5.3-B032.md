## Question

A user's monitor is blank on their desktop, but the same monitor displays
normally on another computer. What does that result rule out first?

## Answer

**It rules out the monitor itself as the most likely fault.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The symptom did not follow the monitor to another computer. The next theory
should focus on the original computer, cable path, input selection, port, or
configuration instead of replacing the monitor.
