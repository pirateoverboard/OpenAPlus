## Question

A monitor has one tiny spot that is always black, regardless of the app, cable,
or computer used. What is the most likely category of issue?

## Answer

**A dead pixel or panel defect, not a cable, power, or operating-system issue.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The spot stays fixed across inputs and systems. That makes the panel itself the
evidence target, while cable and OS theories become less likely.
