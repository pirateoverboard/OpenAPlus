## Question

A known-good monitor remains blank when connected to the user's computer with a
known-good cable. What does that test result suggest?

## Hint

Follow the failed test result back to the remaining side of the path.

## Answer

**The problem is more likely on the computer's display output path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

A known-good monitor and cable reduce suspicion on the display and cable. The
remaining likely area is the computer-side output, such as its port,
configuration, adapter, or video subsystem.
