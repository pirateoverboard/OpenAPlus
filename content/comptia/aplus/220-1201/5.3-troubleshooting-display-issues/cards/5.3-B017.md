## Question

A monitor issue disappears when the same monitor is moved to another desk. What
does that result help you narrow?

## Hint

Use where the symptom follows to narrow the failing path.

## Answer

**The original desk's cable, port, adapter, dock, power, or computer path is
more likely than the monitor panel itself.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Known-good movement changes the scope. This Anki card tests one result and what
it rules in or out.
