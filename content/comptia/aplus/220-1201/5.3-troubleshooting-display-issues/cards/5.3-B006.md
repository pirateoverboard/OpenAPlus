## Question

A lobby display shows a faint outline of the same menu even after the input
changes. What should you tell the user and try first if the panel is an LCD?

## Hint

Distinguish temporary image retention from a cable or input-source problem.

## Answer

**Explain that it may be image sticking and try displaying a plain white screen
for an extended period before deciding the panel must be replaced.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The symptom remains after the content changes, so the input source is less
likely. Some LCD image retention can improve, but permanent burn-in or panel
damage may still require replacement or escalation.
