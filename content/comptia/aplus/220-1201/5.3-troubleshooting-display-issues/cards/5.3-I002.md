## Prompt

What troubleshooting path does this uneven dim-display symptom suggest after
brightness and power-management settings are checked?

## Answer

**A possible backlight or panel-lighting problem, especially if only part of the
image is dim.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Settings can dim the whole display, but a visible uneven dim area suggests a
lighting fault. Check settings first, then escalate with the observed pattern.
