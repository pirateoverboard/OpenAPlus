## Question

A laptop display is visible only faintly, but an external monitor works
normally. What simple setting should you check before escalating for display
repair?

## Hint

Rule out an intentional dim setting before suspecting repair.

## Answer

**Check the laptop brightness setting.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The external monitor confirms the computer can generate video. Brightness is a
specific, reversible check before escalating for panel lighting repair.
