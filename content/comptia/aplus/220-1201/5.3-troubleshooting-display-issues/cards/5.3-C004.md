## Text

HDMI, DisplayPort, and Thunderbolt display connections can carry
{{c1::audio}} as well as video when the OS output is configured correctly.

## Extra

The troubleshooting value is checking routing and output selection, not
memorizing audio codec details.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Built-in monitor speakers may not work if the OS sends sound to another output
or the display expects a different audio input.
