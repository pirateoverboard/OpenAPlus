## Question

An older monitor connection shows a missing color channel after the cable was
unplugged and reconnected. What should you inspect?

## Hint

A physical connector problem can change image quality without changing the app.

## Answer

**Inspect the video cable and connector pins, then reseat or replace the cable
if damage is found.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Missing or damaged pins can affect color or signal quality. The recent cable
handling makes connector inspection a better first step than reinstalling
drivers.
