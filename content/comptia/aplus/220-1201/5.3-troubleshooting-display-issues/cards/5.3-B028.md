## Question

A display shows blocks and flashing colors. What known-good test can help decide
whether the symptom follows the cable?

## Hint

Change one part of the signal path at a time.

## Answer

**Test with a known-good video cable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A known-good cable is a targeted way to test the signal path without replacing
the monitor or graphics adapter first.
