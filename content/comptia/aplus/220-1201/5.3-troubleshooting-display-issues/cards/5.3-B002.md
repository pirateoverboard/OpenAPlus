## Question

A monitor remains blank after you confirm power, input selection, brightness,
and cable seating. What is a useful next test to narrow the fault?

## Hint

Use a test that shows whether the problem follows the display path.

## Answer

**Try the monitor with another known-good computer.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Testing the monitor on a known-good computer checks whether the monitor can
display an image outside the original system. That is stronger evidence than
guessing which display-path component failed.
