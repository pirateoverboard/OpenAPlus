## Question

A monitor has power and the video cable is seated, but it still reports no
signal. What monitor setting should you verify?

## Answer

**Verify that the selected input matches the connected cable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A display can be connected correctly but listening on another input. This check
is fast, reversible, and specific to display troubleshooting.
