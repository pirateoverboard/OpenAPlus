## Question

Only one graphics-heavy application shows display corruption. The desktop and
other apps look normal. What reversible test can help separate a driver path
from display hardware?

## Hint

Use a software isolation step before treating the monitor as failed.

## Answer

**Disable hardware acceleration for the affected app or test with a basic video
driver path, then observe whether the corruption stops.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

If corruption is limited to a hardware-accelerated app, the display panel is
less likely than a software or driver path. The test is reversible and gives
evidence before replacement.
