## Question

Display artifacts remain after a known-good cable is used. What next known-good
test can help decide whether the problem follows the monitor?

## Answer

**Test with a known-good display.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Changing the display after the cable test helps isolate whether the symptom
follows the monitor or remains with the computer, adapter, port, or driver path.
