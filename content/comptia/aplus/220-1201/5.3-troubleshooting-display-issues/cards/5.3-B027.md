## Question

A laptop image remains very faint after brightness and power settings are
verified, and an external monitor works normally. What failure path becomes more
likely?

## Answer

**A built-in display backlight or panel lighting failure.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The external monitor shows video output works. A persistent faint image points
toward the display lighting path rather than the whole graphics subsystem.
