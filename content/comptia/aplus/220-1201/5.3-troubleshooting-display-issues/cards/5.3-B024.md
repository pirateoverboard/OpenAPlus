## Question

A display is selected as the OS audio output, but the monitor speakers are still
silent. What local monitor control should you check?

## Answer

**Check the monitor volume or mute setting.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The operating system can route audio correctly while the monitor itself is muted
or turned down. This keeps the card focused on one local control.
