## Question

A phone is about to synchronize a large amount of business data, and the user has a limited cellular plan. What should you verify before allowing the sync over mobile data?

## Answer

Verify **data restrictions, data caps, and transfer costs**, and prefer Wi-Fi when appropriate.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Synchronization can move enough data to create cost or cap problems. The approved source specifically warns that cellular/mobile and 802.11 choices can have financial consequences.

### Real Help Desk

For managed devices, policy may disable cellular downloads or force certain network choices.
