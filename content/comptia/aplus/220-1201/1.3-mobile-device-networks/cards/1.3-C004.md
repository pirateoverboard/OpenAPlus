## Text

SIM stands for {{c1::Subscriber Identity Module}}.

## Extra

A SIM identifies a cellular device to the carrier and can contain carrier and user information.

## Instructor Notes

### Memory Trick

“Identity” is the key word: the SIM identifies the subscriber/device relationship to the cellular carrier.
