## Question

A user adds a business cloud-storage app to a phone. What two setup tasks are commonly required before data appears on the device?

## Answer

**Authenticate** to the service and **select which items to synchronize**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Business applications commonly require username/password authentication and synchronization choices before data appears on the mobile device.

### Exam Trap

Installing the app is not always the same as syncing data. Account setup and sync selection may still be required.
