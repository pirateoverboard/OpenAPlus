## Question

A mapping app reports a phone's position. Besides GPS satellites, what other network sources may help mobile location services determine location?

## Answer

**Wi-Fi** and **cellular tower** information may also help determine location.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Mobile location services can combine GPS, Wi-Fi, and cellular information. GPS is only one input into the broader location-services feature set.

### Exam Trap

Do not assume every location result came only from satellite GPS.
