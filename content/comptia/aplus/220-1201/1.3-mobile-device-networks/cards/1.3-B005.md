## Question

A phone uses a carrier identity that is built into the device and cannot be physically removed. What SIM type is this?

## Answer

An **eSIM**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

An eSIM is embedded in the device. It provides SIM functionality without a removable physical card.

### Related Cards

`1.3-B004` tests recognition of a removable SIM. This card tests the embedded form.
