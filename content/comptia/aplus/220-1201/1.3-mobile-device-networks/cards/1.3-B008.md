## Question

An organization wants to require screen locks, configure corporate email, and restrict unauthorized apps on employee mobile devices. What type of system should it use?

## Answer

**Mobile Device Management**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

MDM centrally applies mobile-device policies, including access controls, app restrictions, and corporate email configuration.

### Real Help Desk

When an enterprise phone “sets itself up,” MDM may be applying policy rather than a user manually configuring each setting.
