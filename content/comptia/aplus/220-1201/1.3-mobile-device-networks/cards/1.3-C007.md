## Text

BYOD stands for {{c1::Bring Your Own Device}}.

## Extra

In BYOD, the employee owns the device but must meet the organization's requirements.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

BYOD is attractive for flexibility, but it complicates security because personal and company data share the same device.
