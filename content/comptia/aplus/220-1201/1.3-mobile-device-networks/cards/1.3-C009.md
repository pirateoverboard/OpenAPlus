## Text

CYOD stands for {{c1::Choose Your Own Device}}.

## Extra

CYOD is similar to COPE, but the user chooses from approved device options.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

CYOD still keeps the organization inside the device-selection process; it is not the same as unrestricted BYOD.
