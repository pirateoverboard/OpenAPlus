## Question

An employee owns the phone but uses it for company email and apps. Why is this harder to secure than a fully company-owned device?

## Answer

It mixes **personal ownership/use** with **company data and requirements**, so protecting and removing corporate data is more complicated.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

BYOD devices are both personal and work devices. That creates policy, privacy, and data-protection challenges.

### Exam Trap

The ownership model matters. Do not treat BYOD as if the company has the same control it has over a corporate laptop.
