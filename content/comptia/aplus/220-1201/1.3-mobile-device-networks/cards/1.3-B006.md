## Question

A phone cannot find a new Bluetooth headset during setup. What should you check first on the headset side?

## Answer

Make sure the headset is in **discoverable/pairing mode**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Bluetooth pairing requires both devices to have Bluetooth enabled, and the accessory may need to be placed into a discoverable mode before it appears.

### Exam Trap

Manufacturer-specific button sequences vary. If discoverable mode is not obvious, check the accessory instructions.
