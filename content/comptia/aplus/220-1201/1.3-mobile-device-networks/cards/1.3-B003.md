## Question

A user has poor cellular coverage at home but has reliable Wi-Fi. Which feature may allow voice calls to work through the Wi-Fi network?

## Answer

**Wi-Fi calling**, if supported by the device and voice carrier.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Wi-Fi calling can route voice communication through a local Wi-Fi network when cellular coverage is weak or unavailable.

### Source Note

The approved source ties Wi-Fi calling to carrier support, so do not assume every phone plan supports it.
