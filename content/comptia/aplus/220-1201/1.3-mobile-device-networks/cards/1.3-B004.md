## Question

A technician moves a removable cellular identity card from one compatible phone to another. What mobile-network component is being moved?

## Answer

A **SIM**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A physical SIM is a carrier identity component for a cellular device. Moving or replacing it can change the cellular identity associated with the phone.

### Exam Trap

Do not confuse a SIM with general storage. Its primary role in this objective is cellular identification and carrier information.
