## Question

Which tool should a technician use to seat individual wires into a 66 or 110 wiring block?

## Answer

**A punchdown tool.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The tool punches each wire into the wiring block and commonly trims the excess wire during the punch.

### Exam Trap

A crimper attaches a plug to a cable; a punchdown tool is used on wiring blocks.
