## Text

A {{c1::cable stripper}} removes a cable's outer jacket before termination.

## Extra

The goal is to expose the conductors without damaging them.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

The stripper prepares the cable; a crimper or punchdown tool performs the termination.
