## Question

Users report unreliable Wi-Fi in one area. Which tool should a technician use for 802.11 analysis to identify wireless errors or interference?

## Answer

**A Wi-Fi analyzer.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A Wi-Fi analyzer examines 802.11 activity, errors, and interference; a cable tester cannot reveal the wireless environment.
