## Question

A newly terminated network cable may have an open conductor or crossed conductors. Which tool should the technician use first?

## Answer

**A cable tester.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A basic cable tester checks continuity and conductor order, making it appropriate for these wiring faults.

### Tech Wisdom

Basic continuity testing does not certify the cable's full signal performance.
