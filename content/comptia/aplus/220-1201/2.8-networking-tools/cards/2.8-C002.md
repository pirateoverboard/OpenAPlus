## Text

A {{c1::loopback plug}} returns a port's transmitted signal to its receive side for local physical-port testing.

## Extra

Loopback plugs can be made for different physical interfaces and are not crossover cables.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Returning the signal lets a technician test the local port path without a remote endpoint.
