## Question

A technician needs to send a copy of traffic from a live network link to packet-capture equipment. Which physical tool is designed for this purpose?

## Answer

**A network tap.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A physical tap sits in the traffic path and exposes a copy of the traffic to monitoring equipment.

### Exam Trap

A loopback plug tests one port's local transmit/receive path; it does not copy traffic from a live link.
