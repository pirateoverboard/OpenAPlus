## Question

An unlabeled wall cable enters a bundle of many cables in a wiring room. Which tool pair should a technician use to identify the matching cable?

## Answer

**A tone generator and probe.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The generator places a tone on the cable, and the probe detects that tone at the matching run or termination.

### Exam Trap

A cable tester checks wiring continuity after both ends are known; it is not the primary tool for locating an unknown run.
