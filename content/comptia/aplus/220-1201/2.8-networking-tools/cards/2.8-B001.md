## Question

A technician has arranged the conductors in a twisted-pair cable and inserted them into a compatible modular plug. Which tool completes the termination?

## Answer

**A crimper.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The crimper presses the plug contacts into the conductors and secures the plug to the cable jacket.

### Exam Trap

A punchdown tool seats individual wires into a wiring block; it does not attach a modular plug.
