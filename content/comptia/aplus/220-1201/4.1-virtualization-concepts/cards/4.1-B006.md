## Question

What is the purpose of a hypervisor?

## Answer

A hypervisor creates and manages the virtual platform for guest operating systems.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The hypervisor manages virtualized CPU, memory, storage, networking, and guest operation. It is also called a virtual machine manager.
