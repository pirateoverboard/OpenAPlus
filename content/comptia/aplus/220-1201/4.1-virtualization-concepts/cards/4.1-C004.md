## Text

In VM networking, {{c1::NAT}} lets the VM share the host's network connection.

## Extra

NAT converts between the VM's private address and the host's network address.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Bridged networking places the VM on the physical network. Private networking keeps the VM inside an internal virtual network.
