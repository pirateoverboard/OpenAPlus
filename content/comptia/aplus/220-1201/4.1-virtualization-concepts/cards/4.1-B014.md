## Question

Why is network connectivity a major requirement for VDI?

## Answer

The desktop session runs remotely, so the user's display, input, and application interaction depend on the network connection.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

VDI moves much of the desktop experience across the network. A weak connection can make an otherwise functional virtual desktop feel unusable.
