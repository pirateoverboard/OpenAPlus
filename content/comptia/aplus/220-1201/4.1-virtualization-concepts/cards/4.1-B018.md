## Question

Why is hypervisor security important in a virtualization environment?

## Answer

The hypervisor manages the virtual platform and guest isolation, so a compromised hypervisor could affect multiple guest systems.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The hypervisor sits between guests and the underlying hardware. Keep this card at the concept level: the exam target is why that control layer matters, not detailed exploit mechanics.
