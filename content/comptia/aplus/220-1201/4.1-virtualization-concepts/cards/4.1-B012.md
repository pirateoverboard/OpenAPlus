## Question

Why are unknown or third-party virtual machine images risky?

## Answer

They may contain unwanted software or insecure configurations, and the user may not know what is running inside the guest.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A VM image can look convenient, but it is still a complete system. Importing an untrusted image can introduce an unmanaged or malicious guest into the environment.
