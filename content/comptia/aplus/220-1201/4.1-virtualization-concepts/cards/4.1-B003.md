## Question

How do virtual machines support test and development work?

## Answer

They provide separate environments where developers can build and test software before it reaches production.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Development is where the application is built. Testing is where the pieces are checked together before release. VMs help keep both stages separate from production systems.
