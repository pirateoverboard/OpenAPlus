## Question

How do NAT, bridged, and private VM networking differ?

## Answer

NAT shares the host's network connection, bridged networking makes the VM appear as a device on the physical network, and private networking keeps the VM inside an internal virtual network.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Choose the network mode based on the communication requirement. Some VMs need outside access, some need to appear on the LAN, and some should remain isolated.
