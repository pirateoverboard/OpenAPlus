## Question

Why use a virtual machine as a sandbox?

## Answer

A VM sandbox isolates testing from the real production system, so changes, failures, or unsafe behavior are less likely to affect the host or production environment.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Sandboxing is useful for testing code, configuration changes, or suspicious behavior. Snapshots can also help return the VM to a known state after testing.
