## Question

What distinguishes a Type 1 hypervisor from a Type 2 hypervisor?

## Answer

A Type 1 hypervisor runs directly on the hardware as the primary platform; a Type 2 hypervisor runs on top of an existing host operating system.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Type 1 is often called bare metal. Type 2 is often called hosted.
