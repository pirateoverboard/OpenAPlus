## Question

Why does each guest operating system need its own security controls?

## Answer

Each guest is a self-contained computer, so it still needs normal controls such as patching, firewall rules, anti-malware, and access management.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Virtual machines are isolated, but isolation does not make them automatically trusted or patched. Treat each guest as a separate system to protect.
