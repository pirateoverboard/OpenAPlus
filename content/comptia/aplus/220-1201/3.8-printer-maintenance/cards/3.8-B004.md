## Question

Why calibrate a laser printer after toner-related maintenance?

## Answer

Calibration can adjust print density so output is not too dark or too light after cartridge changes.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Calibration is not the same as cleaning. Calibration adjusts output density; cleaning removes toner dust and paper dust.
