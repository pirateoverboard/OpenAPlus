## Question

An inkjet printout has streaks or missing sections of color. Which maintenance action is appropriate?

## Hint

Think about the small openings that place ink onto the page.

## Answer

Clean the printhead using the printer's supported automatic or manual cleaning process.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Inkjet printheads use small holes that can clog. Cleaning printheads is the objective-scoped maintenance task for streaks or missing color sections.
