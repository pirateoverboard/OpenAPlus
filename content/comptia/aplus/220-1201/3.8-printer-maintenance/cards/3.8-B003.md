## Question

What safety step matters before replacing a laser printer fuser as part of maintenance?

## Answer

Power down the printer and let the fuser cool before handling it.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The fuser uses heat and pressure to bond toner to paper. It can be hot enough to injure a technician during maintenance.
