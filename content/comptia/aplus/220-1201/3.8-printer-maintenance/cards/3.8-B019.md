## Question

What matters most when replacing tractor-feed paper in an impact printer?

## Answer

Align the paper holes with the tractor feed and position forms so text lands in the predefined spaces.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Impact printer paper must feed without constraint. Poor alignment can shift form output or stop paper movement.
