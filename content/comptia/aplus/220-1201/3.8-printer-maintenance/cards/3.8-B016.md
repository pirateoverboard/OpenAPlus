## Question

When removing debris from a thermal printer, what vacuum caution applies?

## Answer

Avoid using a vacuum unless it is designed for computer-safe use.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Thermal printers usually have paper bits and dust rather than toner. A computer-safe vacuum resists static buildup and discharge.
