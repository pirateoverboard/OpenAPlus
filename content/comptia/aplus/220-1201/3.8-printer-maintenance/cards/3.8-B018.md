## Question

Why is an impact printer printhead a wear item?

## Answer

It repeatedly strikes the ribbon and paper, takes physical abuse, and can become hot during use.

## Instructor Notes

### Maintenance Note

When replacing an impact printhead, follow the release mechanism and safety guidance for the specific printer.
