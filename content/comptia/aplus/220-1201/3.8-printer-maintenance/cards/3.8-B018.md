## Question

When replacing an impact printer printhead, what maintenance caution matters?

## Answer

Treat the printhead as a hot, modular wear item: let it cool, use the printer's release mechanism, and replace it with the ribbon for best output.

## Instructor Notes

### Maintenance Note

The printhead repeatedly strikes the ribbon and paper, so it takes physical abuse during normal use. Pairing a new printhead with a fresh ribbon helps avoid faint or uneven output after maintenance.
