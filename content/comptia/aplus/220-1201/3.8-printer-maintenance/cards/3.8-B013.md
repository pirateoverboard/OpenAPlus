## Question

What is the feed assembly's role in a thermal printer?

## Answer

It pulls paper through the printer's relatively small paper path.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Thermal printers commonly have small paper paths. Feed problems are handled by keeping the path and feed mechanism clear.
