## Question

When replacing a laser printer toner cartridge that includes an OPC drum, what handling caution matters?

## Answer

Protect the drum from light until installation and remove any packing strips before using the new cartridge or drum assembly.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Some toner assemblies include the organic photoconductor drum. The drum is light-sensitive, and shipping materials must be removed before printing.
