## Question

When should an impact printer ribbon be replaced?

## Answer

Replace the ribbon when the ink is consumed and output becomes too light.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The impact printhead strikes the ribbon against paper. A depleted ribbon produces faint output even if the printhead still moves correctly.
