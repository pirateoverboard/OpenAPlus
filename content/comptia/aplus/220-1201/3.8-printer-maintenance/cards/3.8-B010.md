## Question

When clearing an inkjet paper jam, what should a technician do after removing the main jammed sheet?

## Answer

Check the paper path for loose paper or scraps and remove all remaining pieces.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Inkjet paper paths can have turns and twists. Small scraps left behind can cause another jam.
