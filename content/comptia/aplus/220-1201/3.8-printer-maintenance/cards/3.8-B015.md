## Question

What should be cleaned when a thermal printer needs print-quality maintenance?

## Answer

Clean the heating element using manufacturer-recommended methods such as isopropyl alcohol, a cleaning pen, or a cleaning card.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The heating element creates the output on thermal paper. Cleaning methods should be gentle and documentation-supported.
