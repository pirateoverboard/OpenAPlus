## Question

Why does a thermal printer require special thermal paper?

## Answer

Thermal paper is chemically coated to change color when heated by the printer's heating element.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Thermal printers do not use ink or toner. Ordinary paper will not produce the intended output.
