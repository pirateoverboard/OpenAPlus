## Question

What consumable is normally replaced when an inkjet printer runs out of ink?

## Answer

Replace the ink cartridge or cartridges.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Inkjet printers place small drops of ink onto the page from cartridges. Cartridges are often separate by color, though some models combine colors.
