## Question

When cleaning toner and paper dust from inside a laser printer, what should a technician avoid?

## Answer

Avoid harsh chemicals, normal household vacuums, and compressed air unless the printer documentation specifically supports that method.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Laser printers collect toner and paper dust. The source-supported maintenance approach is controlled cleaning, such as wiping dust away and using approved cleaners where appropriate.
