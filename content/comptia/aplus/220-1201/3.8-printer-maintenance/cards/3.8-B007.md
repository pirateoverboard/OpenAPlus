## Question

Why can replacing an inkjet cartridge sometimes also replace the printhead?

## Answer

Some inkjet printers integrate the printhead into the cartridge, so a new cartridge includes a new printhead.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Other inkjet printers keep the printhead separate from the cartridge. Check the printer model before assuming the printhead and cartridge are one part.
