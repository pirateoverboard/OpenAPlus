## Question

What does inkjet printer calibration align?

## Answer

It aligns the nozzles to the paper so lines are crisp and colors do not overlap.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Calibration adjusts alignment. It is separate from replacing cartridges or cleaning clogged printheads.
