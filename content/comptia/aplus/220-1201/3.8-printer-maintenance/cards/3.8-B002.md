## Question

What is the purpose of a laser printer maintenance kit?

## Answer

It replaces wear items such as feed rollers and the fuser unit according to the printer's maintenance schedule or page counter.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Laser printers use heat, pressure, and moving parts. Maintenance kits address predictable wear instead of waiting for the printer to fail.
