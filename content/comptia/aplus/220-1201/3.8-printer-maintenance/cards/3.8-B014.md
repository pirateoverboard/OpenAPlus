## Question

Why should a technician track the correct thermal paper size and type for each printer?

## Answer

Thermal paper is difficult to substitute, comes in different sizes, and must match the printer.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Thermal printer paper is not interchangeable with ordinary laser or inkjet paper. The paper is part of the printing process.
