## Question

You are escalating a shared print queue or printer connectivity issue. What
should you document?

## Answer

**Affected scope, queue state, printer connection type, IP configuration, print
server status, and any relevant log entries.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Queue and connectivity problems often involve multiple layers. Clear
documentation helps the next support tier see whether the issue is client-side,
server-side, printer-side, or network-related.
