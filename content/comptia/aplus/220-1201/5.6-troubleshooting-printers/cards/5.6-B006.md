## Question

A laser printer leaves a faint repeated copy of earlier page content on the
same sheet. What does that symptom suggest?

## Answer

**The drum is not being cleaned properly during the laser printing process.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Ghost or echo images are repeated remnants from earlier drum rotation. The
important troubleshooting target is the laser imaging or cleaning process, not
an application formatting issue.
