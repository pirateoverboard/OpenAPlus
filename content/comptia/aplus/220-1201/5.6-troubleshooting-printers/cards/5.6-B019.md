## Question

Grinding continues after visible paper is cleared from the printer. What is an
appropriate next decision?

## Answer

**Escalate for maintenance or replacement according to support policy.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Persistent grinding suggests a mechanical issue beyond a simple visible jam.
Continuing to run jobs can cause more damage, so the technician should document
the symptom and escalate.
