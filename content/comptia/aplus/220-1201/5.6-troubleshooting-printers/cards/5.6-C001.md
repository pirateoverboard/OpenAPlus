## Text

For garbled printer output, {{c1::PCL and PostScript}} are examples of page
description language choices to check.

## Extra

The point is not to memorize every printer language. The troubleshooting clue
is that the driver and printer must agree on how the page is described.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

This compact recall supports the driver mismatch troubleshooting path without
turning the card into a vendor-specific driver procedure.
