## Question

A network printer is connected, but users cannot print to it. What IP
configuration should you verify?

## Answer

**Verify the printer's IP address, subnet mask, default gateway, and DNS
settings where applicable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A network printer is still a network device. Incorrect addressing or gateway
information can prevent clients or print servers from reaching it.
