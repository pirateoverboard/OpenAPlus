## Question

In printer troubleshooting, when do finishing problems occur in the print
process?

## Answer

**After the printer has applied ink or toner to the page.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Finishing includes post-print handling such as collating, stapling, binding, or
hole punching. That means the image may print correctly while the finishing
step fails.
