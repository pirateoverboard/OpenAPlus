## Question

A large office printer has a staple jam. What should the technician avoid?

## Hint

Finishing hardware varies by model.

## Answer

**Avoid forcing the finisher open or clearing it without the approved removal
process.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Finishing units differ by manufacturer. Following the approved process reduces
the risk of damaging the stapling or collating mechanism.
