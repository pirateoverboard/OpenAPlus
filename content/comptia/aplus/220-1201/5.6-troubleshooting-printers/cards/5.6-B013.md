## Question

Printed pages come out creased. What should the technician check?

## Answer

**Check the paper path and verify the paper weight is appropriate.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Creases point to a physical handling problem as paper moves through the
printer. The paper itself can also be part of the issue if its weight does not
match what the printer expects.
