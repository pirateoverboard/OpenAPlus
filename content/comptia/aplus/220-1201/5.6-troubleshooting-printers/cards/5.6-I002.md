## Prompt

What output-quality symptom is shown, and what printer process should be
investigated?

## Answer

**Ghosting or echo images; investigate the laser imaging or drum-cleaning
process.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Repeated faint content suggests old image material is not being cleared
properly. The key clue is the repeated shadow, not the document layout.
