## Question

After one specific print job is removed, the remaining queued jobs print
normally. What did that result identify?

## Answer

**That job was likely the blocking or corrupt job in the queue.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The result isolates the queue problem to one job rather than the printer as a
whole. Document the job, user, and file or application involved before closing
or escalating the ticket.
