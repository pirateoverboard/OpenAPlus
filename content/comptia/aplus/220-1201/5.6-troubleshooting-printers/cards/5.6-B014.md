## Question

Many jobs are pending for the same printer. What should you inspect first?

## Hint

Look where jobs wait before they print.

## Answer

**Inspect the print queue for a stuck or corrupt job.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

One bad job can block later jobs and make the printer look offline or broken.
Queue inspection is a low-risk first check before repeatedly sending new jobs
or replacing hardware.
