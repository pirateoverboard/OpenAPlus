## Question

Users can open the printer's web interface, but print jobs remain stuck on a
server queue. What does that result suggest?

## Answer

**The printer is reachable, so focus next on the print server, queue, or job
path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Reaching the printer interface makes a total network outage less likely. The
remaining symptom points back toward the queue or server layer that handles the
jobs.
