## Question

Printed documents are hole-punched in the wrong location. What should you
verify first?

## Answer

**Verify the printer driver settings for the finishing option.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Hole-punch placement is tied to finishing configuration. Driver settings are a
focused first check before assuming the physical finisher is broken.
