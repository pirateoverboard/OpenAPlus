## Question

A network printer has a built-in web utility. What is the support value of
using it during troubleshooting?

## Answer

**It can provide printer-side diagnostic information without relying only on the
user's application.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Printer diagnostics can show device state, configuration, or errors from the
printer's perspective. The exact utility varies by vendor, so the card tests
the decision to use diagnostics, not a model-specific menu path.
