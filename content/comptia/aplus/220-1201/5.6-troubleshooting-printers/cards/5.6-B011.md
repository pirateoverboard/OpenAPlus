## Question

A printer does not feed paper or pulls multiple sheets at once. What should you
check first?

## Answer

**Check the paper tray and paper loading.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Misfeeds can come from the tray, paper stack, or paper placement. Checking the
tray is a simple first step before assuming the printer needs major service.
