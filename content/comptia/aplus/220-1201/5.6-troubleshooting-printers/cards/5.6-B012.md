## Question

A laser printer repeatedly fails to pick up paper even when the tray is loaded
correctly. What maintenance item may be involved?

## Answer

**Pickup rollers, which are commonly handled as part of laser printer
maintenance.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

When tray loading is correct but feed problems repeat, the pickup mechanism
becomes a stronger theory. The card tests the maintenance clue, not a
model-specific roller replacement process.
