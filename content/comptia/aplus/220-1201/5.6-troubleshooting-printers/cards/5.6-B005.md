## Question

A printer produces faded prints or blank pages. What simple condition should
you check first?

## Answer

**Low toner or low ink.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Faded output and blank pages often start with consumables. Checking toner or
ink is faster and less invasive than replacing printer hardware or changing
drivers.
