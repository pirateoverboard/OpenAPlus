## Question

A printer outputs unreadable symbols instead of the expected document. What is
a likely software-side cause?

## Answer

**A bad or incorrect printer driver, including the wrong printer model or page
description language.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Garbled output can happen when the printer receives data it does not interpret
correctly. Driver and page-description-language checks are more targeted than
assuming the paper path or toner is at fault.
