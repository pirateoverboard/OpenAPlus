## Question

A printer starts making grinding noises. What category of problem should you
suspect first?

## Answer

**A mechanical problem, such as a jammed paper path or stalled carriage.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Grinding is not a normal output-quality symptom. It points to something moving
incorrectly inside the printer, so the technician should avoid sending more
jobs until the mechanical path is checked.
