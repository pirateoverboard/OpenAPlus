## Question

A printer test page is normal, but one application still prints incorrectly.
What does that result suggest?

## Answer

**Focus on the application, document output, or driver path rather than the
printer engine alone.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A normal test page proves the printer can produce clean output outside the
application. The next theory should be closer to the application or driver
interaction that creates the bad job.
