## Question

A user prints a portrait document, but it comes out landscape. What should you
check first?

## Answer

**Check the print settings and printer driver orientation settings.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Orientation is commonly controlled in the print dialog and driver. Checking
those settings is faster and safer than replacing printer hardware.
