## Question

A printer is unavailable on the network. What should you confirm before
changing IP settings?

## Hint

Start by identifying how the device is supposed to connect.

## Answer

**Confirm the printer's connection type, such as wired, wireless, or another
configured path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Printer connectivity follows the same basic path-isolation logic as other
network devices. You need to know the intended connection before deciding which
network settings or links to test.
