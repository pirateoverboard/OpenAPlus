## Prompt

What printer symptom is shown, and what configuration area should be checked?

## Answer

**Garbled print output; check the printer driver, printer model, or page
description language setting.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Unreadable symbols can mean the printer is receiving data in a format it does
not interpret correctly. That points to the driver or page-description-language
path before mechanical paper-path troubleshooting.
