## Question

A Windows print spooler repeatedly crashes. What evidence source can help
confirm the print-service problem?

## Answer

**Windows print-service logs, such as Event Viewer or Windows-PrintService
entries.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Repeated spooler failures should be documented with logs when available. Logs
help show whether a service, job, driver, or queue issue is recurring instead
of relying only on user reports.
