## Question

Orientation is wrong for jobs from multiple users even after they choose the
correct orientation while printing. What setting should be checked next?

## Answer

**Check the printer's default orientation setting at the printer console or
device configuration.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

If several users see the same orientation behavior, the printer's default
setting becomes a stronger theory than one user's print dialog.
