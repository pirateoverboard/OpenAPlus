## Question

An office printer has multiple trays, but a user's computer does not show one
of them. What should you compare?

## Answer

**Compare the user's printer driver configuration with the trays actually
installed in the printer.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Tray definitions are part of printer configuration. The tray may physically
exist, but the user's driver may not know it is installed or what paper it
contains.
