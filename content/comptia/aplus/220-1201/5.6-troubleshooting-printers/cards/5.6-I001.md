## Prompt

What printer-output symptom is shown, and what laser-printer component should a
technician suspect?

## Answer

**A vertical line down the page; on a laser printer, suspect a scratched or
damaged photosensitive drum.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Visual output defects are useful because the pattern can narrow the printer
subsystem. This image tests recognition of the page symptom and the laser
printer interpretation.
