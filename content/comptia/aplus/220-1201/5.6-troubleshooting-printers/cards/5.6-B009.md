## Question

When clearing a printer paper jam, what should you avoid doing?

## Hint

Protect the printer mechanism while removing the paper.

## Answer

**Avoid tearing the paper or damaging internal printer components.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Forced removal can leave scraps inside the paper path or damage rollers and
sensors. Careful removal preserves the evidence and prevents a simple jam from
becoming a hardware repair.
