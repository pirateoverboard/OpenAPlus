## Question

A user says one application prints incorrectly. What should you print first to
help isolate the problem?

## Hint

Use output that does not depend on the user's application.

## Answer

**Print a printer or operating-system test page.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A test page helps separate an application or document problem from the printer,
driver, and print path. If the test page prints normally, the printer hardware
is less likely to be the only issue.
