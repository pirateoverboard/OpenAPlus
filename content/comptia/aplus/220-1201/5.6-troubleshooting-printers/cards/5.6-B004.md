## Question

A laser printer produces a consistent line down the printed page. What printer
component should you suspect?

## Answer

**A scratched or damaged photosensitive drum.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Laser printers use a drum in the imaging process. A repeated line defect on
laser output is a clue to inspect or escalate the drum path rather than using
inkjet maintenance logic.
