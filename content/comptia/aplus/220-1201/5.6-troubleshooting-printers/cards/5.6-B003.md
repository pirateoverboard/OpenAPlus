## Question

An inkjet printer produces lines down the printed page. What maintenance action
should you try?

## Answer

**Clean the print heads.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

For inkjet printers, line defects commonly point to the print head or ink flow.
Cleaning the print heads is the relevant first maintenance action before
assuming the whole printer needs replacement.
