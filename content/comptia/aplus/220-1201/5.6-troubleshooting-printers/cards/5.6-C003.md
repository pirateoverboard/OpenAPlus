## Text

Pickup rollers are commonly part of a laser printer {{c1::maintenance kit}}.

## Extra

Repeated feed problems can point to the pickup mechanism after tray loading is
confirmed.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The compact recall supports printer-feed troubleshooting without requiring a
model-specific roller replacement procedure.
