## Question

A legal-size document is sent to a tray loaded with letter-size paper. What
configuration mismatch should you identify?

## Answer

**The job's page size does not match the paper size in the selected tray.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Tray selection must match the requested page size and loaded paper. This card
tests the size mismatch, not a deep printer repair process.
