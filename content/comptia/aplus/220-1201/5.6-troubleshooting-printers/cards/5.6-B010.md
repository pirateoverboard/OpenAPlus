## Question

Paper is stuck deep inside a printer and does not come out easily. What is the
safest next step?

## Answer

**Stop forcing it and follow the printer's approved jam-removal process or
escalate for service.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Printer paper paths vary by model. If paper does not release normally, forcing
it can damage internal components or leave torn pieces behind.
