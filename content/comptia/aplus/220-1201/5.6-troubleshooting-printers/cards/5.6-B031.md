## Question

You are escalating a repeated bad-output issue. What evidence should you
include?

## Answer

**Test-page result, printer type, visible defect, affected users or
applications, consumable status, and recent maintenance.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Escalation is more useful when it separates printer output from application
output and describes the exact defect. This prevents the next technician from
repeating the same isolation steps.
