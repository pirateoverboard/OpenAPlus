## Question

A printer has a jam and the manual describes a specific clearing sequence. What
should the technician follow?

## Answer

**Follow the printer's documented jam-removal procedure for that model.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Printers have different paper paths and access panels. The objective supports
using the approved process; it does not require memorizing model-specific
disassembly steps.
