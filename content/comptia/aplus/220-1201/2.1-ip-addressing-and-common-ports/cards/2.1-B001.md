## Question

An application must recover from lost data and handle messages that arrive out of order. Which transport protocol behavior fits this requirement?

## Answer

Use **TCP** because it provides connection-oriented, reliable delivery behavior.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

TCP handles transport overhead such as recovery and ordering so the application does not have to solve those problems itself.

### Exam Trap

Do not choose UDP when the requirement specifically calls for retransmission or ordering at the transport layer.
