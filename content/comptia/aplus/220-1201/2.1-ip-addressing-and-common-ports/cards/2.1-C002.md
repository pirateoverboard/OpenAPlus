## Text

UDP stands for {{c1::User Datagram Protocol}}.

## Extra

UDP is connectionless and does not provide TCP-style recovery or ordering.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Do not describe UDP as reliable delivery. Reliability decisions are left outside the UDP transport behavior.
