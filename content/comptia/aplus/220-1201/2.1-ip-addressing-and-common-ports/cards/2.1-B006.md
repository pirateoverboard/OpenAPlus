## Question

A new workstation should automatically receive an IP address, subnet mask, and other network options. Which service provides that configuration?

## Answer

**DHCP**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

DHCP provides automatic IP configuration from a server, appliance, or integrated network device such as a SOHO router.
