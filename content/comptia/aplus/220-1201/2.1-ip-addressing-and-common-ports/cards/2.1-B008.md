## Question

An email client can receive messages but cannot send outgoing mail. Which mail protocol path should you check for sending?

## Answer

Check **SMTP**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

SMTP is associated with mail transfer and sending mail from a device to a mail server. POP3 and IMAP are used for receiving mail.

### Exam Trap

Do not use POP3 or IMAP as the sending protocol just because the problem involves email.
