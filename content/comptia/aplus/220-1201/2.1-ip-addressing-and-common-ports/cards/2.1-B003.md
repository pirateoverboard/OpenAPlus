## Question

A firewall rule allows TCP 53, but DNS lookups from clients still fail. What detail should you verify in the rule?

## Answer

Verify that the rule allows the expected **protocol and port**, such as **UDP 53** for standard DNS queries in this objective.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Port numbers are not enough by themselves. TCP and UDP have separate port spaces, so the transport protocol matters.

### Real Help Desk

When a rule “opens the port” but traffic still fails, check whether it opened the correct transport protocol.
