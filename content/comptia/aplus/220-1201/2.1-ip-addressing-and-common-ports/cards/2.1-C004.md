## Text

Ports {{c1::0 through 1023}} are commonly used as non-ephemeral service ports.

## Extra

Client systems commonly use temporary ephemeral ports for individual sessions.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

A service port number does not make a service secure. It identifies where the traffic should go.
