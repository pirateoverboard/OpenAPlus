## Question

A client connects to a web service. The server listens on a well-known service port, while the client uses a temporary port chosen for that session. What is the temporary client-side port called?

## Answer

An **ephemeral port**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Servers commonly listen on stable service ports. Clients commonly use temporary ports for individual conversations.

### Exam Trap

Do not assume both sides of a conversation use the same port number.
