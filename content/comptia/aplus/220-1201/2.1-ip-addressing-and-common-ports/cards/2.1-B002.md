## Question

A real-time service can tolerate some missing data but cannot wait for retransmissions. Which transport protocol is the better fit?

## Answer

Use **UDP**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

UDP is connectionless and does not provide TCP-style recovery, ordering, or flow control. That tradeoff can fit time-sensitive traffic.

### Exam Trap

UDP is not “better” than TCP. It is better only when the scenario values low-delay connectionless delivery over transport-layer recovery.
