## Text

The private IPv4 block covering `172.16.0.0` through `172.31.255.255` is {{c1::172.16.0.0/12}}.

## Extra

The second octet runs from 16 through 31 inside this private block.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Not every address beginning with 172 is private; the private range is limited to this `/12` block.
