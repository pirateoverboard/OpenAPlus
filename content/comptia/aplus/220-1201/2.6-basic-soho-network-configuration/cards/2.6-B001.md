## Question

A SOHO workstation uses `192.168.50.20` internally and reaches the internet through its router. Is the workstation address public or private?

## Answer

It is a **private IPv4 address**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The `192.168.0.0/16` block is reserved for private networks and is not routed directly across the public internet.

### Exam Trap

Internet access does not make the workstation's local address public. A SOHO router commonly uses NAT so private IPv4 hosts can share its public-facing connection when accessing public networks.
