## Text

The private IPv4 block beginning with `192.168` is {{c1::192.168.0.0/16}}.

## Extra

Addresses in this block are common on SOHO networks.

## Instructor Notes

### Memory Trick

The `/16` prefix fixes the first two octets: `192.168`.
