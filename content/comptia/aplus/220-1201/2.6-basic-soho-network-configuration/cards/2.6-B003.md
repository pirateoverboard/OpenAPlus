## Question

A policy requires a technician to enter a device's IP settings directly and keep them unchanged until an administrator edits them. Is this static or dynamic addressing?

## Answer

**Static addressing**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Manually entered settings remain fixed until they are changed. Dynamic settings are supplied automatically and may change.

### Real Help Desk

Manual static settings require careful maintenance because later network changes must also be entered on the device.
