## Text

APIPA stands for {{c1::Automatic Private IP Addressing}}.

## Extra

APIPA supplies an IPv4 link-local address when DHCP configuration is unavailable.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Despite the word “Private,” APIPA is specifically link-local and is not forwarded by routers.
