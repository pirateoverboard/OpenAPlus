## Question

A workstation configured for automatic addressing assigns itself a `169.254.x.x` address and cannot reach routed networks. What does this address indicate?

## Answer

The workstation **could not obtain an address from DHCP** and used APIPA.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

APIPA provides local link communication when automatic DHCP configuration is unavailable.

### Exam Trap

An APIPA address is a symptom to investigate DHCP reachability; it is not a normal routed address for internet access.
