## Question

A workstation can reach devices on its local subnet but cannot reach any off-subnet destination. Its IP address and subnet mask are correct. Which setting should be checked first?

## Answer

The **default gateway**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The default gateway is the local router address used to send traffic beyond the host's subnet.

### Exam Trap

The gateway must be reachable on the local subnet; an unrelated or mistyped address cannot provide the off-subnet path.
