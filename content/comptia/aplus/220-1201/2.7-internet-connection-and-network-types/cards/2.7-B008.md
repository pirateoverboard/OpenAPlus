## Question

A phone, smartwatch, and wireless earbuds communicate over short-range links around one user. Which network type describes this scope?

## Answer

A **PAN**.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

A PAN is defined by its personal, short-range scope. It does not identify the user's upstream internet connection.
