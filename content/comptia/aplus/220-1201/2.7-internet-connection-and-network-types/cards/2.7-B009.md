## Question

A network connects sites across one city and is larger than a building LAN but smaller in scope than a broad WAN. Which network type is it?

## Answer

A **MAN**.

## Instructor Notes

### Memory Trick

“Metropolitan” is the scope cue: a MAN covers a city or metropolitan region.
