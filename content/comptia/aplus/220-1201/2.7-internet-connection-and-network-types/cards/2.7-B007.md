## Question

An organization has one network inside each office and another network that links its offices in different cities. How should these networks be classified?

## Answer

Each office network is a **LAN**; the network linking the offices is a **WAN**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

LAN and WAN describe geographic scope. A WAN commonly interconnects LANs across substantial distance.
