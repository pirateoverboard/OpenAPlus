## Question

A remote site lacks terrestrial internet service. The application tolerates added latency, and the site has a clear view of the sky. Which connection type is the best fit?

## Answer

**Satellite internet**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Satellite can reach locations without wired or terrestrial wireless infrastructure, but the long signal path adds latency.

### Exam Trap

Availability can make satellite appropriate even when another medium would offer lower latency.
