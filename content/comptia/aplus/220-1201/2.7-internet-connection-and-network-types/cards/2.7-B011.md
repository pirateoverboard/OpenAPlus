## Question

Which network type uses 802.11 to provide local connectivity and mobility within a building?

## Answer

A **WLAN**.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

A WLAN is the local wireless network. Its internet connection could separately use fiber, cable, DSL, cellular, satellite, or WISP service.
