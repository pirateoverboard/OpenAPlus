## Question

A technician shares a phone's mobile-provider data connection with a laptop while traveling. Which internet connection type supplies the upstream service?

## Answer

**Cellular internet**.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

The device-to-phone connection is local and separate from the phone's cellular upstream connection.
