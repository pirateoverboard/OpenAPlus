## Question

A rural property cannot obtain cable or fiber service. A provider can deliver terrestrial internet through an outdoor customer antenna. Which connection type is being offered?

## Answer

**WISP service**.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

A WISP uses a terrestrial wireless link and commonly uses outdoor customer equipment. Satellite service instead communicates through a satellite.
