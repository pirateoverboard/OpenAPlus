## Text

WISP stands for {{c1::wireless internet service provider}}.

## Extra

A WISP supplies terrestrial internet access over a wireless link.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

The word “wireless” does not make WISP synonymous with satellite or cellular service.
