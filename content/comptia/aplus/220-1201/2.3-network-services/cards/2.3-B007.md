## Question

A browser requests a page, and a server responds with web content. Which network service is providing the page?

## Answer

A **web server**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Web servers respond to browser requests and provide pages to clients.

### Exam Trap

Do not turn this into HTTP/HTTPS port recall; Objective 2.3 is testing the service role.
