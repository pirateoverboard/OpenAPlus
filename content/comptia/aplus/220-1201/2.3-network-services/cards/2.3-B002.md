## Question

A workstation should receive network settings automatically, but it never receives an address from the central service. Which service should be checked?

## Answer

**DHCP**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

DHCP provides automatic IP address configuration. In larger environments it is commonly centralized and redundant.

### Exam Trap

Do not turn this into manual static addressing until the automatic configuration service has been considered.
