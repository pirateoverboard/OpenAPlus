## Question

A door-security controller is purpose-built and does not normally provide direct operating-system access. What type of system is this?

## Answer

An **embedded system**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Embedded systems are purpose-built devices. Direct access to a general-purpose operating system is not typical.

### Related Cards

`2.3-B016` covers older legacy systems. This card tests purpose-built embedded systems.
