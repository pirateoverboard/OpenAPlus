## Question

A very old system still runs an important business process. What term describes this kind of older but still important system?

## Answer

A **legacy system**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Legacy systems are older systems that may still be important. Supporting old technology can still be operationally necessary.

### Exam Trap

Old does not automatically mean unimportant or safe to remove.
