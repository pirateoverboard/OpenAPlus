## Question

A security team wants diverse devices to send event messages to one central logging receiver. Which network service fits this requirement?

## Answer

**Syslog**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Syslog centralizes message logging from diverse systems. Central logs help troubleshooting and security review.

### Exam Trap

Do not confuse centralized logging with centralized authentication. They solve different problems.
