## Question

A website uses several back-end servers, but users connect to one front-end service that distributes requests. What network service is providing this distribution?

## Answer

A **load balancer**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Load balancers distribute requests across multiple servers and can help keep services available when one server fails.

### Exam Trap

Do not confuse a load balancer with a backup. It distributes active service traffic.
