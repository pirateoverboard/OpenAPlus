## Question

An enterprise wants centralized login authentication for access to multiple resources. Which type of network service is needed?

## Answer

An **authentication server**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Authentication servers centralize login authentication to resources and are commonly enterprise services.

### Real Help Desk

When many unrelated resources reject valid users, check the central authentication path.
