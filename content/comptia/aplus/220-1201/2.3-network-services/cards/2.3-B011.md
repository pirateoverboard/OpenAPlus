## Question

Clients should send web requests to an intermediate server that fetches the content and returns it, while also allowing filtering and caching. What service is this?

## Answer

A **proxy server**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A proxy performs requests on behalf of clients and may provide access control, caching, URL filtering, or content scanning.

### Exam Trap

The proxy is not the final destination service; it sits between the client and destination.
