## Question

Authentication events and log timestamps do not line up across systems. Which network service should be checked for synchronized clocks?

## Answer

**NTP**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Time synchronization supports authentication, encryption, backups, log timestamps, and troubleshooting timelines.

### Real Help Desk

When logs disagree, verify time before assuming the events happened in the order shown.
