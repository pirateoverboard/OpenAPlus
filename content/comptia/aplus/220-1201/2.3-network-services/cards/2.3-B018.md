## Question

Smart speakers, thermostats, and doorbells are being added to a network. What design choice can limit damage if one of these devices is compromised?

## Answer

Use **network segmentation** for IoT devices.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

IoT devices may require a segmented network to limit the impact of a security breach.

### Real Help Desk

Low-cost smart devices should not automatically receive the same network trust as business-critical systems.
