## Question

An organization wants to filter unsolicited and phishing email before it reaches users' mailboxes. Which service should handle that filtering?

## Answer

A **spam gateway**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Spam gateways filter unwanted or malicious email before it reaches the user.

### Source Note

This card tests the service purpose, not whether the gateway is on-site or cloud-based.
