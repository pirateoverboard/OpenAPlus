## Question

A network supports industrial control equipment for manufacturing and must not be reachable from the outside. Which service environment is this most likely describing?

## Answer

**SCADA/ICS**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

SCADA and ICS environments manage industrial equipment and require strong segmentation.

### Exam Trap

Treat these systems as safety- and operations-critical. Broad outside access is a serious design concern.
