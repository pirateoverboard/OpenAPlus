## Text

SAS stands for {{c1::Serial Attached SCSI}}.

## Extra

SAS is associated with higher-end storage systems and storage arrays.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The acronym distinguishes SAS from SATA, which is a common source of storage-interface confusion.
