## Question

Why is RAID not a replacement for backups?

## Answer

RAID can improve availability after some drive failures, but it does not protect against deleted files, malware, corruption, theft, or site-wide loss.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

RAID answers the availability problem: can the system keep using the storage after a drive failure? Backups answer the recovery problem: can lost or damaged data be restored?
