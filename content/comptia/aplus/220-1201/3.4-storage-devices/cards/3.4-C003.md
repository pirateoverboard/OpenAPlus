## Text

NVMe stands for {{c1::Non-Volatile Memory Express}}.

## Extra

NVMe is designed for SSD-speed storage and commonly uses PCIe.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Knowing the expansion helps connect NVMe to non-volatile SSD storage rather than treating it as just a connector shape.
