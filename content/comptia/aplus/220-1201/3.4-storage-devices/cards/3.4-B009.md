## Question

When would an optical drive still be a reasonable storage device choice?

## Answer

When removable disc media is required, such as for legacy installation media, offline distribution, or archival-style storage.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Optical storage is slower than modern SSDs and flash drives, but it can still matter when the requirement is compatibility with removable disc media.
