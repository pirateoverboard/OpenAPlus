## Question

What does RAID 10 combine, and what is the minimum drive count?

## Answer

RAID 10 combines striping across mirrored pairs and requires at least four drives.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

RAID 10 is often described as a stripe of mirrors. It offers performance benefits from striping and redundancy from mirroring, with capacity consumed by the mirror copies.
