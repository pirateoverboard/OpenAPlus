## Question

A user wants the fastest supported SSD option for a modern motherboard. Why would NVMe normally be preferred over SATA if the system supports it?

## Answer

NVMe is designed for SSD performance over PCIe, while SATA is an older storage interface that can limit modern SSD throughput.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

The right answer still depends on system support. An NVMe drive is not a safe purchase unless the motherboard, slot, firmware, and physical keying support it.
