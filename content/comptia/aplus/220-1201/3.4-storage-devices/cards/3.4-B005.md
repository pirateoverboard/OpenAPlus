## Question

Why is it unsafe to assume that every M.2 SSD uses NVMe?

## Answer

M.2 describes the physical module interface and keying; an M.2 slot or drive may use NVMe/PCIe, SATA/AHCI, or only the modes the platform supports.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

M.2 is a form factor/interface shape, not a protocol guarantee. Compatibility depends on the drive key, slot support, and system documentation.
