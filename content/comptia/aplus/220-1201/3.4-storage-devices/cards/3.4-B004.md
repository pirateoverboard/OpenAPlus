## Question

Why is PCIe useful for high-performance SSD storage?

## Answer

PCIe provides a high-speed bus path that SSDs can use for much higher throughput than older storage interfaces.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

PCIe storage may appear as an add-in card or through an M.2 slot using PCIe lanes. The exam point is the high-speed storage path, not a specific product shape.
