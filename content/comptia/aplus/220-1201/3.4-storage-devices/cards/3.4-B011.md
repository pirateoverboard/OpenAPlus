## Question

What does RAID 0 provide, and what major protection does it lack?

## Answer

RAID 0 stripes data across drives for performance and capacity efficiency, but it provides no redundancy.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

RAID 0 is appropriate only when speed or scratch capacity matters more than drive-failure protection. A single failed drive can break the array.
