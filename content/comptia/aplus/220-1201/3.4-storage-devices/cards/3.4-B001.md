## Question

What is the key practical difference between an HDD and an SSD when comparing storage devices?

## Answer

An HDD uses spinning magnetic media and moving parts, while an SSD uses non-volatile flash memory with no moving parts.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The moving-parts difference affects performance, durability, noise, and failure modes. Both are non-volatile storage, but an SSD avoids mechanical seek and rotation delays.
