## Question

What was the purpose of mSATA storage compared with a 2.5-inch SATA drive?

## Answer

mSATA provided SATA-based storage in a smaller card-style form factor for compact systems.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

mSATA is mainly a form-factor distinction. Modern systems more commonly use M.2, so checking the actual slot type matters before ordering a replacement.
