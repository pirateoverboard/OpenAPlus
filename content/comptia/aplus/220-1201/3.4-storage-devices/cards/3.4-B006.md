## Question

In what kind of environment is SAS storage most likely to be chosen?

## Answer

SAS is most likely in higher-end storage systems or large storage arrays that need enterprise-style drive management and throughput.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

SAS is not the normal consumer desktop storage choice. It is associated with managed storage arrays and server-class storage designs.
