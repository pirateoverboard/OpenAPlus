## Question

Why should flash drives and memory cards not be treated as the only copy of important data?

## Answer

They are portable flash storage that can be lost, damaged, or wear out, so important data still needs a separate backup.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Removable flash storage is useful for transfer and convenience. It is not the same thing as a managed backup or archival strategy.
