## Text

SATA stands for {{c1::Serial Advanced Technology Attachment}}.

## Extra

SATA is a common storage interface for hard drives and SATA SSDs. It is also
commonly shortened as Serial ATA.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Objective 3.4 compares SATA with other storage interfaces such as NVMe, PCIe, SAS, and mSATA.
