## Question

What is RAID 1, and what storage tradeoff does it create?

## Answer

RAID 1 mirrors the same data across drives for redundancy, but usable capacity is reduced because data is duplicated.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Mirroring is easy to reason about: another drive has the same blocks. The tradeoff is capacity efficiency, because mirror copies consume drive space.
