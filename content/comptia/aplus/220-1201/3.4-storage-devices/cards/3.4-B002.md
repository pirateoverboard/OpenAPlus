## Question

When comparing hard-drive form factors, where are 2.5-inch and 3.5-inch drives commonly used?

## Answer

2.5-inch drives are common in laptops and compact systems; 3.5-inch drives are common in desktops and storage enclosures.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The form factor is a physical fit requirement. Capacity or interface alone does not make a drive suitable if the bay or enclosure expects a different size.
