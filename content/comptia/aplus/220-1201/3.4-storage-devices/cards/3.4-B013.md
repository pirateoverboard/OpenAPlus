## Question

Why might RAID 5 be chosen when a storage array needs redundancy and better usable capacity than mirroring?

## Answer

RAID 5 stripes data with parity across at least three drives, allowing the array to survive one drive failure without duplicating every block.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Parity uses some capacity for recovery information, but usually less than a full mirror set. The tradeoff is parity overhead and one-drive failure tolerance.
