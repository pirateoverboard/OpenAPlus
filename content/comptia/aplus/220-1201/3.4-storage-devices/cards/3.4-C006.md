## Text

mSATA stands for {{c1::Mini-serial Advanced Technology Attachment}}.

## Extra

mSATA is a smaller SATA-based storage form factor that was largely replaced by M.2.

### Why

Objective 3.4 includes mSATA under SSD form factors, so the compact acronym recall is useful.
