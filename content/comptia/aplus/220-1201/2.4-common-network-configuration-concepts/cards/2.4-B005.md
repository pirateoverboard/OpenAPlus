## Question

Several infrastructure devices use manually configured addresses that fall inside a DHCP scope. Which setting prevents DHCP from offering those addresses to clients?

## Answer

A DHCP **exclusion** for those addresses.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

An exclusion removes selected addresses from the scope's dynamic assignment pool.

### Exam Trap

A reservation assigns a selected address to a known client. An exclusion simply prevents DHCP from distributing an address.
