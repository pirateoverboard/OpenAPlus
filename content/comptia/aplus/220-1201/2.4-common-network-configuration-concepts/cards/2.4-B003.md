## Question

A domain administrator wants receiving mail systems to follow a published policy when messages fail SPF or DKIM checks and to send compliance reports. Which mechanism should the administrator configure?

## Answer

**DMARC**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

DMARC adds handling policy and reporting around SPF and DKIM results.

### Exam Trap

SPF and DKIM supply authentication evidence. DMARC tells receivers what policy to apply when that evidence does not validate.
