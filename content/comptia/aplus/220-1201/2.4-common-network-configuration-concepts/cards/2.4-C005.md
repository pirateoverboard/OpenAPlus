## Text

DNS {{c1::TXT}} records publish text used for purposes such as domain verification and email-security information.

## Extra

SPF, DKIM, and DMARC information can be published through TXT records.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

TXT is a flexible container. The meaning comes from the structured information published in the record.
