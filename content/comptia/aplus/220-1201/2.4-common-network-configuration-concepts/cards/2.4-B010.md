## Question

Two branch-office networks need a persistent encrypted connection across the internet. Which VPN model should connect them?

## Answer

A **site-to-site VPN**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Site-to-site VPN endpoints connect one network to another, allowing devices at both locations to use the protected path.

### Exam Trap

Use client-to-site when the endpoint is an individual remote device rather than another network.
