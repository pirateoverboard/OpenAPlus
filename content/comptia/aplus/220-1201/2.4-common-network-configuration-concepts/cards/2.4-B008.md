## Question

Two departments use the same physical switching infrastructure but must be placed in separate logical broadcast domains. What should the network administrator configure?

## Answer

Separate **VLANs**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

VLANs provide logical separation without requiring a different physical switch for every network group.

### Exam Trap

A VLAN defines logical network membership; it is not merely a label applied to an unchanged broadcast domain.
