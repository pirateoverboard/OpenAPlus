## Question

What DHCP concept allows a client to use an assigned IP address for a configured period and renew the assignment?

## Answer

A DHCP **lease**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Leases let DHCP manage address use over time instead of making every dynamic assignment permanent.

### Tech Wisdom

The appropriate lease duration depends on the network; there is no universal value to memorize for this objective.
