## Question

A receiving mail system must verify the digital signature on a message by retrieving the sender domain's public key from DNS. Which mechanism provides this verification?

## Answer

**DKIM**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

DKIM signs outgoing mail, while the matching public key is published in a DNS TXT record for receiving systems to use.

### Exam Trap

SPF evaluates whether a sending server is authorized; DKIM evaluates the message's digital signature.
