## Question

Existing clients retain valid addresses, but newly connected clients cannot obtain a dynamic address. The DHCP service is running. What DHCP configuration should be checked first?

## Answer

Check whether the DHCP **scope has an available address**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The scope defines the pool available to that subnet. A running server cannot lease a new address from an exhausted pool.

### Exam Trap

Service availability and scope capacity are separate checks; a healthy DHCP process does not guarantee usable addresses remain.
