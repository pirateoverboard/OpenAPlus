## Question

A remote employee needs an encrypted connection from one laptop to the company network. Which VPN model fits this requirement?

## Answer

A **client-to-site VPN**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Client-to-site VPN software connects an individual remote device to an organization's VPN endpoint.

### Exam Trap

Site-to-site VPNs connect networks rather than a single roaming client.
