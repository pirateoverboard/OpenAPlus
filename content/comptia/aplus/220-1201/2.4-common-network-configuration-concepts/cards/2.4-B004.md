## Question

A network printer should always receive the same IP address, but the administrator wants to manage the assignment centrally on the DHCP server. Which DHCP setting should be configured?

## Answer

A **DHCP reservation** for the printer's MAC address.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A reservation lets DHCP consistently offer a selected address to a known device.

### Real Help Desk

Centralizing the assignment avoids manually maintaining the IP configuration on the printer itself.
