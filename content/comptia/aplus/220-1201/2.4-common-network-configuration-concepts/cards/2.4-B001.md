## Question

A company adds an approved outbound mail server. Receiving systems reject some of its messages because the server is not listed as an authorized sender for the domain. Which DNS-based email setting should be updated?

## Answer

The domain's **SPF policy published in a DNS TXT record**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

An SPF policy published in DNS identifies which mail systems are authorized to send for a domain.

### Exam Trap

DKIM validates a message signature. It does not define the domain's authorized sending-server list.
