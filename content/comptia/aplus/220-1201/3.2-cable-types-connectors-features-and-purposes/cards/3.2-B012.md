## Question

When does a cable connection need a converter instead of a simple adapter?

## Answer

Use a **converter** when the signal format must change, not just the physical connector.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

An adapter is enough when the signals are electrically compatible and only the connector shape needs to change. A converter is needed when the source and destination use different signal formats.
