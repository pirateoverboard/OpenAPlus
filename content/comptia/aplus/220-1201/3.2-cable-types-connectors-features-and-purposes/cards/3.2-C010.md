## Text

VGA is {{c1::analog}} video, while DVI-D carries {{c2::digital}} video.

## Extra

DVI variants matter: DVI-A is analog, DVI-D is digital, and DVI-I can include both.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

The card contrasts VGA with a specific DVI digital variant. Do not assume every DVI connector carries the same signal.
