## Question

Which cable and connector pairing is commonly associated with cable TV or cable modem service?

## Answer

**Coaxial cable with an F-type connector.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

RJ45 is the common Ethernet modular connector. F-type is the threaded connector associated with coaxial cable service.
