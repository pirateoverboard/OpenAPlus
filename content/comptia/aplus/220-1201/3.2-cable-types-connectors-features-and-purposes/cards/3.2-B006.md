## Question

Why is it unsafe to assume that every USB-C port supports the same features?

## Answer

**USB-C describes the connector shape, not one guaranteed protocol or capability.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

A USB-C connector can be used for USB data, power, Thunderbolt, or video features depending on the device and cable. The connector shape alone does not prove what signals are supported.
