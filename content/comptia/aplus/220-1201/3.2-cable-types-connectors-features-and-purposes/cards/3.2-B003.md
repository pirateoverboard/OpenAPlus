## Question

A cable will be installed in an air-handling space above a drop ceiling. What cable rating should the technician verify?

## Answer

Verify that the cable is **plenum-rated**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Plenum-rated cable is about the jacket and installation environment. The point is not higher network speed; it is using cable suitable for spaces that can move air through a building.
