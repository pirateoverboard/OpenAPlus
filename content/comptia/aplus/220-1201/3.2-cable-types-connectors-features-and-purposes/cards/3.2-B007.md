## Question

Which high-speed peripheral connection can use a USB-C connector while carrying data and power on the same cable?

## Answer

**Thunderbolt.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Newer Thunderbolt connections can use the USB-C connector, but USB-C and Thunderbolt are not the same thing. USB-C is the physical connector; Thunderbolt is the high-speed interface.
