## Prompt

Identify the highlighted fiber connector that uses a twist-to-lock style.

## Answer

**ST.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

ST is associated with a twist-to-lock style. SC is a push-pull connector, and LC is a smaller clip-style connector.
