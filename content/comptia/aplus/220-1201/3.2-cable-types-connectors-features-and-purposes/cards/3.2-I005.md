## Prompt

Identify this older small USB connector that is larger than MicroUSB.

## Answer

**MiniUSB.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

MiniUSB and MicroUSB are both older small USB connector families. MiniUSB is larger and thicker than MicroUSB.
