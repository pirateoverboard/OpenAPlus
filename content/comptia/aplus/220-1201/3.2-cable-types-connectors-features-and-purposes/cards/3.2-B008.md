## Question

A technician needs text-based console access to older equipment through an RS-232-style connection. Which connector type is commonly associated with that serial connection?

## Answer

**DB9.**

## Instructor Notes

### Real Help Desk

Serial console access still appears on some infrastructure devices. A modern laptop may need a USB-to-serial adapter to connect to that management port.
