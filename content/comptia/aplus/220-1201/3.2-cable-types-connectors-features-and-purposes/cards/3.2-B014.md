## Question

What is the purpose of a punchdown block in structured cabling?

## Answer

It provides a place to **terminate and connect individual network wires**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A punchdown block is not a plug like RJ45. Wires are pressed into the block so cabling can be terminated and cross-connected in a structured cabling area.
