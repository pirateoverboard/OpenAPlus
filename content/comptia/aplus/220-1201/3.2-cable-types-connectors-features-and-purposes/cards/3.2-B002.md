## Question

Which cable feature should a technician look for when an Ethernet cable must be installed underground between nearby structures?

## Answer

Use **direct-burial cable**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Direct-burial cable is built for underground outdoor exposure. In the 3.2 scope, it is tied to shielded twisted-pair cabling and protection from the environment.
