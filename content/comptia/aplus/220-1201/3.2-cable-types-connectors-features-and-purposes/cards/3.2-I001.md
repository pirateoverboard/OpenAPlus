## Prompt

Identify the highlighted modular connector used for Ethernet twisted-pair cabling.

## Answer

**RJ45.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

RJ45 and RJ11 are both modular connectors, but RJ45 is the larger Ethernet connector in this objective. RJ11 is associated with telephone or DSL service.
