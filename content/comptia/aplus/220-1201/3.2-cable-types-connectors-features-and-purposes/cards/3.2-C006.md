## Text

USB 2.0 and USB 3.0 are generations of the {{c1::Universal Serial Bus}} peripheral standard.

## Extra

USB is used for common peripherals such as keyboards, mice, printers, and external storage.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Objective 3.2 names USB 2.0 and USB 3.0 under peripheral cables. The key is recognizing USB as the peripheral connection family.
