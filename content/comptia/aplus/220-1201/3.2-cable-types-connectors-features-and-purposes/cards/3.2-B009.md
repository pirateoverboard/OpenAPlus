## Question

Which two video cable families in Objective 3.2 are digital connections commonly used for audio/video or monitor output?

## Answer

**HDMI and DisplayPort.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

HDMI is common for digital audio/video devices. DisplayPort is common for PC display connections and can also carry digital audio/video data.
