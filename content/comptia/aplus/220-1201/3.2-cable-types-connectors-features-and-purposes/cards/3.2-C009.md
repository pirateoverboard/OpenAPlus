## Text

HDMI stands for {{c1::High-Definition Multimedia Interface}}.

## Extra

HDMI is a digital audio/video connection common on consumer displays, monitors, and projectors.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The expansion reinforces that HDMI is a multimedia audio/video interface, not a network or storage cable.
