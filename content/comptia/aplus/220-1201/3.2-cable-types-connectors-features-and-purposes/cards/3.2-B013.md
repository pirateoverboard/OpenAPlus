## Question

Which modular connector is commonly used for Ethernet, and which is commonly associated with telephone or DSL service?

## Answer

**RJ45 is used for Ethernet; RJ11 is associated with telephone or DSL service.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

RJ45 and RJ11 look related because both are modular connectors, but RJ45 is larger and tied to Ethernet in this objective.
