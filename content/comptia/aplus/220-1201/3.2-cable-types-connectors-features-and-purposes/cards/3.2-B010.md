## Question

How should a technician distinguish VGA from DVI at a high level?

## Answer

**VGA is analog video only; DVI is a video connector family that can carry digital, analog, or both depending on the DVI variant.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Do not treat all DVI connectors as identical. DVI-A, DVI-D, and DVI-I differ by signal support, while VGA remains analog video only.
