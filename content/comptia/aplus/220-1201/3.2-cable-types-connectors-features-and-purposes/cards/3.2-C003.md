## Text

UTP means {{c1::unshielded twisted pair}}; STP means {{c2::shielded twisted pair}}.

## Extra

STP adds shielding for interference protection, while UTP is common for ordinary twisted-pair network cabling.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

This acronym pair is foundational for recognizing copper network cable descriptions in Objective 3.2.
