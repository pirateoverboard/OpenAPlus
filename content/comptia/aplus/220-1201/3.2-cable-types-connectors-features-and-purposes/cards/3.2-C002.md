## Text

{{c1::T568A and T568B}} are twisted-pair termination standards for 8P8C Ethernet connectors.

## Extra

Use a consistent termination standard for a normal straight-through cable.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

The useful Objective 3.2 concept is recognizing the standards and using them consistently. Exact pin-color order is intentionally left out of flashcards here.
