## Text

Ethernet twisted-pair cable performance is described by a cable {{c1::category}} such as Cat 5e or Cat 6A.

## Extra

The category describes manufacturing and performance requirements; it is not a brand name.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The cable category must meet the Ethernet standard being deployed. This is a better A+ recall target than memorizing every possible category table.
