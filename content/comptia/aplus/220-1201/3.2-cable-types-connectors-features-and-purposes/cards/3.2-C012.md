## Text

Common fiber connectors in Objective 3.2 include ST, SC, and {{c1::LC}}.

## Extra

ST twists to lock, SC pushes on, and LC is a smaller connector with a clip.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The objective names ST, SC, and LC connector types. Recognizing their lock styles is useful, but the exact product shape can vary by manufacturer.
