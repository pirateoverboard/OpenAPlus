## Prompt

Identify this USB connector commonly used on host ports and many peripheral cables.

## Answer

**USB-A.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

USB-A is the familiar flat rectangular USB connector. Do not confuse the connector shape with the USB signaling generation, such as USB 2.0 or USB 3.0.
