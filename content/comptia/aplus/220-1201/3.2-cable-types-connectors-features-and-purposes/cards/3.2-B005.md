## Question

For a long-distance fiber link, should a technician generally expect single-mode or multimode fiber?

## Answer

**Single-mode fiber.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Single-mode fiber is the long-distance fiber option in this objective. Multimode fiber is associated with shorter runs and less expensive light sources.
