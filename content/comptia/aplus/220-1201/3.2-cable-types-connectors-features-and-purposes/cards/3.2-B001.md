## Question

A network cable will run near equipment that creates electrical interference. Which twisted-pair cable type is the better fit: UTP or STP?

## Answer

**STP**, because shielded twisted-pair cable adds shielding to reduce interference.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

UTP is common for ordinary Ethernet runs, but the question adds an interference concern. STP is the better match when shielding is the reason for choosing the cable.
