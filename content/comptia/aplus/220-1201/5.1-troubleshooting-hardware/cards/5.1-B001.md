## Question

A desktop shows a black screen before the operating system loads and emits a repeating beep pattern. What should a technician do with that beep pattern?

## Hint

Treat the pattern as evidence that must be matched to the correct system.

## Answer

**Record the exact pattern and check the documentation for that system or BIOS.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

POST beep and code meanings vary by firmware and manufacturer. The strong
support response is to capture the evidence and use the correct reference, not
to guess from a generic code list.
