## Question

A workstation begins blue-screening during startup immediately after a new device driver was installed. What is the best next step before replacing hardware?

## Answer

**Boot into Safe Mode or another recovery path and roll back the driver or use System Restore.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The timing points to the recent driver change. A least-disruptive rollback tests that theory before opening the case or replacing parts.
