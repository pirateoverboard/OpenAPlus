## Question

Before escalating an intermittent hardware issue, what should you document about
each diagnostic step you already tried?

## Answer

**Document the result of each step.**

## Instructor Notes

### Professional Practice

Results matter more than a list of attempted actions. The next technician needs
to know what each test proved or failed to prove.
