## Question

A PC's case fans spin, but drives and the motherboard do not appear to power up and the system never POSTs. What should you avoid assuming?

## Hint

One working low-power component does not prove the whole power system is healthy.

## Answer

**Do not assume the power supply is fully healthy just because fans spin. Check power connections and power supply output.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Fans can require less power than other devices. Spinning fans are evidence that some power exists, not proof that every required output is correct.
