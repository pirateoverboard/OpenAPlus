## Question

A PC powers on, never reaches the OS, and shows no video. POST beeps are present. Which troubleshooting direction is most appropriate?

## Answer

**Treat it as a pre-boot hardware or firmware issue, then use the beep pattern and documentation to narrow video, memory, CPU, or BIOS configuration causes.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

The symptom happens before the operating system loads, so starting with Windows repair would be premature. POST evidence helps separate hardware and firmware theories from OS theories.
