## Text

{{c1::POST}} stands for Power-On Self-Test, the hardware check performed before the operating system boots.

## Extra

POST failures may appear as beeps or codes, but the exact meaning depends on the system firmware or manufacturer.

## Instructor Notes

### Why It Matters

POST is the boundary between pre-boot hardware or firmware investigation and operating-system troubleshooting.
