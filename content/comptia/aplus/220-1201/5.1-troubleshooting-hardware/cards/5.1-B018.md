## Question

A computer is running, the monitor has power, but the screen is still blank.
What signal-path check should you make next?

## Hint

Follow the display path from the computer to the monitor.

## Answer

**Check that the video cable is connected securely.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A loose signal cable is a common, reversible cause of a blank monitor. This is a
separate retrieval target from checking whether the monitor has power.
