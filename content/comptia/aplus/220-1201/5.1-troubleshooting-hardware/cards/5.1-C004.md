## Text

In hardware troubleshooting, rattling often suggests a loose component, while scraping may suggest a {{c1::mechanical drive issue}}.

## Extra

Noise clues are starting points. Confirm with inspection and diagnostics before replacing parts.

## Instructor Notes

### Why It Matters

Unusual noises are useful because they narrow the first inspection area, but they are not a final diagnosis by themselves.
