## Question

A user says the computer is running but the monitor is blank. What should you check before swapping hardware?

## Answer

**Check monitor power, the signal cable, the selected input, and brightness.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

These checks are fast, noninvasive, and common causes of a blank display. Swapping the monitor is reasonable only after the basic external checks are verified.
