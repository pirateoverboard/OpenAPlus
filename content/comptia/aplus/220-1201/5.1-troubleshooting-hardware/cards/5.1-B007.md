## Question

A user says the computer is running but the monitor is blank. What should you check first before swapping hardware?

## Hint

Rule out simple external display causes before replacing parts.

## Answer

**Check that the monitor has power.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Start with the simplest external display cause. A monitor with no power can
make a working computer look like it has failed.
