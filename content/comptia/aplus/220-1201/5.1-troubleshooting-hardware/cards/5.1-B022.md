## Question

A desktop has no signs of power after being moved. The outlet has already been
verified. What should you check next?

## Hint

Movement often affects removable connections.

## Answer

**Check that the power cable is firmly seated at the computer and power source.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

A loose power cable can make a healthy system appear completely dead. Reseating
the cable is a low-risk check before opening the case or replacing parts.
