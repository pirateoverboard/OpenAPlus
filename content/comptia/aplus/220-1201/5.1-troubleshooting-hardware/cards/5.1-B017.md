## Question

You cannot resolve an intermittent blue-screen issue and must escalate it. What information should you include in the ticket?

## Hint

Document enough evidence for the next technician to continue without starting over.

## Answer

**Include symptoms, timing, recent changes, exact error text or screenshots, Event Viewer details, diagnostics run, and results of each step.**

## Instructor Notes

### Professional Practice

Escalation is not just "send it up." Good notes let the next technician continue the investigation without repeating all discovery work.
