## Question

You cannot resolve an intermittent blue-screen issue. What exact user-visible
evidence should you include in the escalation ticket?

## Hint

Capture the evidence the next technician cannot infer later.

## Answer

**Include the exact error text or screenshots.**

## Instructor Notes

### Professional Practice

Exact messages and screenshots preserve evidence that may disappear after a
restart. They are a concrete handoff item, not a vague "it blue-screened"
summary.
