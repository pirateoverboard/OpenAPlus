## Text

A Windows Stop Error is commonly called a {{c1::Blue Screen of Death}} or BSOD.

## Extra

The screen and event logs can provide useful details for troubleshooting or escalation.

## Instructor Notes

### Why It Matters

The term is common in support tickets and interviews, but the useful action is to capture the error details rather than just naming the screen.
