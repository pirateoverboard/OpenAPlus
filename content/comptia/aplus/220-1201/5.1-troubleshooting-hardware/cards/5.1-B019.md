## Question

A powered monitor reports that no signal is detected even though the video cable
is seated. What monitor setting should you verify?

## Answer

**Verify that the monitor is set to the correct input source.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A monitor can be connected correctly but listening to the wrong input. Checking
input selection is faster and safer than replacing the display or graphics
adapter.
