## Question

A user says their computer is "slow today." What tool should you check first to narrow whether CPU, memory, or disk activity is high?

## Hint

Start with a quick way to measure which resource is under pressure.

## Answer

**Task Manager.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Task Manager gives quick evidence about resource use. That evidence is better than guessing between malware, disk pressure, updates, a bad application, or hardware failure.
