## Question

A desktop still has no signs of power after you confirm a working outlet and a
seated power cable. What power-supply control should you check if the system
has one?

## Answer

**Check that the power supply's rear power switch is on.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

Some desktop power supplies have a rear switch that can be turned off during a
move or while handling cables. This is still a simple external check before
assuming an internal failure.
