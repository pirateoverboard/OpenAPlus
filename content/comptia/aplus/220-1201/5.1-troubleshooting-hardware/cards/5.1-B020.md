## Question

A workstation powers off during heavy workloads. What log source can help show
whether the shutdown was recorded by the operating system?

## Hint

Use operating-system evidence to separate recorded errors from sudden loss of power.

## Answer

**Event Viewer.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Event Viewer can preserve shutdown or hardware-related events that help narrow
the issue. It does not replace thermal or hardware checks, but it is a specific
piece of evidence for escalation.
