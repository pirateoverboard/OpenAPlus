## Question

A desktop makes a clicking sound that changes speed when internal fan speed changes. What component should you inspect first?

## Answer

**Inspect the fans for obstruction, bearing problems, or contact with a cable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

Noise type and timing matter. A clicking sound tied to fan speed is more useful evidence than a broad "replace the computer" conclusion.
