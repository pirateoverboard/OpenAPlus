## Question

A desktop loses the correct date, time, and firmware settings every time it is unplugged. What is the most likely hardware cause?

## Answer

**A failed motherboard battery.**

## Instructor Notes

### Symptom Pattern

The motherboard battery maintains firmware settings while the system is powered off. Replacing it is more targeted than reinstalling the OS or replacing storage.
