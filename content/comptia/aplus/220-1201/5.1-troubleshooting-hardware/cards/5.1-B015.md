## Question

Only one application closes unexpectedly; the rest of Windows remains stable. What should you check before reinstalling the application?

## Answer

**Check the application's error message, Event Viewer, and Reliability Monitor.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Application crashes are narrower than system crashes. Logs and Reliability Monitor can show patterns and error details that guide the next step.
