## Question

Only one application closes unexpectedly; the rest of Windows remains stable. What should you check before reinstalling the application?

## Hint

Capture evidence from the affected application first.

## Answer

**Record the exact application error message.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Application crashes are narrower than system crashes. The exact message gives a
specific symptom to search, document, or escalate instead of treating the whole
computer as unstable.
