## Question

A workstation randomly powers off with no warning. The user says it happens more often during video rendering. Which evidence should you gather next?

## Answer

**Check Event Viewer, fan status, heat sinks, and temperature readings during or after load.**

## Instructor Notes

### Narrowing

The load pattern makes heat a strong theory, but logs and diagnostics still matter. A good technician gathers evidence before replacing parts.
