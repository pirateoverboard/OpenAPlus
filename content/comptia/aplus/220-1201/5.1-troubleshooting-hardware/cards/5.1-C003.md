## Text

A system that loses date, time, or firmware settings on every boot often has a failed {{c1::motherboard battery}}.

## Extra

The same symptom pattern can appear after the system has been unplugged long enough for the weak battery to matter.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Do not jump to OS repair when the symptom is firmware settings and time being lost before the OS matters.
