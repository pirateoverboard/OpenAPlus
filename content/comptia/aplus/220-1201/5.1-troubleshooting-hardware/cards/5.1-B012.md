## Question

A gaming PC shuts down after several minutes of heavy graphics load but works at the desktop. What should you check first?

## Hint

A problem that appears under sustained load often points to stress or heat.

## Answer

**Check whether the cooling fans are spinning when the system is under load.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

Load-dependent shutdowns often point toward heat. A non-spinning or stalled fan
is a concrete cooling fault to confirm before replacing the graphics card or
power supply.
