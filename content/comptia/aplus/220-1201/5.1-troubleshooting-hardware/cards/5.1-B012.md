## Question

A gaming PC shuts down after several minutes of heavy graphics load but works at the desktop. What should you check first?

## Answer

**Check cooling: fans, airflow, heat sinks, dust blockage, and temperature readings.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

Load-dependent shutdowns often point toward heat. Confirm the temperature and cooling theory before replacing the graphics card or power supply.
