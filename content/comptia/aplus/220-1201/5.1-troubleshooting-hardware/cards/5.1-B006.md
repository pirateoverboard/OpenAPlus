## Question

A user reports, "The program gave me an error and closed," but they do not know what the error said. What should the technician ask for first?

## Hint

Improve the quality of the evidence before proposing a fix.

## Answer

**Ask for the exact error text or a screenshot, plus what the user was doing when it appeared.**

## Instructor Notes

### Interview Angle

Good support starts by improving the quality of the evidence. Exact text, screenshots, and reproduction context are more useful than a vague ticket note.
