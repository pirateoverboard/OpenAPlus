## Question

A PC shuts down during heavy load and the fans are spinning. What cooling issue
should you visually check next?

## Hint

Air must be able to move through the system, not just spin a fan.

## Answer

**Check for blocked vents or dust buildup restricting airflow.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

Working fans do not prove the system can remove heat. Blocked vents or dust can
still cause load-related shutdowns by preventing airflow through the case or
heat sink area.
