## Question

A desktop has no lights, no fans, and no response when the power button is pressed. What is the first area to verify?

## Answer

**Verify the power source path: outlet, power strip, power cable, and power supply input.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

No signs of life can be caused outside the computer. Checking the power path first avoids replacing internal components when the system is not receiving power.
