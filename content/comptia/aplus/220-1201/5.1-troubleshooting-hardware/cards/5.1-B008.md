## Question

A desktop has no lights, no fans, and no response when the power button is pressed. What is the first area to verify?

## Hint

Start with the simplest source of power outside the computer.

## Answer

**Verify that the outlet or power strip is actually providing power.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

No signs of life can be caused before power ever reaches the computer. Testing
the outlet or power strip first prevents unnecessary internal hardware work.
