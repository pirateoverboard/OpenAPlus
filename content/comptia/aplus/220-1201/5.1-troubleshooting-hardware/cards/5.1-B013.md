## Question

A user reports smoke and a burning smell from a desktop. What is the safest first action?

## Hint

When safety is involved, stop normal troubleshooting before gathering more data.

## Answer

**Disconnect power and stop using the system until the damaged component is found and replaced.**

## Instructor Notes

### Safety

Burning odor is not a normal troubleshooting state. Do not keep power applied to "see if it still works."
