## Question

A laptop performs normally on AC power but becomes sluggish when running on battery. What setting should you check before replacing hardware?

## Answer

**Check for a power-saving mode or policy that throttles performance on battery.**

## Instructor Notes

### Support Logic

The symptom changes with the power state. That points toward power management before CPU, memory, or storage replacement.
