## Question

Which customer-premises device connects a local network to broadband service delivered over a cable-provider network?

## Answer

A **cable modem**.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

This card identifies the cable-service handoff device. Provider speed tiers and DOCSIS versions are separate implementation details.
