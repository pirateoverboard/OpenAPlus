## Question

A new installation must centrally provide Ethernet connectivity and power to several IP cameras. Which device best combines both functions?

## Answer

A compatible **PoE switch**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A PoE switch supplies data and power from its switch ports, avoiding a separate injector for every camera.
