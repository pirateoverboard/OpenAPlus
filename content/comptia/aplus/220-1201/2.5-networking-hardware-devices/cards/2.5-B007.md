## Question

An existing switch does not provide PoE, but one ceiling-mounted access point needs power and data over its Ethernet cable. What should the technician add?

## Answer

A compatible **PoE injector**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

An injector adds power inline without requiring replacement of a non-PoE switch for a single powered device.

### Exam Trap

The injector and access point must support compatible PoE standards.
