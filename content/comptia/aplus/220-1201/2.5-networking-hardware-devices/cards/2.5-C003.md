## Text

PoE stands for {{c1::Power over Ethernet}}.

## Extra

PoE can carry electrical power and network data over an Ethernet connection.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

PoE can simplify installation where a separate nearby power outlet is inconvenient, but the source and powered device must support compatible standards.
