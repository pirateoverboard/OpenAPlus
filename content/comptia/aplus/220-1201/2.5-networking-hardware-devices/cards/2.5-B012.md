## Question

Fiber service reaches a customer's premises. Which device terminates the provider's optical connection and presents the customer-side network handoff?

## Answer

An **optical network terminal (ONT)**.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Use an ONT for the fiber handoff. Cable and DSL services use different customer-premises devices.
