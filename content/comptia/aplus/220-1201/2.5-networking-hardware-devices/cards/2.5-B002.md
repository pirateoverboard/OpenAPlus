## Question

Which device connects multiple wired devices on a LAN and forwards Ethernet frames according to MAC addresses?

## Answer

A **switch**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Switching provides local Ethernet connectivity. A router is needed when traffic must cross an IP subnet boundary.
