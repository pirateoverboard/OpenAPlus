## Question

Which customer-premises device provides broadband connectivity over a telephone-line connection?

## Answer

A **DSL modem**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

DSL uses telephone-line infrastructure. Detailed speed and distance comparisons belong to internet connection types rather than this hardware-selection card.
