## Question

A powered device requires PoE++, but the switch supports only PoE+. What should the technician provide before deployment?

## Answer

Provide a power source that supports the device's required **PoE++ standard**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The source standard must meet the powered device's requirement. A lower-power PoE tier cannot satisfy a higher-tier requirement.

### Tech Wisdom

Confirm standards compatibility rather than assuming that every port labeled PoE supplies the same capability.
