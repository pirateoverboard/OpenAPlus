## Question

Which network device applies rules to allow or block traffic at a network boundary?

## Answer

A **firewall**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Firewalls enforce traffic policy. Depending on their capabilities, they may evaluate transport information, applications, or both.

### Exam Trap

A router forwards between networks; routing alone does not describe policy-based traffic filtering.
