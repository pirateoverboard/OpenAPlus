## Question

Which device bridges wireless clients onto an existing wired network without inherently providing routing?

## Answer

An **access point**.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

A consumer wireless router may combine an access point, router, switch, and firewall. A standalone access point supplies the wireless bridge function.
