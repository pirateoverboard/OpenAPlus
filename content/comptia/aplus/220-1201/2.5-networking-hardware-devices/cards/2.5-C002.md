## Text

In a physical MAC address, MAC stands for {{c1::media access control}}.

## Extra

Switches use MAC addresses when forwarding Ethernet frames on a local network.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Do not confuse the NIC's MAC address with its network-layer IP address.
