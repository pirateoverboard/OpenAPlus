## Question

A network requires VLAN configuration, traffic prioritization, port mirroring, and remote administration. Should the technician install a managed or unmanaged switch?

## Answer

A **managed switch**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Those requirements depend on administrative and monitoring features provided by managed switches.

### Exam Trap

An unmanaged switch is appropriate for simple plug-and-play connectivity, not networks requiring configuration or visibility.
