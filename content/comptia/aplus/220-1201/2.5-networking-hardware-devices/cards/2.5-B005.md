## Question

Which passive component terminates permanent network cable runs and lets technicians patch those runs to switch ports?

## Answer

A **patch panel**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The patch panel organizes fixed cabling while short patch cables provide changeable connections to the switch.

### Exam Trap

A patch panel does not forward, route, or filter traffic.
