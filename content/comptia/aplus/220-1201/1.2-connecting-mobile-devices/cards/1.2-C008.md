## Text

A port replicator is similar to a docking station, but it commonly lacks {{c1::expansion card}} capability.

## Extra

Port replicators are usually simpler connectivity expanders, often connected by USB.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

If the question emphasizes adding desktop-style expansion hardware, a docking station is usually the stronger answer.
