## Question

A user buys a digital stylus for a tablet, but pressure-sensitive drawing and button features do not work. What is the best first thing to verify?

## Answer

Verify that the **stylus is compatible with that tablet model or device family**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Advanced stylus features depend on direct communication between the stylus and the device. A generic stylus may not support device-specific features.

### Exam Trap

Do not assume any pointed input tool is equivalent to an active stylus.
