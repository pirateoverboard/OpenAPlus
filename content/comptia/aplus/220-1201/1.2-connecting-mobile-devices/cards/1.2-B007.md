## Question

A laptop has no available Wi-Fi network, but the user's phone has cellular data. What mobile feature can share the phone's Internet connection with the laptop, and what should you check before relying on it?

## Answer

Use **hotspot/tethering**, and check whether the phone and cellular plan allow it, including possible data limits or charges.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Hotspot and tethering features let the phone extend its cellular data connection to other devices.

### Real Help Desk

If the feature is missing or blocked, the issue may be the carrier plan or provider policy rather than the laptop.
