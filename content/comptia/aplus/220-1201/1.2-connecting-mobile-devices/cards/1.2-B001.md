## Question

A user connects a phone to a computer with a cable and expects more than one task to happen. Name two common purposes that mobile-device connections can support.

## Answer

Common purposes include **charging**, **data synchronization**, **file transfer**, **backup**, and **device identification**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Mobile-device connections are not limited to one function. A single connection may provide power and data movement at the same time.

### Exam Trap

Do not treat “connecting a mobile device” as only a charging topic; the objective also includes data and identification use cases.
