## Question

A user keeps accidentally moving the pointer while typing on a laptop. Which built-in input device setting should you consider disabling or adjusting?

## Answer

The **trackpad**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Trackpads can replace a mouse, but accidental taps or pointer movement can interfere with typing.

### Real Help Desk

This is often a usability issue, not a hardware failure. Check the trackpad settings before replacing parts.
