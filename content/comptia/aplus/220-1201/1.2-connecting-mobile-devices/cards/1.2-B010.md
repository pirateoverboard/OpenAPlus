## Question

A user wants a desktop-style setup with external keyboard, mouse, and additional interfaces. Which accessory is the better fit: a docking station or a port replicator?

## Answer

A **docking station** is the better fit when the user needs a broader desktop-style expansion setup.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Docking stations extend existing interfaces and can add functionality. Port replicators are similar but are usually simpler and commonly connect over USB.

### Exam Trap

If the question emphasizes expansion-card capability, the approved source points away from a basic port replicator.
