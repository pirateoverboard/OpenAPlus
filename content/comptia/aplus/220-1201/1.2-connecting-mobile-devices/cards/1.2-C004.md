NFC stands for {{c1::Near Field Communication}} and is used for very short-range wireless exchange.

## Extra

Typical Objective 1.2 examples include payment, transportation, in-person information exchange, and access-token workflows.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

NFC is about close proximity. It is not the best answer for a headset, speaker, or smartwatch connection.
