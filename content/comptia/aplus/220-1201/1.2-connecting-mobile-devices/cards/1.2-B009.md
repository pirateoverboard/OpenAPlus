## Question

A user needs hands-free audio with both earphones and a microphone. Name two supported headset connection options from Objective 1.2.

## Answer

Examples include **Bluetooth**, **USB**, a **3.5 mm TRRS analog connector**, or a compatible device-specific connector.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Headsets combine listening and microphone input. The connection choice depends on the device ports and whether the user needs wired or wireless operation.

### Exam Trap

Standard stereo headphones and a headset are not always the same thing; the microphone path matters.
