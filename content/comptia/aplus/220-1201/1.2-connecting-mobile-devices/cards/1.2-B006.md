## Question

A user wants a phone to connect wirelessly to headphones and a smartwatch over short distances. Which technology should they use?

## Answer

**Bluetooth**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Bluetooth is used for short-range personal-area connections between mobile devices and nearby peripherals.
