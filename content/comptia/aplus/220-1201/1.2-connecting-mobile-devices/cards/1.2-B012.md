## Question

A desktop user needs real-time video for meetings. Which accessory category from Objective 1.2 commonly provides this capability?

## Answer

A **camera/webcam**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Cameras and webcams are used by video conferencing, photo, and multimedia applications. Desktops commonly use external webcams, while many mobile devices include internal cameras.

### Source Note

This card stays at the accessory-role level and does not make claims about specific camera models or resolutions.
