## Question

A technician finds an older mobile device with a connector that is larger than Micro-USB but still smaller than full-size USB. Which older connector type is most likely?

## Answer

**Mini-USB**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Mini-USB is an older mobile-device connector and is larger than Micro-USB. Micro-USB became more common on many mobile devices before USB-C adoption.

### Tech Wisdom

Connector age and size are not the same as data capability. Identify the physical connector first, then verify what the device supports.
