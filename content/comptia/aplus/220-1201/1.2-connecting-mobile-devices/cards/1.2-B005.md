## Question

A company wants users to tap a phone or badge near a workstation reader for quick authentication. Which mobile-device connection method best fits this requirement?

## Answer

**NFC**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

NFC is designed for very short-range wireless exchange, which makes it useful for tap-style payment, identity, and access-token workflows.

### Exam Trap

NFC is not the best answer for an ongoing headset or speaker connection. Use Bluetooth for that type of peripheral.
