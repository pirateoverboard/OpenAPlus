## Question

A tablet has a USB-C port, and the user wants to connect it to an external display through a USB-C adapter. What should you verify before assuming the adapter will work?

## Answer

Verify that the device and adapter support the needed **display signal**, not just the USB-C connector shape.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

USB-C describes the reversible physical connector. The same connector can carry different supported signals depending on the device and adapter.

### Exam Trap

Do not assume every USB-C port supports DisplayPort, HDMI, Thunderbolt, or every USB data mode.
