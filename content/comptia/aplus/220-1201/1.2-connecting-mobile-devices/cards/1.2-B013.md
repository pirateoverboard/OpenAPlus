## Question

A designer needs precise pen input on a workstation, but the display is not a touchscreen. Which mobile-device accessory best fits this requirement?

## Answer

A **drawing pad** with an **active stylus** and external digitizer.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A drawing pad provides a separate digitizer surface for precise stylus input. That makes it different from tapping directly on a touchscreen or using a trackpad for pointer movement.

### Exam Trap

Do not answer with “stylus” by itself when the scenario requires an external input surface. The drawing pad is the accessory; the stylus is the tool used with it.
