## Text

USB-C is a {{c1::reversible}} connector that can be inserted either way.

## Extra

USB-C is a connector shape. Supported signals and features still depend on the device and adapter.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Reversible connector shape is not the same as universal support for every USB-C feature.
