## Question

A mobile device has high CPU use, overheating, and excessive battery drain with
no obvious user activity. What threat category should you consider?

## Hint

The symptoms are not limited to battery hardware.

## Answer

**Possible mobile malware.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Malware can show up as resource, heat, data, or battery symptoms. The answer is
a theory to test, not a final diagnosis.
