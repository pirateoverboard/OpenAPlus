## Question

Before escalating a suspected damaged mobile charging port, what tested-path
details should you document?

## Hint

Preserve which parts of the charging path were already ruled out.

## Answer

**Document the visible port condition and the results of known-good cable and
power-adapter tests.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Escalation is more useful when it includes both physical evidence and isolation
results. The next technician should not need to repeat basic charging-path
tests.
