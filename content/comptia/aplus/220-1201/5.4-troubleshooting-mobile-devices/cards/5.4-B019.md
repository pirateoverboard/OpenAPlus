## Question

A user's phone shows unexpected pop-ups and unfamiliar apps. What should you
run to test the malware theory?

## Hint

Use the suspicious symptoms to choose a way to test the theory.

## Answer

**Run an approved mobile security app or scanner.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Unusual apps and pop-ups are malware clues. The card tests the evidence-based
next step without naming a vendor-specific tool.
