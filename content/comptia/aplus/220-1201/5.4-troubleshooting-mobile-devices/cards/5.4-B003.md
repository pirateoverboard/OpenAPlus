## Question

A user's phone started losing battery rapidly after a new app was installed.
What evidence source should you check first?

## Hint

Measure which software is using the battery before blaming the battery itself.

## Answer

**Check the phone's app battery-usage information.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Sudden drain after an app change should be tested with usage evidence. This is
more targeted than replacing the battery or charger first.
