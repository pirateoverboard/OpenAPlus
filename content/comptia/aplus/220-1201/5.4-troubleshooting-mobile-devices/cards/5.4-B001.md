## Question

A phone shows poor battery health after years of normal use. What repair
direction is most appropriate?

## Answer

**Plan for battery replacement rather than treating it as an app or charging
cable problem.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Poor health after many charge cycles points to battery aging. App usage and
signal checks matter for sudden drain, but the stated evidence is long-term
battery wear.
