## Question

A tablet has slow Wi-Fi only in one corner of the office. What network condition
should you consider before blaming the tablet hardware?

## Hint

Think about what changes as the user moves around.

## Answer

**Limited Wi-Fi range or local interference.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The one-location pattern points to the wireless environment. Tablet hardware is
less likely when the symptom follows a physical area.
