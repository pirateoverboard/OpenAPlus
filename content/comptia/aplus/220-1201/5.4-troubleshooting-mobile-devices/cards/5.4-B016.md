## Question

A phone overheats while sitting on a dashboard in direct sunlight. What should
you do before hardware troubleshooting?

## Hint

Remove the obvious heat source first.

## Answer

**Move the device out of direct sunlight and let it cool.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Environmental heat can trigger shutdowns or overheating symptoms. Correct the
environment before treating the phone as failed.
