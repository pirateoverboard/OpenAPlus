## Question

A user's phone has poor cellular service only inside one concrete building.
What should you test before replacing the phone?

## Hint

Use location as a troubleshooting variable.

## Answer

**Test cellular connectivity in another location, such as outdoors or away from
the building.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Mobile signal is location-dependent. A location-only symptom should be tested
as coverage or signal strength before it becomes a device-failure conclusion.
