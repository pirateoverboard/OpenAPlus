## Question

A user spilled water on a phone that is still powered on. What should you do
first?

## Hint

Prevent additional electrical damage before testing.

## Answer

**Power down the phone and do not charge it.**

## Instructor Notes

### Real Help Desk

Testing a wet device by charging or powering it can make the damage worse. Stop
normal troubleshooting until the device is safe to handle.
