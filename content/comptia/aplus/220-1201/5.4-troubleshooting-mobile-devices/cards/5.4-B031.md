## Question

A tablet slows down after many apps have been left open in the background. What
should you try before replacing the tablet?

## Hint

Reduce active resource pressure first.

## Answer

**Close unnecessary background apps.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Background apps can consume memory and processing resources. Closing them is a
low-risk test before hardware escalation.
