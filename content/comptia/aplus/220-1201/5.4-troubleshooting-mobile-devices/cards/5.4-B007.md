## Question

A phone will not charge after being carried in a pocket all day. What should you
inspect first?

## Hint

Start where the cable physically meets the device.

## Answer

**Inspect the charging port for debris, obstruction, or visible damage.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The charging path starts at the device interface. A simple obstruction can look
like a failed battery or charger.
