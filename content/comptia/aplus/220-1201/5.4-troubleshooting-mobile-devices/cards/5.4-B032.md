## Question

An older phone becomes slow only when running a newly released app. What should
you consider before diagnosing a device fault?

## Hint

Compare the app's expectations with the device's age.

## Answer

**The app may not support the older hardware well.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Newer apps may assume newer hardware capabilities. That is different from a
random hardware failure and should be documented as a compatibility concern.
