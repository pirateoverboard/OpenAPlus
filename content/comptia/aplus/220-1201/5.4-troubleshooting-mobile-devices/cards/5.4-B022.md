## Question

A phone cannot install a new app and reports there is not enough space. What
should you check first?

## Answer

**Check available storage and remove unnecessary data if needed.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The error already points to storage. Do not troubleshoot the app store or
network first when the device says space is the blocker.
