## Question

A phone case is bulging and the battery appears swollen. What is the safest
first action?

## Hint

Treat physical battery deformation as a safety issue.

## Answer

**Stop using the device immediately and do not open or puncture the battery.**

## Instructor Notes

### Real Help Desk

A swollen battery can be a fire risk. Normal troubleshooting stops until the
device is handled through the proper replacement and disposal process.
