## Question

A stylus is charged, but the tablet no longer sees it as connected. What should
you check next?

## Hint

Confirm the wireless relationship between the accessory and tablet.

## Answer

**Check the Bluetooth connection and try re-pairing the stylus.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Once power is ruled out, pairing is the next likely path for a wireless stylus.
The card tests the accessory connection decision.
