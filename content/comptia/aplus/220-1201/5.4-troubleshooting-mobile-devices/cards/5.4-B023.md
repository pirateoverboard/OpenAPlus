## Question

A mobile app download never starts, but the device has plenty of free storage.
What should you verify next?

## Hint

The app still has to come from somewhere.

## Answer

**Verify network connectivity.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

App installation requires access to an external service. After storage is ruled
out, connectivity becomes a focused next check.
