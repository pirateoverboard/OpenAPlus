## Question

A required app says it is not compatible with the current mobile OS version.
What should you check?

## Hint

Compare the app requirement with the device software level.

## Answer

**Check for an OS update or an updated app version that supports the device.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Compatibility is a different problem from storage or network access. The next
step is to verify whether supported software versions exist.
