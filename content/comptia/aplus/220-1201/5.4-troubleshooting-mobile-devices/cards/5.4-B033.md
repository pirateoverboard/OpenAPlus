## Question

You must escalate a liquid-damaged phone. What liquid-exposure evidence should
you include in the ticket?

## Hint

Capture evidence the next technician cannot infer later.

## Answer

**Document the liquid exposure details and any Liquid Contact Indicator status.**

## Instructor Notes

### Real Help Desk

Good handoff records what happened, what was powered or charged, and whether an
indicator showed exposure. This helps the next technician avoid unsafe retests.
