## Question

A powered tablet stylus suddenly stops responding. What should you check first?

## Hint

Start with the simplest accessory condition.

## Answer

**Check the stylus battery or charge level.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Powered accessories fail when they are discharged. This comes before re-pairing
or replacing the stylus tip.
