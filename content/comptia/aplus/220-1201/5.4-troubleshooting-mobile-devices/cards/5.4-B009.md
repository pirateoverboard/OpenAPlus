## Question

A phone still does not charge after you inspect the port and try a known-good
cable. What part of the charging path should you verify next?

## Hint

Follow the power path away from the phone.

## Answer

**Verify the power adapter or charging power source.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The adapter is a separate part of the charging chain. Checking it keeps the
diagnosis evidence-based before concluding the device has failed.
