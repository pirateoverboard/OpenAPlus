## Question

A phone overheats whenever one navigation app is running. What should you check
before assuming the battery is bad?

## Hint

Look for the workload that appears with the heat.

## Answer

**Check whether the app is using excessive CPU or battery.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Overheating can be caused by workload. App usage evidence helps separate a
software/resource issue from a battery or hardware failure.
