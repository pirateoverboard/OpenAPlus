## Question

A phone charging port is visibly bent and known-good charging parts do not
help. What should you do next?

## Hint

Visible physical damage changes the support path.

## Answer

**Escalate for hardware repair or replacement instead of continuing cable
swaps.**

## Instructor Notes

### Real Help Desk

Mobile ports are often not simple modular field repairs. Document the visible
damage and test results before handoff.
