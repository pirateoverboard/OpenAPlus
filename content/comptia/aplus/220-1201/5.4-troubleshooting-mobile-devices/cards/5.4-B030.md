## Question

A phone is slow and frequently warns that storage is almost full. What should
you address first?

## Answer

**Free up storage by removing unnecessary data or apps.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Low storage can contribute to slowdowns and crashes. The symptom gives a
specific target before broader performance troubleshooting.
