## Question

A user wants to keep using a phone with cracked glass until a replacement part
arrives. What safety concern should you address?

## Answer

**The broken glass can be sharp, so protect the surface temporarily and arrange
repair.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

This card is not testing cosmetic damage. It tests the safety angle that a help
desk technician should communicate when a screen is physically broken.
