## Question

A phone is slow and has not been updated or restarted in months. What
least-invasive action should you try first?

## Hint

Start with reversible software maintenance.

## Answer

**Update the OS and apps as appropriate, then restart the device.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Outdated software and long uptime can degrade behavior. Updating and restarting
are safer first steps than replacing hardware.
