## Text

The mobile touchscreen input layer is commonly called the {{c1::digitizer}}.

## Extra

Digitizer symptoms are input symptoms, not automatically display-output
symptoms.

## Instructor Notes

### Memory Trick

The digitizer turns physical touch into digital input. Keep that separate from
display brightness or image-quality problems.
