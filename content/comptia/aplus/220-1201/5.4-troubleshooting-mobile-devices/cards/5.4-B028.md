## Question

A charged and paired stylus skips or scratches on the screen. What physical
part should you inspect?

## Answer

**Inspect the stylus tip for wear or damage.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

If power and pairing are fine, the stylus itself may have a worn contact point.
This is a targeted accessory check, not a full tablet replacement decision.
