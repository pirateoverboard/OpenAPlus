## Question

A mobile device screen is on, but touch input is not responding. What simple
first isolation step should you try?

## Hint

Start with a reversible step that resets the device state.

## Answer

**Restart the mobile device.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A restart can clear a frozen mobile interface before repair or replacement is
considered. Exact key combinations vary by device, so the card tests the support
decision rather than model-specific buttons.
