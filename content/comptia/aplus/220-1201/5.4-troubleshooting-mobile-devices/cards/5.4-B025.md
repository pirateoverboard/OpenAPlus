## Question

A mobile app store keeps failing after storage and connectivity check out. What
account or store-state check should you try?

## Hint

Look at the service state rather than the app being installed.

## Answer

**Check store cache where applicable, or account sign-in state, then sign out
and back in if appropriate.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Store cache on supported platforms or credentials can block installation even
when storage and network are fine. This card keeps the target to one
account/store-state decision.
