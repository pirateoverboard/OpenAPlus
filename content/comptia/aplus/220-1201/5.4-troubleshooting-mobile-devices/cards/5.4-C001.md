## Text

In mobile liquid-damage triage, LCI stands for {{c1::Liquid Contact Indicator}}.

## Extra

An LCI can support the damage history, but it does not make the device safe to
power on.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

This compact acronym recall supports escalation notes and liquid-damage
inspection without turning the card into a broad procedure.
