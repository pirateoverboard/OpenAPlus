## Question

A wet phone appears dry on the outside after a few hours. What should you avoid
doing too soon?

## Hint

Outside dryness does not prove the inside is safe.

## Answer

**Avoid powering it on or connecting it to power too soon.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

The safe decision is patience, not a quick power-on test. Moisture inside the
device can still cause damage after the exterior looks dry.
