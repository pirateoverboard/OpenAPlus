## Question

A phone will not charge, and the user's cable is frayed near the connector. What
is the best next test?

## Hint

Use a safe replacement for the suspect part of the charging path.

## Answer

**Try a known-good charging cable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A damaged cable should not be trusted or reused. A known-good cable isolates the
cable before replacing the phone.
