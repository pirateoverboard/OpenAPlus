## Text

Random mobile cursor or touch movement without user input is called
{{c1::cursor drift}}.

## Extra

If the device supports it, touch calibration can be a targeted troubleshooting
step.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The term helps connect the symptom to the calibration decision card without
requiring a broad repair answer.
