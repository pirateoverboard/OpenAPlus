## Question

After a liquid spill, what removable items should you take off only if the phone
design allows it?

## Hint

Remove external pieces without forcing the device open.

## Answer

**Remove the case, cards, back cover, or battery if they are designed to be
removed.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The goal is to reduce trapped liquid and power risk without creating more
damage. Do not pry apart a sealed device as part of entry-level support.
