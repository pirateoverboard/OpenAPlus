## Question

A phone battery drains quickly only when the user works in a basement with weak
cellular reception. What likely cause should you investigate before replacing
the battery?

## Hint

Look for a condition that makes the device keep searching.

## Answer

**Poor reception causing the phone to spend extra power searching for signal.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

The location pattern matters. A weak signal area can create battery drain even
when the battery itself is not the root problem.
