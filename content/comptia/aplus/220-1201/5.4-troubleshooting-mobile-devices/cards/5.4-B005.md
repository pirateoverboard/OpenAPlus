## Question

A mobile phone has a broken screen but still responds to touch. What should you
do before sending it for screen replacement?

## Hint

Protect what the user cannot easily replace.

## Answer

**Back up the user's data first if the device is still usable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Screen repair can fail or require device replacement. A backup reduces the risk
that a repair task becomes a data-loss incident.
