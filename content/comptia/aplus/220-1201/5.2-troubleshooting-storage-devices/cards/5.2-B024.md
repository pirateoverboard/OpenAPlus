## Question

An external drive has power and a known-good cable, but it is not detected on
one laptop port. What should you test next?

## Answer

**Try another compatible port on the computer.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Testing another port helps separate a port problem from a drive problem. It is
still a low-risk check before escalating to storage failure.
