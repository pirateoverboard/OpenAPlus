## Question

An SSD can still read some files but fails whenever users save changes. What
should the technician do before trying aggressive repairs?

## Hint

Do not rely on the absence of mechanical noise when judging SSD risk.

## Answer

**Back up readable data and treat the drive as potentially failing.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

SSD failure may not include noise. Read-only behavior or sudden write failure is
enough reason to protect data and plan replacement or escalation before trying
steps that could reduce access further.
