## Question

After a desktop was moved to a new desk, it no longer detects its internal SATA
drive. What is the best first physical check?

## Hint

Recent movement makes a simple physical cause more likely.

## Answer

**Check the drive data and power cable connections for looseness or damage.**

## Instructor Notes

### Help Desk Logic

The timing points to a physical connection problem. Cable checks are fast,
reversible, and less disruptive than reinstalling the OS or replacing the drive.
