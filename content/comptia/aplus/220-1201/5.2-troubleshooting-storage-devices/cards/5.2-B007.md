## Question

A PC keeps trying to boot from a USB recovery drive instead of the internal
disk. What is the best first fix?

## Hint

Rule out the simplest boot-source mistake before changing hardware.

## Answer

**Remove the unintended bootable USB media and confirm the BIOS or UEFI boot
sequence if needed.**

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Wrong boot media can look like a storage failure. Start with the simple cause,
then verify boot order if the symptom repeats.
