## Question

A newly installed internal drive does not appear during OS setup. Cables are
seated. What should you check next?

## Hint

For a new install, verify configuration and detection before declaring the drive bad.

## Answer

**Check the hardware configuration, including whether the storage interface is
enabled and whether another compatible interface or port detects the drive.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

New-installation failures can come from configuration, interface, or port
problems. This tests the detection path before deciding the drive itself is bad.
