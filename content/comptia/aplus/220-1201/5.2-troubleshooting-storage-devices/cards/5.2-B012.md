## Question

You must escalate a RAID failure to the infrastructure team. What RAID-specific
status should your ticket include?

## Hint

Capture the array evidence before physical action.

## Answer

**Include the RAID console status for the affected array or volume.**

## Instructor Notes

### Professional Practice

RAID console status helps the next technician understand whether the array is
healthy, degraded, rebuilding, or failed.
