## Question

You must escalate a RAID failure to the infrastructure team. What information
should your ticket include?

## Hint

Escalation should capture the array state and data-risk context.

## Answer

**Include the exact alert, array or volume affected, console status, failed disk
identifier if known, recent power or communication issues, backup status, and
steps already taken.**

## Instructor Notes

### Professional Practice

Escalation should let the next technician continue without repeating discovery.
For RAID, the console details and backup status are especially important because
the wrong recovery action can affect multiple drives or volumes.
