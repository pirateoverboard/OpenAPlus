## Text

Clicking, grinding, or scraping noises are warning signs associated with
{{c1::mechanical hard drive failure}}.

## Extra

Noise is a clue, not a complete diagnosis. Confirm with data protection,
inspection, and diagnostics when appropriate.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Mechanical drives contain moving parts. This compact recall target supports the
scenario cards about recognizing serious drive symptoms.
