## Question

A user's external drive disappeared from the OS after they moved their laptop.
What should you check before assuming the drive failed?

## Hint

Rule out simple external power and connection issues first.

## Answer

**Check external drive power, the cable connection, and the port or adapter path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

External storage depends on power and a removable connection. Movement makes a
loose cable or missing power path more likely than sudden media failure.
