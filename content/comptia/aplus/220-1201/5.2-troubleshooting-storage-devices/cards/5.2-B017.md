## Question

A user's external drive disappeared from the OS after they moved their laptop.
What should you check before assuming the drive failed?

## Hint

Rule out simple external power and connection issues first.

## Answer

**Check whether the external drive is receiving power.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Some external drives require their own power source or enough bus power from
the host. A drive without power can disappear from the OS even when the storage
media itself has not failed.
