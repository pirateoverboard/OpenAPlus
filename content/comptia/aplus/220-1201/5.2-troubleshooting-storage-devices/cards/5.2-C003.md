## Text

During a RAID failure, check the {{c1::RAID management console}} before pulling
drives.

## Extra

The console helps identify the failed disk or volume and reduces the chance of
removing the wrong drive.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

This is compact recall for a safety-critical order of operations. It supports
the RAID scenario card without duplicating the full escalation decision.
