## Question

A user says a database app is slow, and you suspect storage latency. What kind
of evidence helps test the storage-performance theory?

## Hint

Measure the suspected bottleneck before trusting a vague performance complaint.

## Answer

**Measure storage access performance, such as I/O operations per second, instead
of relying only on the user's description.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Read/write delays can occur in many parts of the path. A storage metric helps
separate a drive or storage bottleneck from an application, CPU, or memory
theory.
