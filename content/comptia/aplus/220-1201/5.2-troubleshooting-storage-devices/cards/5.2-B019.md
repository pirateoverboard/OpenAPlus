## Question

You cannot resolve a storage issue and must escalate it. What storage-specific
details should you document?

## Hint

Include what proves the issue is storage-related and how much data is at risk.

## Answer

**Document the exact error, drive detection status, noises or lights, cable and
power checks, S.M.A.R.T. or diagnostic results, backup status, and data-risk
concerns.**

## Instructor Notes

### Interview Angle

This is the difference between "the drive is broken" and a useful escalation.
Storage tickets should make the risk to data clear and show what evidence was
already gathered.
