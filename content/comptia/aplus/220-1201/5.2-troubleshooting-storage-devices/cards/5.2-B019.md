## Question

You cannot resolve a storage issue and must escalate it. What data-risk detail
should you document?

## Hint

Escalation should make the risk to user data clear.

## Answer

**Document backup status and any data-risk concerns.**

## Instructor Notes

### Support Logic

Storage escalation is different from a generic hardware ticket because continued
testing can affect recoverable data. Backup status and data risk are concrete
handoff details.
