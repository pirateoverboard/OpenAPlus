## Question

An internal drive is not detected after cable and firmware checks. What known-good
test can help separate a bad drive from a bad system connection?

## Hint

A known-good path helps show whether the problem follows the drive.

## Answer

**Try the drive in another compatible computer or known-good storage connection.**

## Instructor Notes

### What It Rules Out

Testing through a different path helps isolate whether the failure follows the
drive or stays with the original computer, cable, port, or power path.
