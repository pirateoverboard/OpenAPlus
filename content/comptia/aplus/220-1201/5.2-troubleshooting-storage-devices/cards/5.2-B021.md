## Question

A RAID console identifies a specific failed drive. What identifier should you
document before escalation or replacement?

## Hint

The next technician needs to avoid acting on the wrong physical drive.

## Answer

**Document the failed disk identifier shown by the RAID console.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

RAID actions can affect multiple drives. Recording the console's failed-disk
identifier reduces the chance of pulling or replacing the wrong device.
