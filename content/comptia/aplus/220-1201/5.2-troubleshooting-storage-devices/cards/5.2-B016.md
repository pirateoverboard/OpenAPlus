## Question

Windows boots normally, but a secondary internal drive no longer appears in the
OS. What should you check first?

## Hint

Because the OS still boots, focus on whether the missing drive is detected.

## Answer

**Check whether firmware detects the drive, then inspect the drive's data and
power connections.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Because the OS boots, the missing item is the secondary storage device. Firmware
detection and internal cabling narrow the issue before file-system or OS repair
is attempted.
