## Question

An external drive disappeared after a laptop was moved. The drive has power, but
the OS still does not detect it. What cable test should you try?

## Hint

Use a simple swap to test the removable connection.

## Answer

**Try a known-good data cable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

External storage depends on a removable data connection. A known-good cable
tests the cable theory without assuming the drive has failed.
