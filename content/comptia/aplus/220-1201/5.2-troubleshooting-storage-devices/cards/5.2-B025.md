## Question

An external drive appears when connected directly to the laptop but disappears
when connected through a dock. What does that result point toward?

## Hint

Pay attention to where the symptom appears in the connection path.

## Answer

**The dock or adapter path is the likely problem area.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

When the drive works directly, the test reduces suspicion on the drive itself.
The failure follows the intermediate connection path, so escalation notes should
include the dock or adapter tested.
