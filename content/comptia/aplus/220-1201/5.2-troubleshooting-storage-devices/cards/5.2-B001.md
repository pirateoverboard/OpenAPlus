## Question

A user reports "Cannot read from the source disk" while copying critical files
from a laptop drive. What should you prioritize before attempting repair steps?

## Hint

Protect the user's data before attempting risky repair.

## Answer

**Preserve the data by getting a backup or verified copy if the drive is still
readable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Read/write failures may get worse during troubleshooting. A repair command or
heavy diagnostic can stress a failing drive, so the support decision is to
protect recoverable data before taking riskier action.
