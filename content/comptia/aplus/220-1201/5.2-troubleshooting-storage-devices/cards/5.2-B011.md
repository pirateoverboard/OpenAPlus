## Question

A server storage array is beeping and reports a RAID warning. What should you do
before pulling any drives?

## Hint

Use array evidence before taking physical action.

## Answer

**Check the RAID management console to identify the affected array, volume, and
drive status.**

## Instructor Notes

### Safety

RAID arrays vary. Pulling the wrong drive can turn a degraded array into an
outage or data-loss event. Console evidence comes before physical action.
