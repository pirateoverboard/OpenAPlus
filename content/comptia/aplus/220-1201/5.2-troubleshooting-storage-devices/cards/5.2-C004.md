## Text

S.M.A.R.T. stands for {{c1::Self-Monitoring, Analysis, and Reporting Technology}}.

## Extra

Use S.M.A.R.T. as a warning system for drive health trends, not as a guarantee
that a drive will or will not fail.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The acronym is a compact recall fact, but the troubleshooting value is acting on
warning trends before users lose access to data.
