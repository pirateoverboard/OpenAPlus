## Question

A workstation displays a "Boot Device Not Found" style message. What first
distinction should a technician verify before assuming Windows is corrupted?

## Hint

First decide whether this is a detection problem or a bootability problem.

## Answer

**Verify whether the firmware detects the drive.**

## Instructor Notes

### Narrowing

This symptom can happen before the operating system is involved. If firmware
cannot see the drive, Windows repair is premature.
