## Question

A user logs in and their mapped network drive is missing, but local disks are
present. What should you verify first?

## Hint

Decide whether this is local storage or a mapped network resource.

## Answer

**Verify network availability and whether the share is set to reconnect at
sign-in or through a login script.**

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

A missing network share is not the same as a failed local disk. The correct
starting point is the mapping and sign-in behavior, not opening the computer to
replace storage.
