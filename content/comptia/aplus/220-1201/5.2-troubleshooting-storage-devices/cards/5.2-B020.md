## Question

A boot failure must be escalated. What exact startup evidence should you record
before handing off the ticket?

## Hint

Preserve the symptom text before it changes or disappears.

## Answer

**Record the exact boot error message.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

"Boot failed" is too vague. Exact wording helps separate missing device,
missing operating system, boot order, or other storage-related theories.
