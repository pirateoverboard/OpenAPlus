## Text

Before risky repair work on a suspected failing drive, the priority is
{{c1::getting a current backup if possible}}.

## Extra

Backups should be verified before actions that may write heavily to unstable
storage.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

This is one of the highest-value storage troubleshooting decisions. The goal is
not to "fix first"; the goal is to avoid turning a storage symptom into avoidable
data loss.
