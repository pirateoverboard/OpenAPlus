## Question

Monitoring software shows worsening S.M.A.R.T. warning values for a storage
drive. What is the best next step?

## Hint

Treat health warnings as a chance to act before users lose access.

## Answer

**Verify a good backup and plan to replace the drive before it fails completely.**

## Instructor Notes

### Reasoning

S.M.A.R.T. is useful because it can show warning signs before total failure.
Treat an increasing warning trend as evidence to act early, not as trivia to
memorize by attribute number.
