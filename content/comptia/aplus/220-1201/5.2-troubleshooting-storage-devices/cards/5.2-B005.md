## Question

Firmware detects the internal drive, but startup stops with an "Operating system
not found" style message. What does that evidence suggest?

## Answer

**The storage device may be present, but the operating system or boot data is
not usable.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Drive detection changes the theory. A missing OS message is not the same as a
missing drive, so the next work should focus on boot configuration, boot files,
or OS state after data risk is considered.
