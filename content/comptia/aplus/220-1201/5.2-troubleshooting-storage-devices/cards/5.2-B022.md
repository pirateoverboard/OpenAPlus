## Question

When escalating a suspected drive failure, what detection detail helps the next
technician choose the correct troubleshooting path?

## Answer

**Document whether the drive is detected by firmware or the operating system.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Detection status separates a missing-device path from an unbootable or
unmountable-data path. That distinction is more useful than simply writing
"drive bad."
