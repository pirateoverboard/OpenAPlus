## Question

A desktop is extremely slow and its storage activity light stays on almost
constantly. What theory should you test before replacing memory or the CPU?

## Hint

Look for evidence that the storage device is struggling before blaming other components.

## Answer

**Test whether the storage device is retrying reads/writes or has unusually slow
disk I/O.**

## Instructor Notes

### Troubleshooting Logic

Constant storage activity can point to repeated access attempts or a storage
bottleneck. Measure the storage path first so the repair plan is based on
evidence instead of guessing at unrelated components.
