## Question

A NAS sends repeated storage health notifications over email. Why should those
alerts be handled before users lose access to files?

## Hint

Repeated warnings are an opportunity for planned action instead of emergency recovery.

## Answer

**They may indicate a drive problem that can be backed up and replaced before it
becomes a data-loss outage.**

## Instructor Notes

### Interview Angle

Good support responds to warning signs while there is still time to protect
data. Waiting until the share is unavailable turns planned maintenance into an
incident.
