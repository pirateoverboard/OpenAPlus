## Text

Screen resolution describes the number of pixels {{c1::across and down}}, commonly written as width x height.

## Extra

Resolution is the pixel count; pixel density relates that count to the display's physical size.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Do not replace the core resolution concept with memorized resolution-name tables.
