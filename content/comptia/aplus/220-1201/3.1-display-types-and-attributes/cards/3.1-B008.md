## Question

A photo editor needs a display that can represent a wider range of colors for the target workflow. Which display attribute should be compared?

## Answer

**Color gamut or color-gamut coverage.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Color gamut describes the range of colors a display can represent. For color-sensitive work, gamut support is more relevant than choosing by screen size alone.
