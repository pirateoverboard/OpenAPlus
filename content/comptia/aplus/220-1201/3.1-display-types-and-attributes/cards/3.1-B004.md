## Question

A hybrid laptop must support precise pen-style sketching and annotation. Which display input component matters most for that requirement?

## Answer

**A digitizer.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A touchscreen accepts direct touch input, while a digitizer supports stylus or pen-like input for more precise work.

### Exam Trap

Do not treat every touch-capable display as equivalent when the requirement specifically calls for pen precision.
