## Question

Two displays use the same resolution, but one is physically smaller. Which attribute explains why the smaller display can look sharper at the same viewing distance?

## Answer

**Higher pixel density.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Pixel density relates the pixel count to physical size. The same number of pixels packed into less space produces more pixels per inch.
