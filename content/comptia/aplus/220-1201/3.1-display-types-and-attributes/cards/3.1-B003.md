## Question

A low-cost gaming display prioritizes fast pixel response, but users notice color shifts when viewing from the side. Which LCD technology matches that tradeoff?

## Answer

**TN.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

TN panels are associated with fast response times, while weaker viewing angles and color shift are the expected tradeoffs.
