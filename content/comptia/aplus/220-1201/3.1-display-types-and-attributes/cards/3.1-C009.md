## Text

Color gamut describes the {{c1::range of colors}} a display can represent.

## Extra

Coverage of a particular gamut standard can matter for color-sensitive work.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

A larger screen or higher resolution does not automatically mean better color coverage.
