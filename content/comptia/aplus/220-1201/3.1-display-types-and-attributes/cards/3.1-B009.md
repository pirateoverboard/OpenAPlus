## Question

A mobile device display is thin and does not need a separate backlight because its pixels emit light directly. Which display type is this?

## Answer

**OLED.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

OLED uses organic light-emitting material at the pixel level, so the panel does not depend on a separate LCD backlight.
