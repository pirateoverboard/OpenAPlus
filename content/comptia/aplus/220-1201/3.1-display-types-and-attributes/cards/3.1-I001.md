## Prompt

In the simplified diagram, which panel design does not require a separate backlight?

## Answer

**Panel B, the OLED-style self-emissive panel.**

## Instructor Notes

### Visual Cue

Panel A shows a separate light layer behind the display stack. Panel B shows each picture element producing its own light, which is the key OLED distinction.
