## Question

A user wants an LCD display with better control of dark areas than a conventional LED-backlit LCD. Which display technology should the technician consider?

## Answer

**Mini LED.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Mini LED is still an LCD backlight design, but it uses many smaller LEDs so lighting can be controlled in smaller zones.

### Exam Trap

Mini LED improves backlight control; it is not the same as OLED, where the pixels emit their own light.
