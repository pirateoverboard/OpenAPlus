## Question

A user wants smoother motion for fast games and sports video. Which display attribute should the technician prioritize, assuming the graphics hardware and cable also support it?

## Answer

**A higher refresh rate.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Refresh rate describes how often the image can update. Fast-moving content benefits when the display, graphics adapter, and connection all support the higher mode.
