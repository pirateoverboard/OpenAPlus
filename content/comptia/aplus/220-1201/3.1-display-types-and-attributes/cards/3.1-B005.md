## Question

A laptop LCD appears black, but a faint desktop image is visible when a flashlight is held close to the screen. Which display subsystem should be checked first?

## Answer

**The backlight path, including the inverter if that model uses one.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A faint image means the panel may still be producing the picture, but the lighting behind the LCD is not making it visible.

### Exam Trap

Do not jump directly to replacing the graphics adapter when the image is present but not lit.
