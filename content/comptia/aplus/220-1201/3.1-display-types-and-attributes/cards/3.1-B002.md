## Question

A designer needs an LCD panel type known for strong color representation. Which LCD technology is the best fit?

## Answer

**IPS.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

IPS panels are associated with excellent color representation, which makes them a better fit for color-sensitive work than a choice based only on response time.

### Exam Trap

TN can be attractive for fast response, but the scenario asks for color representation.
