## Text

Mini LED is an LCD backlight approach that uses {{c1::smaller LEDs}} for finer lighting control.

## Extra

It improves backlight control, especially in dark areas, without changing the panel into OLED.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Mini LED describes the backlight system; OLED describes self-emissive pixels.
