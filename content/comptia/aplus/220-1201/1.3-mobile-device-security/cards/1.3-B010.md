## Question

A company buys and controls a phone, but allows the employee to use it personally. Which ownership model best matches this?

## Answer

**COPE**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

COPE means the device is corporate-owned but personally enabled. The organization keeps strong control while permitting personal use.

### Related Cards

BYOD starts with employee ownership. CYOD is similar to COPE but gives the user a choice from approved devices.
