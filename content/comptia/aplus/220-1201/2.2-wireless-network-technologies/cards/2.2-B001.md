## Question

A laptop and access point from different vendors can still connect because both follow the same Wi-Fi standards family. Which IEEE standards family is this?

## Answer

**802.11**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

802.11 is the IEEE wireless networking family used for Wi-Fi. Standards let different vendors build compatible wireless devices.

### Exam Trap

Do not confuse the standards family with a single vendor feature or a router model.
