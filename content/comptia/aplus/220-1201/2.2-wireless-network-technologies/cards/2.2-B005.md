## Question

A phone completes a payment by being held very close to a payment terminal. Which short-range wireless technology is being used?

## Answer

**NFC**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

NFC supports short-range two-way communication and is commonly associated with payment systems and access-token style interactions.

### Exam Trap

Do not choose Bluetooth just because the device is wireless. The close tap-style interaction points to NFC.
