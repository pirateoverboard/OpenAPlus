## Text

802.11ax is also known as Wi-Fi {{c1::6 and 6E}}.

## Extra

The approved source associates 802.11ax with Wi-Fi 6 and Wi-Fi 6E.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Do not treat Wi-Fi 6E as a completely different 802.11 base standard from 802.11ax in this objective.
