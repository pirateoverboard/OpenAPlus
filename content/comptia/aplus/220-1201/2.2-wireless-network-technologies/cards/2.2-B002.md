## Question

Several nearby Wi-Fi networks use overlapping frequency groups. What channel-planning goal should improve coexistence when possible?

## Answer

Use **non-overlapping channels** when possible.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Channels are groups of frequencies. When nearby networks avoid overlap, they are less likely to compete with each other on the same radio space.

### Exam Trap

Do not jump to replacing hardware before checking basic wireless planning choices.
