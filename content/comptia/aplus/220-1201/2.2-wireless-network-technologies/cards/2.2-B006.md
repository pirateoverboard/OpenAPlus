## Question

Which technology is the better match for a short-range two-way tap interaction: RFID-style inventory tracking or NFC?

## Answer

**NFC**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

NFC builds on RFID concepts but is associated with very short-range two-way interactions such as payment and access-token workflows.

### Exam Trap

RFID is often used for identification and tracking; NFC is the stronger match when the scenario emphasizes a close two-way tap interaction.
