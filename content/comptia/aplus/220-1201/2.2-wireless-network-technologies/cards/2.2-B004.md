## Question

A warehouse wants tags that can identify inventory items when scanned by radio. Which wireless technology fits that use case?

## Answer

**RFID**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

RFID is commonly used for identification and tracking, including inventory, access badges, and similar tagged items.

### Source Note

This card tests the use case, not a specific vendor's tag format.
