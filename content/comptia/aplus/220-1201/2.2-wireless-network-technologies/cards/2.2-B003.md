## Question

A user wants a short-range wireless headset without joining the office Wi-Fi network. Which wireless technology is the best match?

## Answer

**Bluetooth**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Bluetooth is commonly used to remove cables from nearby peripherals such as headsets, speakers, keyboards, and mice.

### Exam Trap

Bluetooth personal-area connections are not the same as Wi-Fi infrastructure networking.
