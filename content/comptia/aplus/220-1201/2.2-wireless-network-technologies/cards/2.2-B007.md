## Question

A device uses a brief tap to help start pairing with another wireless technology. Which technology can help bootstrap Bluetooth pairing?

## Answer

**NFC**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The approved source identifies NFC as a possible bootstrap method for another wireless connection such as Bluetooth pairing.

### Exam Trap

The tap does not mean the entire connection stays on NFC. NFC can assist setup for a different wireless link.
