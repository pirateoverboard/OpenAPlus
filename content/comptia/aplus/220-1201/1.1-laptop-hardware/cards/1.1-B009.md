## Question

A workstation should authenticate a user when a supported device is tapped within a few centimeters, without requiring a typed password. Which laptop feature fits this scenario?

## Answer

**Near-field communication (NFC)**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

NFC is designed for very short-range exchanges and can support authentication interactions.

### Exam Trap

Bluetooth also connects nearby devices, but its personal-area range and pairing model do not match this tap-at-the-reader scenario.
