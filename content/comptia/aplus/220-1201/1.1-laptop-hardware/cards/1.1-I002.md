## Prompt

Which labeled path—A, B, or C—shows the common Wi-Fi antenna routing?

## Answer

**Path A**, around the display assembly.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The source places multiple antenna wires around the laptop screen, including main and auxiliary Wi-Fi leads.

### Related Cards

`1.1-B012` tests identifying the routed wires; this card tests their placement visually.
