## Question

A laptop has failed RAM that is soldered to the system board. What hardware must typically be replaced to correct the memory hardware failure?

## Answer

The **system board**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Soldered RAM is not a removable module, so there is no separate SO-DIMM to exchange.

### Real Help Desk

Confirm whether memory is modular or soldered before promising an upgrade or quoting a repair.
