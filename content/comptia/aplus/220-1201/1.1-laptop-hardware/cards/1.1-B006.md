## Question

What should a technician consult before attempting to remove or replace an individual laptop keycap?

## Answer

The **manufacturer's instructions for that keyboard design**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Keycaps and the mechanisms beneath them are delicate, and removal methods differ between designs.

### Exam Trap

Do not treat every laptop keycap as if it uses the same release method.
