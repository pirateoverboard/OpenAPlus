## Question

A laptop owner prioritizes quiet operation and lower storage access latency. Should the replacement use an HDD or an SSD?

## Answer

An **SSD**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

An SSD stores data without spinning platters or other moving parts, supporting quiet operation and faster access.

### Exam Trap

“Better” depends on the stated requirement. This card selects an SSD because latency and noise are explicit priorities.
