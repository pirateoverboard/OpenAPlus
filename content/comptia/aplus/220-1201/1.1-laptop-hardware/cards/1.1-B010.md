## Question

A laptop must support sign-in using “something you are.” Which physical security feature meets that requirement?

## Answer

**Biometrics**, such as a fingerprint reader or face-recognition hardware.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Fingerprint and facial characteristics are biometric factors. The feature also requires appropriate operating-system configuration.

### Exam Trap

Having a camera or sensor does not by itself prove that biometric sign-in is configured.
