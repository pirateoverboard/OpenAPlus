## Question

Why should a technician verify the exact laptop model and service procedure before estimating a battery replacement?

## Answer

Battery access is **model-specific**: it may be modular or may require substantial laptop disassembly.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Battery chemistry does not determine how the battery is mounted or accessed. The physical design controls the repair process.

### Real Help Desk

Avoid turning an illustrative repair time or screw count into a universal procedure.
