## Text

In the supported laptop-storage context, an M.2 drive connects directly to its socket without separate {{c1::data and power cables}}.

## Extra

This describes the physical connection used by the source; it does not assign every M.2 drive to one storage protocol.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

M.2 removes separate drive cables, but the laptop design still determines how much disassembly is required for access.
