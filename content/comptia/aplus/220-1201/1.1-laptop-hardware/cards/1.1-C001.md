## Text

SO-DIMM stands for {{c1::Small Outline Dual In-line Memory Module}}.

## Extra

SO-DIMM is the compact removable memory-module form factor commonly used in laptops.

## Instructor Notes

### Memory Trick

“Small Outline” distinguishes the compact module from the larger desktop DIMM form factor.
