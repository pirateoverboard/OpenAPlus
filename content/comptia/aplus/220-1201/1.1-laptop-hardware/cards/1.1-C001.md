## Text

Modern laptops commonly use {{c1::SO-DIMM}} memory.

## Extra

SO-DIMM stands for Small Outline Dual In-line Memory Module.

## Instructor Notes

Do not confuse SO-DIMM laptop memory with larger desktop DIMMs.
