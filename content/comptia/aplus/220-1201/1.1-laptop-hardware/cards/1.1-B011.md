## Question

A user needs to record audio beyond casual video calls, and the built-in laptop microphone does not meet the requirement. What input option should the technician recommend?

## Answer

Use an **external analog or USB microphone**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The built-in display microphone is convenient for everyday calls. The private source recommends an external microphone for non-casual use.

### Source Note

This card makes no universal audio-quality claim; it follows the source's distinction between casual built-in use and an external option for other recording needs.
