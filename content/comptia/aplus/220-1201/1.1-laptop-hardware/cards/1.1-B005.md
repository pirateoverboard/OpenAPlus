## Question

A technician is preparing to replace a laptop keyboard that uses screws and a ribbon cable. What should determine the correct disassembly and reconnection sequence?

## Answer

The **manufacturer's service documentation for that laptop model**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Keyboard access varies by model. Service documentation identifies the required fasteners, access order, and ribbon-cable handling for the specific design.

### Exam Trap

The presence of screws and a ribbon cable does not make the complete replacement procedure universal across laptops.
