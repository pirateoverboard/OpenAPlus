## Question

A technician opens a laptop display assembly and sees multiple thin wires routed around the screen. Which wireless hardware are these most likely associated with?

## Answer

The **Wi-Fi antenna leads**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Laptop Wi-Fi antenna wires are commonly routed around the screen, where the antennas can sit high in the system.

### Source Note

This card tests sourced antenna placement only; it does not infer a fault or prescribe a repair procedure.
