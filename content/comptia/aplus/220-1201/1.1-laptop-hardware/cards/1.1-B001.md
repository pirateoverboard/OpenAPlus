## Question

What does SO-DIMM stand for, and where is it commonly used?

## Answer

**Small Outline Dual In-line Memory Module**; used in laptops and many mobile devices.

## Instructor Notes

Desktop systems usually use larger DIMMs. SO-DIMMs are designed for compact systems.
