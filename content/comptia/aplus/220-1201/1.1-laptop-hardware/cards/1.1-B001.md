## Question

You are selecting replaceable memory for a typical laptop. Which module form factor should you look for?

## Answer

**SO-DIMM**.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

SO-DIMM is the compact memory-module form factor commonly used in laptops and other space-constrained systems.

### Exam Trap

Do not assume every laptop has a replaceable module; some systems use memory soldered to the system board.
