## Text

Bluetooth commonly provides short-range {{c1::personal area network (PAN)}} connectivity for nearby devices and peripherals.

## Extra

This role differs from Wi-Fi local-area networking and NFC's much shorter interaction range.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The PAN label helps connect Bluetooth's short range with its common peripheral use.
