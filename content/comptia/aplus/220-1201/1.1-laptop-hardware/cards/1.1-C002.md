## Text

Li-ion and LiPo laptop batteries do not develop the {{c1::memory effect}} associated with older battery technologies.

## Extra

Routine charging does not progressively reduce their capacity through memory effect.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Do not apply a full-discharge routine from older battery technologies solely to prevent memory effect in Li-ion or LiPo batteries.
