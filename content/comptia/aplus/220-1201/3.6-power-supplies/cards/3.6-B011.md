## Question

Why is simply choosing the highest-wattage power supply not always the best answer?

## Answer

A larger PSU does not make the computer faster, can cost more, and may not operate at its most efficient point for the actual load.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Appropriate wattage means enough capacity for the components and reasonable growth, not the largest number available.
