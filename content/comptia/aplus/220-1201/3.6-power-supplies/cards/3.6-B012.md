## Question

What does higher power-supply energy efficiency mean in practical terms?

## Answer

More of the AC input becomes usable DC output, with less power wasted as heat.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Efficiency affects heat and operating cost. It does not replace checking wattage, connector support, or input-voltage compatibility.
