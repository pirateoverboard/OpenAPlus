## Question

What is the basic electrical role of a desktop computer power supply?

## Answer

It converts AC wall power into the DC voltages used by internal computer components.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

The input side is regional wall power. The output side is the low-voltage DC power distributed inside the computer.
