## Text

A {{c1::20+4-pin}} connector can support either a 20-pin or 24-pin ATX motherboard power socket.

## Extra

The detachable four-pin section is attached when the motherboard expects 24 pins.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

This is the main motherboard power connector, not a PCIe auxiliary power connector.
