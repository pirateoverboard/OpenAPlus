## Question

When selecting a replacement power supply for a desktop with a high-performance video adapter, what should the technician verify?

## Hint

The graphics adapter often drives the power budget.

## Answer

Verify that the PSU wattage and required power connectors meet the system and video adapter requirements.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Video adapters are often the largest power draw in a desktop. Component documentation commonly lists a recommended power supply wattage.
