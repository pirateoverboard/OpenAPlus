## Question

A desktop with a manual input-voltage selector is being installed in a region that uses 220-240 VAC. What must be checked before powering it on?

## Hint

Match the supply input to the wall power before applying power.

## Answer

Verify that the power supply is set for the correct input-voltage range, 220-240 VAC, or that it supports that range automatically.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Input voltage is selected on the AC side of the power supply. A wrong setting can prevent operation or damage the unit.
