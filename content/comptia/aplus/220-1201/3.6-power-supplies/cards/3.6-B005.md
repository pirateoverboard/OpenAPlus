## Question

Why does Objective 3.6 compare 3.3 V, 5 V, and 12 V power-supply outputs?

## Answer

Different internal components use different DC output voltages, so the power supply must provide the required rails for the system.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The useful exam distinction is not a complete rail table. It is recognizing that a PSU must provide the correct DC outputs for the motherboard, storage, cooling, and adapters.
