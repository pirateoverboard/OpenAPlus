## Question

What is the main installation advantage of a modular power supply over a fixed-cable power supply?

## Answer

A modular power supply lets the technician attach only the cables needed for the build, reducing unused cabling and improving cable management and airflow.

## Instructor Notes

### Tradeoff

Modular cabling is cleaner but can cost more. Fixed-cable supplies may leave unused cables in the case or may not provide every cable a build needs.
