## Question

Before installing or replacing an internal computer power supply, what safety step should a technician take first?

## Answer

Disconnect the system from its power source before working inside it.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Power supplies handle hazardous electricity, and some devices can store charge after being unplugged. Follow the system documentation before touching components that may retain charge.
