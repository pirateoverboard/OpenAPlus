## Question

What is the practical advantage of an auto-voltage power supply when a computer may be used in different countries?

## Answer

It can use different supported input-voltage ranges without the technician manually setting a voltage switch.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Auto-voltage input helps with regional AC differences. It does not remove the need to check connectors, wattage, efficiency, or component requirements.
