## Question

Which power-supply output is most associated with higher-power devices such as PCIe adapters, drive motors, and cooling fans?

## Answer

The 12 V output.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The 12 V rail is commonly tied to motors, fans, high-power adapters, and many modern components. The objective still expects awareness of 3.3 V and 5 V outputs as well.
