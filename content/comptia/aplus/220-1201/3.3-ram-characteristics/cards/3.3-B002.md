## Question

A laptop needs a memory replacement. Which RAM form factor is the likely match: DIMM or SODIMM?

## Answer

**SODIMM** is the likely match because it is the smaller RAM module form factor commonly used in laptops and compact systems.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

DIMM and SODIMM are both memory modules, but physical size and slot type matter. A desktop-style DIMM will not fit a SODIMM slot.
