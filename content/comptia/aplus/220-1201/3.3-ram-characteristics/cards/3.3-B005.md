## Question

Before installing ECC RAM, what compatibility concern should a technician verify?

## Answer

Verify that the system or platform supports ECC and that the installed modules match that requirement.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

ECC is not only a module label. If the platform does not support ECC behavior, buying ECC modules does not guarantee error-correction support.
