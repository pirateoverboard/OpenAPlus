## Question

What should be checked before installing an expansion card?

## Answer

Check the motherboard slot type, available space, power requirements, operating system support, and driver requirements.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Expansion-card installation is not just plugging in a board. Documentation helps avoid slot mismatch, blocked adjacent slots, missing auxiliary power, or unsupported drivers.
