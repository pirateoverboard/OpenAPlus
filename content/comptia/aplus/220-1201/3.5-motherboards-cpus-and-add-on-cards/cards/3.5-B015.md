## Question

Why are temperature monitoring and fan settings commonly found in BIOS/UEFI?

## Answer

They let the motherboard monitor hardware temperatures and adjust or report fan behavior before relying on operating system software.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Firmware-level monitoring is useful because the system can check cooling hardware before the OS loads. Exact fan curves and thresholds are vendor-specific.
