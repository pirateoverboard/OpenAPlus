## Question

A hypervisor cannot use hardware virtualization features even though the CPU supports them. What firmware setting should be checked?

## Answer

Check whether CPU virtualization support is enabled in BIOS/UEFI.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The processor may support virtualization, but firmware can disable those features. Hypervisor settings do not replace the need to enable hardware support at the platform level.
