## Question

What should a technician verify before installing a device in an M.2 motherboard connector?

## Answer

Verify that the motherboard supports the device type, keying, size, and interface required by the M.2 device.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

M.2 is a physical connector family, not a guarantee that every M.2 device works in every M.2 slot. The motherboard documentation controls compatibility.
