## Question

Why might an organization disable or restrict USB access in BIOS/UEFI?

## Answer

To reduce the risk of unauthorized removable storage, data transfer, or booting from external USB devices.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

USB permissions are a firmware-level security control. They can stop access before the operating system loads or before OS policy is applied.
