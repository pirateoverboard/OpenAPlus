## Question

When choosing among ATX, microATX, and ITX motherboards, what is the main tradeoff a technician is evaluating?

## Answer

The tradeoff is system size versus expansion room. ATX usually provides the most room and expansion, microATX reduces size with fewer slots, and ITX prioritizes very small systems.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Form factor affects case fit, mounting, power layout, airflow, and expansion capacity. Exact dimensions are less useful than recognizing which board class fits the scenario.
