## Question

How do fans, heat sinks, and liquid cooling differ in a cooling plan?

## Answer

Fans move air through or across components, heat sinks conduct heat away from hot parts, and liquid cooling carries heat to a radiator where it can be removed.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Cooling is a system design issue. Airflow, cable routing, component placement, and heat output all affect whether the selected cooling approach works.
