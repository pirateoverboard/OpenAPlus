## Text

TPM expands to {{c1::Trusted Platform Module}}.

## Extra

A TPM provides hardware-backed cryptographic functions for a local system.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

A TPM is tied to one device, which is why it is commonly associated with local device encryption and trust.
