## Question

When would a technician install a sound card or a NIC instead of relying on motherboard functions?

## Answer

Install a sound card for additional or higher-quality audio input/output, and install a NIC to add, replace, or expand network connectivity.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Many motherboards include onboard audio and networking, but expansion cards are still useful when the built-in feature is missing, failed, insufficient, or needs additional ports.
