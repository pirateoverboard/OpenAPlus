## Question

What type of system is ITX best suited for compared with ATX?

## Answer

ITX is best suited for compact or small-form-factor systems where low size and limited expansion are acceptable.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Angle

ITX often appears in scenarios where the system is space-constrained or single-purpose. Do not choose it when the scenario requires many adapter cards.
