## Question

What is the key difference between a TPM and an HSM?

## Answer

A TPM provides hardware-backed cryptographic functions for one local device, while an HSM protects cryptographic keys for many systems or services.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

TPM is local trust tied to a computer. HSM is centralized or shared key protection, often implemented as a dedicated appliance or high-end security device.
