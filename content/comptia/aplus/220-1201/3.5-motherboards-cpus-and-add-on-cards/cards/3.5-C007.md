## Text

HSM expands to {{c1::Hardware Security Module}}.

## Extra

An HSM is often used to protect cryptographic keys for many systems or services.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

An HSM is the centralized or shared-key counterpart to the single-system TPM concept.
