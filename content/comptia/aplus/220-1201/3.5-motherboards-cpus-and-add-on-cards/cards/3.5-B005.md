## Question

What is the purpose of motherboard headers during a PC build?

## Answer

Headers are motherboard pin blocks used for small internal or case wiring connections.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Headers are easy to confuse with expansion slots. Expansion slots accept add-on cards; headers are smaller motherboard connection points for wiring.
