## Question

Which expansion card should be installed when a workstation needs to receive video input from an external camera or console?

## Answer

Install a capture card.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

A video card primarily outputs graphics to displays. A capture card is for video input, recording, or streaming from another source.
