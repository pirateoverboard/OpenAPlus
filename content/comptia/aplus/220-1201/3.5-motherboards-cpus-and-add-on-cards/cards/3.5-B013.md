## Question

What is the purpose of Secure Boot in UEFI?

## Answer

Secure Boot helps ensure that trusted, signed startup software is used during the boot process.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Secure Boot is not a boot password. It verifies trust in startup code; a password controls access to startup or firmware settings.
