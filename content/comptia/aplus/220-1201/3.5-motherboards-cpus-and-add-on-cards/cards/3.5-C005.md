## Text

UEFI expands to {{c1::Unified Extensible Firmware Interface}}.

## Extra

UEFI is the modern firmware interface listed in Objective 3.5.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

UEFI is firmware, not an operating system. It configures platform behavior before the OS takes over.
