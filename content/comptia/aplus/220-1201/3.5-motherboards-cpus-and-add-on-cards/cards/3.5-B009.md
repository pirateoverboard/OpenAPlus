## Question

What is the first compatibility item to check before installing a CPU in a motherboard?

## Answer

Check that the motherboard supports the CPU socket and processor family; AMD and Intel CPUs are not interchangeable between socket families.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Socket compatibility is a hard physical and electrical boundary. Chipset and firmware support can also matter, but the socket is the first practical install check.
