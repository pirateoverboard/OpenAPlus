## Question

How should a technician distinguish PCI from PCIe when selecting an expansion slot?

## Answer

PCI is an older parallel expansion bus, while PCIe is the newer serial expansion interface used for modern adapter cards and scalable lane widths.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

PCIe replaced PCI for most modern expansion-card use. PCIe slots may have different lane sizes, such as x1 or x16, so the card and slot documentation still matters.
