## Question

What is the practical difference between SATA and eSATA motherboard connections?

## Answer

SATA is used for internal storage connections, while eSATA is the external version used for external SATA storage devices.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Both are storage-related interfaces. The key exam distinction in this objective is internal connector versus external connector.
