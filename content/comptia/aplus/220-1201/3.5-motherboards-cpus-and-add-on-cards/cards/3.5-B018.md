## Question

Why is ARM commonly chosen for mobile, embedded, or IoT-style systems?

## Answer

ARM is commonly chosen because it can provide efficient processing with lower power use and lower heat output.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

The exam contrast is architecture fit, not brand loyalty. ARM is often associated with efficient and compact devices; x86/x64 is common in traditional PC platforms.
