## Question

What is the purpose of thermal paste or a thermal pad between a CPU and a heat sink?

## Answer

It improves thermal contact so heat can move efficiently from the CPU into the heat sink.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

The heat sink dissipates heat; the paste or pad improves the contact surface between the component and the heat sink. Thermal pads are generally not reusable after removal.
