## Question

How does a boot password differ from a BIOS or supervisor password?

## Answer

A boot password blocks the system from starting until the password is entered, while a BIOS or supervisor password restricts changes to firmware settings.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Lost firmware passwords may require a motherboard reset procedure, such as a jumper reset, depending on the system. Always check vendor documentation before changing firmware security settings.
