## Question

What is the purpose of audit logs on a public or shared printer?

## Answer

Audit logs help track printer usage, costs, and security-related events.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Logging may be available from the printer, print server, or operating system events. The key idea is accountability for shared-device activity.
