## Question

Which printer setting should be enabled when a user wants output on both sides of each page?

## Answer

Enable duplex printing, assuming the printer supports it.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Duplex is about two-sided output. It is separate from orientation, tray selection, or quality.
