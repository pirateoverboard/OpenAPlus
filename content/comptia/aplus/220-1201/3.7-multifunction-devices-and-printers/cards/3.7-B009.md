## Question

What is the key deployment difference between a printer share and a print server?

## Answer

A printer share depends on the computer connected to the printer sharing it, while a print server manages jobs for a shared printer without relying on a user's workstation as the sharing point.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

With a printer share, the sharing computer must be running. A print-server deployment centralizes queue and job management for shared access.
