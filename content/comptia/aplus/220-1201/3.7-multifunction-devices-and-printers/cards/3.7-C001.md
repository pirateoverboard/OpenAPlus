## Text

MFD stands for {{c1::multifunction device}}.

## Extra

An MFD can combine printing, scanning, faxing, network, phone-line, and web-printing functions.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The acronym appears often in deployment discussions for all-in-one office devices.
