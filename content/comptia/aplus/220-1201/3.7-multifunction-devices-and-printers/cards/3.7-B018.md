## Question

When should a user choose an automatic document feeder instead of a flatbed scanner?

## Answer

Choose an ADF for scanning multiple pages automatically; use the flatbed for single pages or items that need to lie flat.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

The ADF feeds pages through the scanner. The flatbed is better for one-off pages, fragile originals, or items that should not be fed through rollers.
