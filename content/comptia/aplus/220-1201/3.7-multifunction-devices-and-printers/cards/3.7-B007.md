## Question

When is Ethernet the appropriate printer connectivity choice?

## Answer

Use Ethernet when the printer should connect directly to the wired network with an RJ45 network connection.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Ethernet lets the printer operate as a network device instead of depending on one attached workstation's USB connection.
