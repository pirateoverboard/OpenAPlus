## Question

What role does firmware play in a multifunction printer?

## Answer

Firmware is the device's internal software that starts the system, connects to the network, interprets incoming data, and runs the print process.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Firmware is updated for compatibility and bug fixes, but the update should use the vendor's supported process from a stable platform and power source.
