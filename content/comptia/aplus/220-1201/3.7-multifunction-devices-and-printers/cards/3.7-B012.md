## Question

A user needs a job to print on company letterhead loaded in a specific tray. Which configuration setting matters most?

## Answer

Tray settings; select the tray that contains the letterhead or required paper.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Tray settings map the print job to the correct paper source. This is separate from duplex, orientation, and quality settings.
