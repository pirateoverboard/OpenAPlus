## Question

Before unboxing a large shared multifunction printer, what should a technician verify about the setup location?

## Answer

Verify that the location has enough space, safe walkways, power, network access, and accessibility for users.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Deployment problems often start before configuration. A device that blocks walkways or lacks power/network access will not be usable even with the correct driver.
