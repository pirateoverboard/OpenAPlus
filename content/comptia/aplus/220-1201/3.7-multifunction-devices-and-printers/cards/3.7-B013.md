## Question

Which printer setting should be adjusted when a user needs to change resolution, color, greyscale, or color-saving behavior?

## Answer

Quality settings.

## Instructor Notes

### Distinction

Quality settings affect how output is rendered. They do not choose the paper tray, page orientation, or two-sided printing.
