## Question

What makes a device a multifunction device in Objective 3.7?

## Answer

It combines printer functions with other capabilities such as scanning, faxing, network connectivity, phone-line connectivity, or web-based printing.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Multifunction devices require more deployment planning than a simple single-purpose printer because several services may need power, network, phone, driver, and access configuration.
