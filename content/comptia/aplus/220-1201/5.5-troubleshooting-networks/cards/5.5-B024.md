## Question

A port-flapping problem follows the same workstation and patch cable to a
different switch port. What does that suggest?

## Answer

**The issue likely follows the endpoint or its cable path, not the original
switch port.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

When the symptom moves with the device or cable, the original switch interface
is less likely to be the root cause. The next checks should focus on the
endpoint NIC, patch cable, dock, wall run, or local connection path.
