## Question

An escalation team needs precise timing evidence for a latency complaint. What
method can provide high-detail packet timing?

## Answer

**Collect packet captures from the relevant sides of the path for timing
comparison.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Packet captures can show when packets leave and arrive at different points.
That timing evidence is useful when ordinary user reports or single ping tests
are not precise enough for escalation.
