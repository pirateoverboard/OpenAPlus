## Question

An authentication problem happens in the background, and the user never sees a
clear error message. What can help reveal the failed communication?

## Answer

**A packet capture or network trace from the affected system.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Some authentication failures are hidden inside background service traffic. A
capture can show whether the system reaches the authentication service and
whether communication fails before, during, or after the authentication attempt.
