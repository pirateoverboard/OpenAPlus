## Question

A user moved a desktop, and now the wired network connection is down. The link
light is off. What is the most useful next test?

## Hint

Use a simple substitution test before changing software settings.

## Answer

**Try a known-good Ethernet cable and, if needed, a known-good jack or switch
port.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

After a move, the most likely change is physical: a loose patch cable, damaged
cable, bad wall jack, or wrong switch port. A known-good substitution narrows
the fault without introducing unnecessary configuration changes.
