## Question

Users report poor Internet performance, and you need a quick throughput
comparison against expected service. What test is useful?

## Answer

**Run a speed test from an appropriate client or network location.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A speed test gives a measurable data point for download, upload, and sometimes
latency. It does not diagnose every cause by itself, but it helps compare the
observed performance with expected service or historical baselines.
