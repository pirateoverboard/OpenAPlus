## Question

A loopback ping succeeds. What should pinging the computer's own assigned IP
address help verify next?

## Answer

**Whether the host can respond through its configured network interface and IP
configuration.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Pinging the local assigned address moves one step beyond loopback. It helps
confirm the host's network stack, local address, and adapter configuration
before testing the gateway or remote destinations.
