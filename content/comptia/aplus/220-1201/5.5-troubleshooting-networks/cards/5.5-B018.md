## Question

Voice and video calls are choppy even when the connection does not fully drop.
What network condition is a likely cause?

## Answer

**Jitter, or inconsistent delay between packets.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Real-time traffic is sensitive to uneven packet arrival. A connection can have
enough bandwidth for a speed test and still sound bad if delay varies
significantly during the call.
