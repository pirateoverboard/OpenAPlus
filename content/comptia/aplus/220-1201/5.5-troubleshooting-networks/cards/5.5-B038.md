## Question

You are escalating a no-connectivity issue to the network team. What concise
evidence should you include?

## Answer

**Link-light status, cable or port tests, IP configuration, gateway test result,
remote test result, affected scope, and recent changes.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Escalation is more useful when it describes what has already been isolated.
This prevents the next technician from repeating basic checks and helps them
focus on the unresolved part of the path.
