## Question

A port-flapping problem stops when the workstation is moved to another switch
port. What does that result suggest?

## Answer

**The original switch interface or its configuration is more likely involved.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

If the endpoint and cable path work on a different port, the evidence shifts
back to the original switch interface. The technician should document the test
and escalate or repair according to local network procedures.
