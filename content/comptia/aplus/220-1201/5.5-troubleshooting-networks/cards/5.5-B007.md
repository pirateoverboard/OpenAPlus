## Question

A Windows laptop reports limited connectivity and has a 169.254.x.x address.
What is the likely network issue?

## Answer

**The device did not receive a usable DHCP address and assigned itself a local
automatic address.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

An APIPA-style address is useful troubleshooting evidence. It usually means the
device is not successfully receiving DHCP configuration, so the technician
should investigate DHCP reachability, wireless association, VLAN, cabling, or
local network access instead of treating the Internet as the first suspect.
