## Question

Wi-Fi drops mainly in a break room when appliances are in use. What likely
wireless cause should you investigate first?

## Answer

**Radio interference affecting the wireless signal.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Intermittent wireless problems that correlate with a location or equipment use
often point to interference. The technician should gather timing and location
details before replacing client hardware or assuming the access point has
failed.
