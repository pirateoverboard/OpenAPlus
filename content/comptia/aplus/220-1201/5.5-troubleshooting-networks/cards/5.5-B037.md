## Question

You are opening an ISP ticket for intermittent Internet connectivity. What
should you document before escalation?

## Answer

**Affected scope, timestamps, internal versus external test results, ping or
traceroute evidence, speed test results, and account/contact information.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Clear documentation helps the provider reproduce and triage the issue. It also
shows that local checks were performed before escalation.
