## Text

SNR stands for {{c1::signal-to-noise ratio}}.

## Extra

In wireless troubleshooting, low SNR means the useful signal is not strong
enough compared with background noise.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The acronym is worth memorizing because it appears in wireless troubleshooting
evidence. It helps connect survey readings to real user symptoms such as drops,
slow speeds, and unstable connections.
