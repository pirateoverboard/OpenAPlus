## Question

Wi-Fi is inconsistent in a room with many reflective surfaces, even though the
access point is nearby. What wireless effect should be considered?

## Answer

**Signal bounce or multipath interference.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Wireless signals can reflect and arrive out of phase, causing unstable service
in specific locations. The important troubleshooting clue is that the symptom
is location-specific despite apparent proximity to the access point.
