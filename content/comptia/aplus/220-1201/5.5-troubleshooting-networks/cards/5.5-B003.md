## Question

A technician pings 127.0.0.1 while troubleshooting no connectivity. What does a
successful reply show?

## Answer

**The local TCP/IP stack is responding on the computer.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The loopback test stays inside the host. A successful reply does not prove the
network cable, wireless link, gateway, DNS, or Internet path works; it only
supports that the local protocol stack can respond.
