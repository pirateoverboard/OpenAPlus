## Question

Internet speed looks normal, but multiple VoIP phones at one site still have
poor call quality. What local network area should you check next?

## Answer

**Check the local network equipment and path used by the phones, such as the
router, switch, cabling, and configuration.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Once the broad Internet path looks healthy, the investigation should move to
the local path shared by the affected phones. Multiple devices at one site make
one bad handset less likely.
