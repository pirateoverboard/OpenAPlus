## Question

Internal tests are normal, but external pings and route tests show Internet
path failures. What is the appropriate escalation?

## Answer

**Contact the ISP or provider with the gathered test evidence and account
information.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Provider escalation is stronger when the technician can show internal resources
work and external paths fail. Account details, timestamps, affected scope, and
test output help the provider act on the ticket.
