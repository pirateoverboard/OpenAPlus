## Question

A workstation's switch port is flapping, and a known-good cable did not fix it.
What is a useful next isolation test?

## Answer

**Move the connection to another known-good switch port and compare the result.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Changing one variable at a time helps isolate whether the issue follows the
device/cable path or stays with the switch interface. This is more useful than
guessing which component to replace.
