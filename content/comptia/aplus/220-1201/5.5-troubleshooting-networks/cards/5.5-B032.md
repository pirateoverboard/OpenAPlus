## Question

Several users report Internet drops at the same time, but local network
resources stay available. What should you determine first?

## Hint

Separate local impact from external impact.

## Answer

**Determine the scope by testing internal resources and external destinations
from more than one affected client.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

If local resources remain reachable while external destinations fail, the
problem may be on the Internet edge or provider path. Scope evidence is stronger
than a single user's report.
