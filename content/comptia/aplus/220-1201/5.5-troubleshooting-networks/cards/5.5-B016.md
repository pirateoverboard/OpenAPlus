## Question

Performance is good on the local LAN but becomes slow after one network hop.
What does that test result suggest?

## Answer

**Focus on that hop or the path beyond it, such as congestion, errors,
filtering, or an upstream link problem.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Comparing performance across segments helps isolate where the slowdown begins.
The answer is not to replace the user's workstation; the evidence points to a
specific boundary in the network path.
