## Question

Several users report poor VoIP call quality. What broad network condition
should you verify early?

## Hint

Start with the path real-time media depends on.

## Answer

**Verify Internet connection performance, especially speed and latency.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

VoIP problems often surface when the WAN or Internet path cannot support
real-time traffic consistently. Checking speed and latency early helps separate
a provider/path problem from one bad phone or headset.
