## Question

What does a successful ping to the default gateway prove during a no-network
troubleshooting path?

## Answer

**The computer can reach the local network's router or gateway.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The default gateway is the boundary between the local subnet and other networks.
If it responds, the local link, local IP settings, and path to the router are
more likely working, so the next question is whether traffic can go beyond that
router.
