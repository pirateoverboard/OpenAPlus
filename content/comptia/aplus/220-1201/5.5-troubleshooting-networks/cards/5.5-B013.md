## Question

Several users in the same corner of a building report weak Wi-Fi, while nearby
areas work normally. What likely infrastructure issue should you investigate?

## Answer

**Access point placement or coverage for that area.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Multiple users affected in the same physical area points away from one bad
laptop. It is more useful to check coverage and AP placement before replacing
client adapters.
