## Question

A user says, "The network is slow." What should the technician do before
changing network equipment?

## Hint

Turn the complaint into measurable evidence.

## Answer

**Confirm the symptom with a specific application, destination, time, or network
test.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

"Slow network" can mean an application issue, wireless issue, local device
problem, server problem, or provider issue. Gathering measurable evidence keeps
the troubleshooting path systematic and avoids guessing.
