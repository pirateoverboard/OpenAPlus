## Question

A wireless survey shows low signal-to-noise ratio in the area where users drop
connections. What does that suggest?

## Answer

**The useful wireless signal is not sufficiently stronger than the surrounding
noise.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Signal strength alone is not the whole story. A client can be close to an
access point and still perform poorly if the noise level is high enough to make
the signal difficult to distinguish.
