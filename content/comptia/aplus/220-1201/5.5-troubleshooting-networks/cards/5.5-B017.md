## Question

Simple ping and speed tests do not explain an ongoing slow-network complaint.
What evidence-gathering method can show what traffic is actually occurring?

## Hint

Move from summary tests to traffic evidence.

## Answer

**Use a packet capture at an appropriate point in the path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Packet captures can reveal retransmissions, failed sessions, excessive chatter,
or unexpected traffic. For A+ scope, the learning target is when capture
evidence becomes useful, not exact capture syntax.
