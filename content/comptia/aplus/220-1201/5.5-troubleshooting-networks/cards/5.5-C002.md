## Text

{{c1::Jitter}} is variation in packet delay and can make real-time voice or
video feel choppy.

## Extra

Jitter can be noticeable even when a connection does not fully disconnect.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The term is a compact troubleshooting clue. It connects directly to help desk
reports such as choppy calls, robotic audio, or unstable video meetings.
