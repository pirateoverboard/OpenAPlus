## Text

SLA stands for {{c1::service level agreement}}.

## Extra

An SLA helps set expectations for service availability, provider response, or
other support commitments.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The acronym is useful during ISP escalation. It gives the technician the right
language for discussing expectations without promising an unsupported repair
time.
