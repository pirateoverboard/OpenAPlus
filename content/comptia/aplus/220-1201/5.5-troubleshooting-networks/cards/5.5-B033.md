## Question

Internet connectivity is intermittent, and pings show occasional loss to a
remote destination. What path test can show where traffic stops or slows?

## Answer

**Run a traceroute or equivalent route-tracing test to the remote destination.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Route tracing helps identify whether loss or delay appears near the local
network, at the provider edge, or farther away. It is evidence for escalation,
not a guarantee that the failing hop is the root cause by itself.
