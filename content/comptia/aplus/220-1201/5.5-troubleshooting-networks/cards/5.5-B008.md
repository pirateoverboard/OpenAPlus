## Question

A computer has a normal-looking DHCP address but still cannot reach websites.
What is the best next network test?

## Hint

Do not stop at the address; test the path.

## Answer

**Ping the default gateway, then a known remote IP address, to isolate whether
the failure is local or beyond the router.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

A valid address only proves the host received configuration. Gateway and remote
IP tests show where connectivity stops, which is more useful than immediately
changing DNS, browser, or application settings.
