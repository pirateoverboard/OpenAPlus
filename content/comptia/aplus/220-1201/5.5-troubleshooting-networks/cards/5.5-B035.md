## Question

An ISP outage is confirmed. What document helps set expectations for provider
response or uptime commitments?

## Answer

**The service level agreement.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

An SLA defines service expectations such as availability, response, or support
terms. It helps the technician communicate realistic next steps instead of
promising a repair time without a contractual basis.
