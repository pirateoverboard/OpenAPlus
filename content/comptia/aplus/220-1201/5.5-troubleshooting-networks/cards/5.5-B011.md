## Question

Wi-Fi became unreliable after a manual access point channel change. What should
the technician verify?

## Hint

Review the most recent wireless configuration change.

## Answer

**Verify that the wireless channel or frequency configuration is appropriate
for the environment.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Incorrect or congested channel selection can create wireless instability even
when the access point is functioning. This card tests the configuration clue,
not a full wireless design exercise.
