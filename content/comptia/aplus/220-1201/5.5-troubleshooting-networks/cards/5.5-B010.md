## Question

A user's Wi-Fi works near the access point but drops at the far end of the
office. What should you check first?

## Answer

**Check wireless signal strength and coverage in that area.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Distance and obstacles can weaken a Wi-Fi signal enough to cause roaming,
disconnects, or slow speeds. Measuring or comparing signal strength in the
problem area is a focused test before changing unrelated client settings.
