## Question

A switch port repeatedly goes up and down for the same workstation. What should
you check first?

## Hint

Start with the simplest part of the physical path.

## Answer

**Check or replace the cable and verify the wiring path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Port flapping often starts with a physical link problem. Cable and wiring checks
are low-risk and help avoid replacing switch hardware before proving the link
path is stable.
