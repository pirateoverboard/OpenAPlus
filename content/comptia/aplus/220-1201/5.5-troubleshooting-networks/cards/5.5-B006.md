## Question

A workstation can ping its default gateway, but it cannot ping a known remote
IP address. What does that result suggest?

## Hint

The local boundary responds, but the farther destination does not.

## Answer

**The failure is likely beyond the local gateway or on the upstream path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Gateway success makes a bad local cable or local IP configuration less likely.
The failed remote IP test shifts the investigation past the local router instead
of keeping the focus on the workstation alone.
