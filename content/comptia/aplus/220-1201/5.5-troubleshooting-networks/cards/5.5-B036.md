## Question

Internet drops are intermittent, and the provider asks for evidence. What
performance data should you collect over time?

## Answer

**Document timestamps with ping results, traceroute results, and speed test
results when symptoms occur.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Intermittent problems are difficult to prove with one clean test. Time-stamped
evidence helps correlate user impact with measurable loss, latency, route
changes, or throughput drops.
