## Text

The IPv4 loopback address commonly used to test the local TCP/IP stack is
{{c1::127.0.0.1}}.

## Extra

The loopback test stays on the local host. It does not prove the physical
network link, gateway, DNS, or Internet path works.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

This compact recall supports the troubleshooting sequence for no-connectivity
issues. The address itself is worth memorizing because it appears often in
entry-level network isolation steps.
