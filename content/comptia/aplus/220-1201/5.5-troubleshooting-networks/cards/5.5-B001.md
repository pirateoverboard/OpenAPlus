## Question

A wired workstation has no network connectivity, and the Ethernet port has no
link light. What should you check first?

## Hint

Start with the evidence closest to the device.

## Answer

**Check the physical connection: cable seating, cable condition, and the switch
port or wall jack.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

No link light points to a physical-layer problem before it points to IP,
gateway, DNS, or application settings. Reseating or swapping the cable and
checking the connected port can quickly confirm whether the device has an
actual network link.
