## Question

VoIP quality problems persist, and basic speed and ping tests do not explain
them. What can provide more detailed evidence?

## Answer

**A packet capture or network performance analysis at the affected path.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Voice problems can depend on timing, loss, retransmissions, or traffic handling
that simple tests miss. Capture or performance evidence helps escalation teams
avoid guessing.
