## Question

A user can reach the network, but one service repeatedly reports authentication
failures after a long session. What simple step should you try first?

## Hint

Start with a low-risk account/session check.

## Answer

**Have the user sign out and sign back in, or otherwise refresh the session
according to the service's normal process.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Authentication symptoms can come from expired sessions, cached credentials, or
account state rather than a broken network path. A session refresh is a low-risk
first step when the symptom is limited to authentication.
