## Question

A user reports that cloud apps respond slowly, but local file shares are fast.
What should you measure next?

## Answer

**Measure latency along the path to the remote service or beyond the local
network.**

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Fast local resources make a general workstation problem less likely. Latency
tests help identify whether delay begins after traffic leaves the local
network.
