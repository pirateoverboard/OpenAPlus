## Question

A cloud provider supplies virtual servers, storage, and networking, while the customer manages the operating system and applications. Which service model is this?

## Answer

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS).

## Instructor Notes

IaaS provides the infrastructure layer. The customer still has significant management responsibility above that layer.
