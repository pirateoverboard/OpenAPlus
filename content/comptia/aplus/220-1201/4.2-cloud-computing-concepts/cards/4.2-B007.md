## Question

A developer uses a cloud-hosted application framework and does not manage the underlying servers or operating systems. Which service model is this?

## Answer

Platform as a Service (PaaS).

## Instructor Notes

PaaS sits between raw infrastructure and a finished application. The customer builds on the platform instead of managing the full server stack.
