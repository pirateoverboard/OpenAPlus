## Question

How can you distinguish elasticity from availability in a cloud scenario?

## Answer

Elasticity changes resource capacity as demand changes; availability keeps the service reachable when users need it.

## Instructor Notes

This is a common distractor split. Scaling for load points to elasticity; continuing service through failures points to availability.
