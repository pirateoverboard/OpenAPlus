## Question

In cloud computing, what is the difference between shared resources and dedicated resources?

## Answer

Shared resources are drawn from infrastructure used by multiple customers or workloads, while dedicated resources are reserved for one customer, organization, or workload.

## Instructor Notes

This distinction is about resource allocation, not whether the service is secure. Shared cloud platforms still use logical separation between tenants.
