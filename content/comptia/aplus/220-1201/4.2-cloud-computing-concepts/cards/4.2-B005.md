## Question

Several organizations with similar requirements share the same cloud environment. Which cloud deployment model best matches this?

## Answer

Community cloud.

## Instructor Notes

Community cloud is shared by a defined group of organizations. The trap is confusing "shared by a community" with public cloud, which is broadly available to many customers.
