## Question

In cloud metering, what is the difference between ingress and egress traffic?

## Answer

Ingress traffic enters the cloud service; egress traffic leaves the cloud service.

## Instructor Notes

Cloud cost questions often care about traffic direction. Egress is especially worth recognizing because moving data out of a cloud service may be metered separately.
