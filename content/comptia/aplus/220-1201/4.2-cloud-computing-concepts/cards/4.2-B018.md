## Question

A public cloud customer uses compute capacity from a large provider pool also used by other customers. Which resource characteristic is being used?

## Answer

Shared resources.

## Instructor Notes

The clue is provider capacity used by multiple customers. This card reinforces resource sharing without implying that tenant data is mixed.
