## Question

How does non-metered cloud utilization differ from metered utilization?

## Answer

Non-metered utilization uses a fixed block, allocation, or flat access model instead of charging directly for each measured unit of use.

The official objective names metered utilization, and non-metered appears as a useful contrast in the approved notes. Keep this as a contrast card rather than expanding into provider pricing examples.
