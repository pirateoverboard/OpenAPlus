## Question

What is the main idea of cloud computing in Objective 4.2?

## Answer

Cloud computing provides scalable, on-demand computing resources through cloud-style infrastructure that can be provisioned and consumed as needed.

## Instructor Notes

Keep the answer at the concept level. This wording includes public, private, hybrid, and community cloud models without implying that every cloud must be externally provider-managed.
