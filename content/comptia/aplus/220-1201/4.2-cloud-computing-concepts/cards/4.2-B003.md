## Question

A provider offers cloud services over the Internet to many unrelated customers. Which cloud deployment model is this?

## Answer

Public cloud.

## Instructor Notes

Public cloud focuses on broad provider access and shared provider infrastructure. It does not mean customer data is publicly visible.
