## Question

What does multitenancy mean in cloud computing?

## Answer

Multiple customers or tenants use the same cloud infrastructure while their data and access remain logically separated.

## Instructor Notes

Multitenancy helps explain public cloud scale. It does not mean tenants can see or manage each other's data.
