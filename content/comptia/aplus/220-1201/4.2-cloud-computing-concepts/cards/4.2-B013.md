## Question

A cloud application automatically increases resources during heavy demand and reduces them when demand drops. Which cloud characteristic is this?

## Answer

Elasticity.

## Instructor Notes

Elasticity is about scaling up and down with demand. It is not simply having redundant systems or a large initial capacity.
