## Question

Across IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS, what changes as the provider manages more of the service stack?

## Answer

The customer has less responsibility for the underlying infrastructure and platform, while the provider takes over more management of the stack.

## Instructor Notes

This is the core service-model comparison. IaaS leaves more to the customer, PaaS manages the platform, and SaaS delivers a finished application.
