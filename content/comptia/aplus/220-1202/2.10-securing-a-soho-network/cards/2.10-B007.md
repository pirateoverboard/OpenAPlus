## Question

A small office must host a public service while adding separation between that
service and its internal systems. Where should it place the public resource?

## Answer

In a screened subnet.

## Instructor Notes

A screened subnet is positioned between the Internet and the internal network
for public-facing resources. It is the current term for what was commonly
called a DMZ in this network design.
