## Question

Applications on a SOHO network are automatically opening inbound router ports
without administrator approval. Which feature should be disabled unless a
required application needs it?

## Answer

Universal Plug and Play (UPnP).

## Instructor Notes

UPnP supports zero-configuration discovery and lets internal applications
request inbound openings. Disabling it restores deliberate administrator
control over that exposure.
