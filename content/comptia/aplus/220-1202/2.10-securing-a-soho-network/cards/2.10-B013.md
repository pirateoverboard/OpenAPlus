## Question

Several active network jacks in a public conference room are not needed. What
should the administrator do with their switch ports?

## Answer

Administratively disable the unused ports.

## Instructor Notes

An enabled jack is a connection point even when no approved device currently
uses it. Disabling the associated port removes that access until an
administrator deliberately enables it again.
