## Question

A SOHO router has guest wireless access enabled by default, but the business
does not provide guest access. What should the administrator do?

## Answer

Disable the guest network.

## Instructor Notes

An unneeded guest network creates unnecessary access. If guest access is
actually required, it should be deliberately configured and protected with
WPA2 or WPA3 rather than left open.
