## Question

A router is mounted where visitors can reach its cables and reset controls.
What physical security change should the administrator make?

## Answer

Move or secure the router in a location accessible only to authorized people.

## Instructor Notes

Physical access can bypass or disrupt logical settings. Placement should also
preserve useful wireless coverage and allow authorized maintenance, so it
should be planned rather than merely made inconvenient.
