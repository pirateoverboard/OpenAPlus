## Question

A new access point broadcasts a factory SSID that reveals its brand. What
wireless setting should the administrator change?

## Answer

Replace the default SSID with a less obvious network name.

## Instructor Notes

The service set identifier is the wireless network name. Changing an obvious
default avoids advertising a factory name, but the new name still needs real
authentication and encryption protections.
