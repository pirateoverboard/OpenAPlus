## Question

A SOHO router needs administration only from inside the local network. Which
management-access change best matches that requirement?

## Answer

Disable remote management and allow management only from the local network.

## Instructor Notes

Limiting where administrative sessions can originate reduces exposure of the
management interface. Strong credentials and an additional factor, if
available, protect the account itself and complement the location restriction.
