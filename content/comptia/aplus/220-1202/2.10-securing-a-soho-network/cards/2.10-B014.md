## Question

An internal service must accept connections sent to a specific port on the
SOHO router's public address. Which firewall setting provides that mapping?

## Answer

Port forwarding (port mapping).

## Instructor Notes

The rule persistently maps an external IP address and port to an internal IP
address and port. The two port numbers can differ, so the required mapping
should be stated and configured deliberately.
