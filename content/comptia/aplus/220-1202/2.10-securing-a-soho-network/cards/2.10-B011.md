## Question

A SOHO wireless network uses one shared credential and must not remain open.
Which encryption configuration should the administrator choose?

## Answer

WPA2-Personal or WPA3-Personal with a pre-shared key.

## Instructor Notes

Personal mode fits a shared-key SOHO requirement. Enterprise mode instead
authenticates users individually through an authentication service and is a
different deployment model.
