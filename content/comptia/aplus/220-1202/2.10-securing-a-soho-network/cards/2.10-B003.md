## Question

A router manufacturer releases firmware that corrects a security flaw. What
should the administrator do to apply that protection?

## Answer

Update the router to the current supported firmware.

## Instructor Notes

SOHO appliances commonly receive fixes through manufacturer firmware. Keeping
firmware current applies security patches as well as other supported fixes and
improvements.
