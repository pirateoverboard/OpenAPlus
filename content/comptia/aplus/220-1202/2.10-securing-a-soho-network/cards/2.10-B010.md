## Question

An administrator disables SSID broadcast. What security result should the
administrator expect?

## Answer

The network name disappears from ordinary discovery lists, but wireless
analysis can still identify it.

## Instructor Notes

SSID suppression provides obscurity rather than strong access control. It does
not replace WPA2 or WPA3 encryption and authentication.
