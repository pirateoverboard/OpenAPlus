## Question

A small office wants its router to block websites in an inappropriate-content
category. Which setting should it configure?

## Answer

Content filtering.

## Instructor Notes

Content filtering can act on URLs or website categories. IP filtering instead
fits a requirement stated in terms of specific network addresses or ranges.
