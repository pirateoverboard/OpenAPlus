## Question

A SOHO administrator must block traffic associated with one specific IP
address while leaving other addresses allowed. Which router setting matches
the requirement?

## Answer

An IP filtering deny rule for that address.

## Instructor Notes

IP filtering makes allow-or-deny decisions using addresses or address ranges.
Content filtering is the nearby alternative when the requirement concerns a
URL or category within the traffic instead.
