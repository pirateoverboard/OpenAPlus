## Text

TKIP expands to {{c1::Temporal Key Integrity Protocol}}.

## Extra

TKIP was the transitional encryption method used with the original WPA on
existing hardware.

## Instructor Notes

Associate TKIP with the older WPA transition. AES is the stronger encryption
choice associated with WPA2.
