## Question

Which authentication protocol can let wireless 802.1X, VPN, router, or switch
services validate users through a central authentication server?

## Answer

Remote Authentication Dial-in User Service (RADIUS).

## Instructor Notes

RADIUS is broadly supported for centralized AAA. Despite “dial-in” in its
name, its use is not limited to dial-up access.
