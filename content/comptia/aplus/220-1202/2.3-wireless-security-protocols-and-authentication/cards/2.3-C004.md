## Text

TACACS+ is the current “plus” version of {{c1::Terminal Access Controller Access-Control System}}.

## Extra

TACACS+ is a remote authentication protocol commonly associated with Cisco
network devices.

## Instructor Notes

The plus sign distinguishes the current protocol described by the objective
from earlier TACACS versions.
