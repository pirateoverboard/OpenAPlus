## Question

A Microsoft-oriented network needs ticket-based single sign-on and mutual
authentication between clients and services. Which authentication protocol
fits?

## Answer

Kerberos.

## Instructor Notes

Kerberos uses cryptographic tickets after the initial authentication, reducing
repeated credential entry and helping resist replay and on-path attacks.
