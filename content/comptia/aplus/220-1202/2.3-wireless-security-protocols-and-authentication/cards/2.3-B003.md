## Question

Which two security improvements can justify upgrading a compatible wireless
network from WPA2 to WPA3?

## Answer

WPA3 offers stronger cryptographic options and better protection for the
initial key exchange.

## Instructor Notes

WPA3 also supports encryption on open networks, which 2.3-B004 tests
separately.
