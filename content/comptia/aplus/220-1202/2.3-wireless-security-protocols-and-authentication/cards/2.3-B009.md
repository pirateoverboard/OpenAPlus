## Question

A network-device environment, particularly one built around Cisco equipment,
needs a compatible remote authentication protocol. Which Objective 2.3 method
is the likely fit?

## Answer

TACACS+.

## Instructor Notes

Protocol selection depends on what the devices and authentication service
support. TACACS+ is commonly associated with Cisco equipment; it is an
authentication method, not wireless encryption.
