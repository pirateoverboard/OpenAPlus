## Question

When compatible hardware offers AES or TKIP for wireless encryption, which
should be selected for stronger protection?

## Answer

AES.

## Instructor Notes

TKIP let the original WPA improve security on older hardware and served as a
bridge to WPA2. AES is the stronger encryption choice associated with WPA2.
