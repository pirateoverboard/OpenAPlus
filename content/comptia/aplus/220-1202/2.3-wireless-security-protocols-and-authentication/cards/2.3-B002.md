## Question

How do the encryption methods commonly associated with WPA2 and the original
WPA differ?

## Answer

WPA2 uses Advanced Encryption Standard (AES), while the original WPA used the
transitional Temporal Key Integrity Protocol (TKIP).

## Instructor Notes

AES is stronger than TKIP. This card tests the protocol-to-encryption
association; 2.3-B005 separately tests which method to select when both are
offered.
