## Question

A wireless design must both protect traffic from listeners and verify which
users may connect. Why are encryption and authentication both needed?

## Answer

Encryption protects the wireless data, while authentication verifies the user
or device requesting access.

## Instructor Notes

These controls solve different problems. WPA encryption does not by itself
provide individual identity verification, and an authentication server does
not replace wireless traffic encryption.
