## Text

RADIUS expands to {{c1::Remote Authentication Dial-in User Service}}.

## Extra

RADIUS provides centralized AAA for services such as wireless 802.1X and VPN
access; it is not limited to dial-up connections.

## Instructor Notes

Do not let “Dial-in” make the term seem obsolete. The applied Basic card tests
its modern centralized-authentication role.
