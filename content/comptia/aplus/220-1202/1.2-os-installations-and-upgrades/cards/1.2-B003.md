## Question

A user needs Windows and Linux available on the same computer and wants to
choose between them at startup. What installation approach supports this?

## Answer

Configure a multiboot system with separate bootable operating-system
installations.

## Instructor Notes

Multiboot presents a boot menu for selecting among installed operating systems.
It is different from replacing the old OS with a clean install.
