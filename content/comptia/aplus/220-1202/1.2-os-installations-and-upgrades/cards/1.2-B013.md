## Question

How does a full format differ from a quick format during installation?

## Answer

A quick format creates a new file table, while a full format writes zeros
across the disk and checks for bad sectors.

## Instructor Notes

Quick format can make data appear erased without overwriting all of it. Full
format is slower and destructive, so the choice matters when data recovery or
disk checking is a concern.
