## Question

What are the major limitations of MBR partitioning compared with GPT?

## Answer

MBR is limited to 2 TB partitions and up to four primary partitions per hard
disk.

## Instructor Notes

One primary partition can be marked active. Extra logical partitions require an
extended partition, but that does not remove MBR's older design limits.
