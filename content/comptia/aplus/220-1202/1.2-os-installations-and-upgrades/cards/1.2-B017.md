## Question

A technician has OS installation files stored on a removable SSD or external
drive that can boot through USB. Which boot-source category fits?

## Answer

Solid-state/flash drive or external/hot-swappable drive boot media.

## Instructor Notes

The exam lists these as separate boot-method examples, but both are local
storage-based installation sources. The device still has to be bootable and
supported by the target computer.
