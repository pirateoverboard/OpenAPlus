## Question

During an OS install, the installer cannot see the target storage controller.
What should the technician consider loading?

## Answer

The appropriate third-party storage or disk-controller driver.

## Instructor Notes

Some installers need additional drivers before they can access the storage
hardware. This is different from choosing a different installation type.
