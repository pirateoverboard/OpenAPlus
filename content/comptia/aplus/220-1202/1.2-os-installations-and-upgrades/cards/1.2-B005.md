## Question

Which upgrade method keeps existing applications, documents, and settings when
the upgrade path supports it?

## Answer

An in-place upgrade.

## Instructor Notes

An in-place upgrade starts from inside the existing OS. It is the preservation
choice, but it still depends on supported upgrade paths and compatibility.
