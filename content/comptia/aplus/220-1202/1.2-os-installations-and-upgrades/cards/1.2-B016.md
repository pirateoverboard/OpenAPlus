## Question

Why should product lifecycle be considered before choosing a Windows upgrade
target?

## Answer

Support windows and update availability affect whether the OS version will
continue receiving security fixes, bug fixes, and feature updates.

## Instructor Notes

Do not choose an upgrade target only because it installs successfully. The
target should remain supportable after deployment.
