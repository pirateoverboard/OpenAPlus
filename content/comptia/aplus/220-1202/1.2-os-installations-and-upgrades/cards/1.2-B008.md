## Question

What is the purpose of a recovery partition during OS installation or recovery?

## Answer

It is a hidden partition that stores installation or recovery files.

## Instructor Notes

A recovery partition must already exist to be useful. Do not assume it is
available on every system or that it performs every kind of upgrade.
