## Question

What compatibility checks should be performed before relying on an OS upgrade?

## Answer

Check hardware requirements and verify application and driver compatibility.

## Instructor Notes

Compatibility checks reduce the risk of a failed upgrade or a working OS with
broken devices and applications.
