## Question

A phone is misplaced nearby, and the owner wants to view its location or make
it produce a sound. Which mobile security feature should be used?

## Answer

A locator application.

## Instructor Notes

Location and alert functions help recover the hardware. They do not erase its
data or create a recoverable copy of that data.
