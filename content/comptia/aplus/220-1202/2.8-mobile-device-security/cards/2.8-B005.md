## Question

An organization needs one central system to manage mobile-device profiles,
security controls, and application use. Which technology fits this need?

## Answer

Mobile device management (MDM).

## Instructor Notes

MDM is the centralized management layer. A configuration profile is one bundle
of settings that the management system can deploy.
