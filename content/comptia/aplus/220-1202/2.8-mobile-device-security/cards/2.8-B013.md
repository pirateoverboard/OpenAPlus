## Question

An attacker repeatedly guesses a phone's unlock code. Which mobile policy
control directly limits continued guessing?

## Answer

A failed log-in attempt restriction.

## Instructor Notes

The configured response may lock the device, require additional account
authentication, or wipe data. The exact threshold and response should follow
policy rather than an assumed universal value.
