## Question

A company is concerned that someone could read locally stored data from a
stolen smartphone. Which mobile-device hardening technique directly addresses
this risk?

## Answer

Full-device encryption.

## Instructor Notes

A screen lock controls normal access to the device interface. Encryption adds
protection for the stored data when an unauthorized person has physical
possession of the device.
