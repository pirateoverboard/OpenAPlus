## Question

A user leaves a phone unattended on a desk. Which hardening control should
require authentication before another person can use the device interface?

## Answer

A screen lock.

## Instructor Notes

The lock can use an approved method such as a PIN, biometric, or pattern.
Device encryption serves a related but different purpose by protecting stored
data.
