## Question

A stolen corporate phone is unlikely to be recovered, and its local data must
be removed from a distance. Which action directly meets this requirement?

## Answer

Initiate a remote wipe.

## Instructor Notes

A locator may help find a device, while a remote backup preserves a copy of its
data. Remote wipe is the control that deletes data from the device.
