## Question

Which two screen-lock methods listed in Objective 2.8 authenticate by a user's
biometric traits?

## Answer

Facial recognition and fingerprint recognition.

## Instructor Notes

A PIN or pattern depends on information the user provides. Facial and
fingerprint recognition instead measure a physical characteristic.
