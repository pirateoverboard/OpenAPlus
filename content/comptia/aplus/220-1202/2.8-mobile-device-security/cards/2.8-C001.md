## Text

MDM expands to {{c1::mobile device management}}.

## Extra

MDM provides centralized administration for mobile devices and their security
settings.

## Instructor Notes

Do not confuse the management platform with a configuration profile. The
profile is a settings bundle that MDM can distribute.
