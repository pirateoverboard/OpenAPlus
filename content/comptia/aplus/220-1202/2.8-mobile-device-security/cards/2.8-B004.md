## Question

An administrator must apply one predefined set of email and device settings to
every phone in a department. What should the administrator assign through the
management platform?

## Answer

A configuration profile.

## Instructor Notes

A profile packages a consistent group of settings for distribution to many
devices. This makes the setup easier to manage consistently than configuring
each phone individually.
