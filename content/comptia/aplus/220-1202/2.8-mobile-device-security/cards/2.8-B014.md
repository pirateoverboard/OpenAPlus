## Question

Before applying mobile management controls, which ownership distinction should
an organization's policy define?

## Answer

Whether the device is bring your own device (BYOD) or corporate-owned.

## Instructor Notes

The policy establishes which devices are managed and what management scope
applies. MDM then provides the centralized mechanism for enforcing supported
controls.
