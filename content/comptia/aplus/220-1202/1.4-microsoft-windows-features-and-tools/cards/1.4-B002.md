## Question

In Task Manager, which area lets a technician quickly start, stop, or restart
background services?

## Answer

The Services tab.

## Instructor Notes

This is a quick service-control view inside Task Manager. Dedicated service
management may offer more detail, but the objective specifically includes
services under Task Manager.
