## Question

Which Windows tool is used to defragment a traditional hard drive, and why is
it not a routine SSD maintenance task?

## Answer

Disk Defragment. SSDs do not need traditional file-fragment defragmentation.

## Instructor Notes

The objective lists Disk Defragment as a Windows tool. Messer notes the SSD
distinction, which helps prevent applying hard-drive maintenance habits to SSDs.
