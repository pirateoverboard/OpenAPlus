## Question

Which Windows tool should you use to configure local policy settings on a
standalone Windows system?

## Answer

Group Policy Editor.

## Instructor Notes

`gpedit.msc` manages local policy on a single device. In Active Directory
environments, Group Policy can also be managed centrally with domain tools.
