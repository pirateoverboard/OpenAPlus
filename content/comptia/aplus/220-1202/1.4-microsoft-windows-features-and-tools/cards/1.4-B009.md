## Question

A Windows device has a driver problem after a hardware change. Which tool is
the best place to inspect the device status and manage the driver?

## Answer

Device Manager.

## Instructor Notes

Device Manager is the Windows hardware and driver management tool. It is the
right match when the support task points to hardware recognition or
hardware-specific driver compatibility.
