## Question

Company laptops are left unattended in an accessible office area. Which
end-user hardening practice directly reduces the risk that a laptop is taken?

## Answer

Physically secure the laptop, such as with a suitable lock or secure storage.

## Instructor Notes

Encryption protects data if a device is stolen, but it does not prevent the
hardware loss. Physical protection addresses the theft risk itself.
