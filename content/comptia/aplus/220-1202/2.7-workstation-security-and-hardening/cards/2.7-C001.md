## Text

PII expands to {{c1::personally identifiable information}}.

## Extra

Workstation hardening includes protecting PII from unauthorized disclosure or
observation.

## Instructor Notes

PII describes information that identifies or can be linked to a person; it is
not the name of a particular security control.
