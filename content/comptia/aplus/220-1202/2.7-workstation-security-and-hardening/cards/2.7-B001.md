## Question

A company is concerned that someone could read a laptop's storage after
stealing the device. Which workstation security option directly addresses
this risk?

## Answer

Data-at-rest encryption, such as full-disk encryption.

## Instructor Notes

Authentication controls normal sign-in, but drive encryption protects stored
data even when an attacker has physical possession of the storage device.
