## Question

A newly deployed workstation still uses its default administrator account name
and password. What hardening change is required?

## Answer

Change both the default administrator account name and its password.

## Instructor Notes

Defaults can be widely known and give an attacker a predictable username and
credential to target. Changing only the password still leaves a known account
name.
