## Question

A partner account should be usable only during an approved nightly support
window. Which account control best enforces that requirement?

## Answer

Restrict the account's permitted log-in times.

## Instructor Notes

Log-in time restrictions limit when authentication is allowed. Account
expiration is different: it ends access on a date rather than defining a
recurring time window.
