## Question

A fixed workstation displays PII where people passing in a hallway can see the
screen, and the display cannot be relocated. Which protection directly
addresses this exposure?

## Answer

Use a privacy filter.

## Instructor Notes

This scenario involves visual disclosure from an authorized session. A screen
lock protects the display after inactivity, but positioning or a privacy filter
helps while the user is actively working.
