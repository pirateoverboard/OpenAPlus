## Question

Employees sometimes step away from signed-in workstations without securing
them. Which hardening option protects these unattended sessions automatically?

## Answer

A password-protected screen lock that activates after an inactivity timeout.

## Instructor Notes

The timeout limits the window in which an unattended authenticated session is
exposed. Users should also lock the screen manually when stepping away.
