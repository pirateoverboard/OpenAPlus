## Question

Why can requiring varied character types improve password complexity?

## Answer

It broadens the possible password combinations and discourages simple,
single-pattern choices.

## Instructor Notes

Character types are one part of complexity. Length and unpredictability still
matter; a familiar word with a predictable symbol added is not strong merely
because it satisfies a category rule.
