## Question

An employee needs one business application but has administrator rights for
all workstation tasks. Which account-management change best limits the impact
of misuse or malicious software?

## Answer

Restrict the account to only the permissions required for the employee's work.

## Instructor Notes

This is least privilege. Unnecessary administrative rights expand what can be
changed or accessed if the account is misused or compromised.
