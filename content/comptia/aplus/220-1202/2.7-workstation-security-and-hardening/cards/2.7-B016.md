## Question

Logs show repeated failed password guesses against the same workstation
account. Which account control directly slows this online guessing attempt?

## Answer

A failed-attempt lockout policy.

## Instructor Notes

Lockout stops further authentication attempts after a configured threshold.
The threshold and recovery method should follow organizational policy rather
than a universal value.
