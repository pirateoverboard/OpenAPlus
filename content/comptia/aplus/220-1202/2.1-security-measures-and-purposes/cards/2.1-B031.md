## Question

Which broader security system vaults powerful credentials, controls requests
for their use, and records privileged activity?

## Answer

Privileged access management (PAM).

## Instructor Notes

PAM governs administrator and root access as a program. Just-in-time elevation
is one PAM technique, not a synonym for the complete management system.
