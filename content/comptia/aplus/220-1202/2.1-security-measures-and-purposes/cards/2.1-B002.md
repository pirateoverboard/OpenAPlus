## Question

Which physical control uses two interlocked doors so one door remains secured
while passage through the other is controlled?

## Answer

An access control vestibule.

## Instructor Notes

The two-door design limits the flow of people through a checkpoint. A fence or
bollard directs movement but does not provide this controlled intermediate space.
