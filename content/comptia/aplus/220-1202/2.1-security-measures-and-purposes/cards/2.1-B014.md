## Question

Why does compromise of biometric authentication data create a different
long-term risk than compromise of a password?

## Answer

A password can be replaced, but a person's biometric trait is difficult or
impossible to change.

## Instructor Notes

Biometric systems generally compare a stored mathematical representation of a
trait. Protecting that representation matters because the underlying fingerprint,
face, retina, palm, or voice belongs permanently to the user.
