## Question

A facility must stop vehicles from approaching an entrance while still
directing people toward a pedestrian checkpoint. Which physical control fits?

## Answer

Bollards.

## Instructor Notes

Bollards are vehicle-resistant barriers. They manage physical approach to an
area; they do not authenticate the people who pass between them.
