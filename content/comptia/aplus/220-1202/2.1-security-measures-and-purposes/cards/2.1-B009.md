## Question

What is the primary security purpose of a fence around a facility?

## Answer

To establish and reinforce a physical perimeter.

## Instructor Notes

Fence height, visibility, cut resistance, and anti-climb features affect its
strength, but the stable concept is the perimeter boundary.
