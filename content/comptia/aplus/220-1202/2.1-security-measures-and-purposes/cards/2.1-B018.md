## Question

How can improved lighting strengthen physical security around an entrance?

## Answer

It increases visibility for people and cameras and reduces concealment.

## Instructor Notes

Lighting is both a deterrence and visibility control. Placement matters because
glare or deep shadows can reduce rather than improve useful visibility.
