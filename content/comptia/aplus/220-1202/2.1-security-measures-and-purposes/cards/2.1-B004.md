## Question

Which physical security measure provides recorded visual evidence for live
monitoring or later review?

## Answer

Video surveillance.

## Instructor Notes

Surveillance is primarily a detection and evidence control. It does not by
itself prevent entry, so facilities often pair it with barriers or access controls.
