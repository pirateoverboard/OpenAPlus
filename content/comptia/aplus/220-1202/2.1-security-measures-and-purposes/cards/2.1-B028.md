## Question

Which open standard allows one service to rely on authentication and
authorization assertions supplied by a separate identity provider?

## Answer

Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML).

## Instructor Notes

SAML carries assertions between trust participants. SSO describes the user's
one-login experience; SAML is one standard that can help implement federated access.
