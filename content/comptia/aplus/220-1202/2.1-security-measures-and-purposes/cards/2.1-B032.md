## Question

An organization must centrally require screen locks and control applications
and company data on enrolled phones. Which control fits?

## Answer

Mobile device management (MDM).

## Instructor Notes

MDM applies device policy from a central service. IAM governs identities and
access more broadly; MDM focuses on the managed mobile endpoint.
