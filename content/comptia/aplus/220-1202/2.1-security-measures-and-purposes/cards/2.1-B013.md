## Question

Why should shared mechanical keys be stored in a controlled key cabinet with a
check-in/check-out record?

## Answer

To control who can obtain each key and create an accountability trail for its use.

## Instructor Notes

A mechanical key may be necessary for a standalone lock, but a loose key has no
built-in identity or event log. The management process supplies that missing control.
