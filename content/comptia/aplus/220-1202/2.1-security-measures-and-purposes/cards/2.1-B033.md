## Question

Which logical control identifies sensitive information and blocks or controls
its unauthorized disclosure through endpoints, email, or cloud services?

## Answer

Data loss prevention (DLP).

## Instructor Notes

DLP protects the movement and disclosure of data. It is not a backup system and
does not replace identity or device-access controls.
