## Question

A checkpoint must screen people and bags for metal objects. Which control is
appropriate, and what important limitation does it have?

## Answer

A magnetometer; it does not detect nonmetallic objects.

## Instructor Notes

A magnetometer is a screening detector, not an authentication factor. It says
nothing about whether the person carrying an object is authorized.
