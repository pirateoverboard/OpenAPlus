## Question

What security boundary is a door lock primarily intended to enforce?

## Answer

Entry into a room, building, or other enclosed area.

## Instructor Notes

Door locks may be mechanical, electronic, token-based, biometric, or
multifactor. Their shared purpose is controlling passage through the doorway.
