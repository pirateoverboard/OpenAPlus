## Question

Which small carried token commonly unlocks a door through proximity or
contactless operation instead of a mechanical key?

## Answer

A key fob.

## Instructor Notes

A key fob is something the user possesses. Its convenience does not prove that
the holder is the authorized person unless another factor is also required.
