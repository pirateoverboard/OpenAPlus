## Question

What is the purpose of a badge reader at a controlled entrance?

## Answer

It reads an identification credential so the access system can allow or deny
entry.

## Instructor Notes

The credential may use RFID, NFC, or a magnetic stripe. Reading a badge is an
automated access check; a printed badge viewed by a guard is a manual check.
