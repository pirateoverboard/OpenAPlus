## Question

Which security model rejects the assumption that a user, device, or process is
trustworthy merely because it is already inside the network?

## Answer

The Zero Trust model.

## Instructor Notes

Zero Trust applies verification and policy across users, devices, and
processes. Least privilege narrows granted rights; Zero Trust is the broader
approach that avoids implicit trust.
