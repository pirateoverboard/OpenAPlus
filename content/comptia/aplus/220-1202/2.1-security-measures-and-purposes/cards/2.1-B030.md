## Question

An administrator receives elevated credentials only for one approved session,
and they are removed afterward. Which control is this?

## Answer

Just-in-time access.

## Instructor Notes

Temporary elevation reduces the time during which a stolen everyday account
could hold administrator rights. It applies least privilege to access duration.
