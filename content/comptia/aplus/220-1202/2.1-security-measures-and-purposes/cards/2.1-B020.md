## Question

A user's account can perform every assigned job task but cannot install
unapproved software or administer other users. Which principle is being applied?

## Answer

The principle of least privilege.

## Instructor Notes

Least privilege minimizes permissions to the job requirement. It is an
authorization principle, not a method of proving the user's identity.
