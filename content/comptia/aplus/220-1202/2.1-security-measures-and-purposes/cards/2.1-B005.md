## Question

How does a motion sensor differ from an alarm system in a physical-security
design?

## Answer

A motion sensor detects movement; an alarm system signals that a configured
security event has occurred.

## Instructor Notes

A motion sensor can feed an alarm system, but the detector and the notification
system are different roles. Alarms may also react to doors, windows, or duress inputs.
