## Question

Which security discipline manages digital identities, authentication,
authorization, and access changes throughout an identity's lifecycle?

## Answer

Identity and access management (IAM).

## Instructor Notes

IAM aims to give the right identity the right access at the right time and to
remove access when it is no longer justified. A directory can support IAM but
is not the entire discipline.
