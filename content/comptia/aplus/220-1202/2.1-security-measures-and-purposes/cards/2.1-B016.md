## Question

What physical characteristic does facial recognition technology use as an
authentication key?

## Answer

A digital representation of the user's facial features.

## Instructor Notes

Facial recognition is a biometric factor. Camera and depth-sensing techniques
vary, so the durable exam concept is the trait being evaluated.
