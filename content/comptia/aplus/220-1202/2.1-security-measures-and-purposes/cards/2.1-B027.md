## Question

What security dependency do SMS-delivered codes and voice-call codes share?

## Answer

Both depend on control of the registered phone number and the security of the
telephone channel.

## Instructor Notes

Messages or calls may be redirected, intercepted, or received after a number is
reassigned. The two delivery methods are different, but they share that channel risk.
