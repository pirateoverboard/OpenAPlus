## Question

How does voice recognition technology authenticate a user?

## Answer

It compares the user's spoken voice with a stored voice representation.

## Instructor Notes

The system evaluates who is speaking, not merely whether the words are correct.
Environmental noise or inability to speak can make this factor impractical alone.
