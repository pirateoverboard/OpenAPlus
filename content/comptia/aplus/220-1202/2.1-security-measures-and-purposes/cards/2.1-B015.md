## Question

Which biometric scanner identifies a person from the distinctive capillary
pattern at the back of the eye?

## Answer

A retina scanner.

## Instructor Notes

Retina scanning uses an internal eye pattern. It should not be confused with
facial recognition, which builds a representation from facial features.
