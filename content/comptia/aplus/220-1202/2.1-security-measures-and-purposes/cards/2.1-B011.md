## Question

An organization wants a carried card that stores a certificate-based
credential and works with a reader. Which access technology fits?

## Answer

A smart card.

## Instructor Notes

The smart card supplies a possession factor. Requiring a PIN or biometric with
it adds a different factor rather than merely another possession item.
