## Question

Which logical control uses permit or deny rules to decide whether matching
network traffic or resource access is allowed?

## Answer

An access control list (ACL).

## Instructor Notes

Network ACL criteria can include source, destination, protocol, and port. The
same allow/deny concept also appears in operating-system resource permissions.
