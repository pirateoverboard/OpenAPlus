## Question

A shared data-center room is already access controlled, but one team must also
restrict physical access to its own servers. Which control addresses that need?

## Answer

Equipment locks, such as a locked rack or cabinet.

## Instructor Notes

The room lock protects the area. An equipment lock adds a narrower boundary
around a particular asset or group of assets.
