## Question

Which physical control can verify an employee's identity, enforce a visitor
access list, and respond to unusual activity at an entrance?

## Answer

A security guard.

## Instructor Notes

A guard can interpret circumstances and manage exceptions that an automated
reader cannot. Consistent procedures and visitor records make that judgment auditable.
