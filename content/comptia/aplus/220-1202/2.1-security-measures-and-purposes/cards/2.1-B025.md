## Question

An organization wants users to generate changing login codes on their phones
without receiving a text message for every attempt. Which method fits?

## Answer

An authenticator application.

## Instructor Notes

The application acts as a software token. It avoids dependence on per-login SMS
delivery, although the phone and the application's secret still require protection.
