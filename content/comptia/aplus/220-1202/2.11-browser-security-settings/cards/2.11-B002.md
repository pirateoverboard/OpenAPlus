## Question

A browser publisher lists a hash for its installer. How should a technician use
that value after downloading the file?

## Answer

Calculate the downloaded file's hash and compare it with the publisher's known
hash. The values should match.

## Instructor Notes

The comparison checks whether the downloaded bytes match the version
represented by the publisher. It complements—but does not replace—the decision
to obtain software from a trusted source.
