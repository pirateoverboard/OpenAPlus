## Question

An approved browser extension remains installed, but its expected feature is
no longer available. Which browser setting should be checked?

## Answer

Check browser feature management to confirm that the extension is enabled.

## Instructor Notes

Installed and enabled are separate states. This decision differs from trusting
a new extension source: here the component is already approved, and the task is
to verify its current state.
