## Question

A technician needs a new browser installer. Which download approach best
reduces the risk of receiving a substituted malicious installer?

## Answer

Go directly to the browser publisher's official site or another
organization-approved trusted source.

## Instructor Notes

An unfamiliar third-party site or an untrusted message link can deliver a
different file than the legitimate publisher intended. Source trust is the
first decision; a later integrity check does not make an unknown publisher
trustworthy.
