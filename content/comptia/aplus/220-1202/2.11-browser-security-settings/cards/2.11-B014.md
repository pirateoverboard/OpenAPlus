## Question

A user wants the browser to reduce displayed advertising. Which browser
control matches that requirement?

## Answer

Enable an ad blocker at an appropriate security level.

## Instructor Notes

Ad blocking is imperfect because a browser may not recognize every
advertisement, and the control is not always available. When supported, its
security level can be adjusted for the required amount of blocking.
