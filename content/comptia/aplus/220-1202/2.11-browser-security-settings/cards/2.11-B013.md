## Question

A user wants the same browser favorites, history, extensions, and settings on
their computer, tablet, and phone. Which browser feature should be configured?

## Answer

Sign in to the browser and enable browser-data synchronization for the intended
categories.

## Instructor Notes

Synchronization deliberately shares selected browser data across the signed-in
user's devices. Do not enable it on a device or account where that sharing is
not intended.
