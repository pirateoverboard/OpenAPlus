## Question

A site loads an outdated or incomplete local copy of its content while the
current site works on another device. Which browser data should be cleared?

## Answer

Clear the browser cache.

## Instructor Notes

The cache stores local copies of website resources. Removing those copies lets
the browser retrieve fresh content without unnecessarily erasing every other
browsing-data category.
