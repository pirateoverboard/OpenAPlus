## Question

A workstation's clock was reset to the wrong date. Secure sites now report that
their certificates are outside their validity periods. Which local setting
should be corrected?

## Answer

Check and correct the computer's date and time.

## Instructor Notes

Certificate validation compares a certificate's validity period with the local
clock. A clock that is far ahead or behind can make valid certificates appear
expired or not yet valid.
