## Question

A browser contains an unfamiliar extension that was not approved or
intentionally installed. What should the technician do in browser feature
management?

## Answer

Disable the extension.

## Instructor Notes

Unexpected add-ons can be installed by unwanted software and may gain access
to browser activity. Disabling an untrusted component reduces the active
browser attack surface.
