## Question

A user needs an approved browser extension. From where should the extension be
installed?

## Answer

Use the browser's official extension catalog or another organization-approved,
known-good source.

## Instructor Notes

Extensions execute within a browser that handles sensitive sessions and data.
An official catalog improves source accountability, although installed add-ons
should still be limited to those that are needed and approved.
