## Question

A trusted business site requires a pop-up for one legitimate workflow. How
should the browser be configured without allowing pop-ups from every site?

## Answer

Keep the pop-up blocker enabled and allow a narrow exception for the trusted
site.

## Instructor Notes

Browser pop-up controls can block or allow by site. A scoped exception meets
the stated need while preserving the broader protection.
