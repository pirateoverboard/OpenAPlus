## Question

A browser reports a certificate warning for a secure site. Which three
certificate details should be checked before trusting the connection?

## Answer

Check that the certificate:

1. matches the requested domain,
2. is within its valid date range, and
3. is signed through a trusted certificate authority.

## Instructor Notes

Encryption alone does not establish the site's identity. A mismatch in any of
these checks explains why the browser cannot validate the certificate as
expected.
