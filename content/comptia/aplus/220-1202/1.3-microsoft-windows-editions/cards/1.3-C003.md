## Text

A Windows {{c1::domain}} provides centralized authentication and device access,
while a workgroup uses standalone peer systems.

## Extra

Domains are the business-network model; workgroups are the peer-style model.

## Instructor Notes

This cloze supports fast recall of the domain vs. workgroup contrast.
