## Question

Before deploying Windows 11, which two hardware capabilities from Objective
1.3 should a technician verify?

## Answer

TPM support and UEFI support.

## Instructor Notes

Messer specifically notes TPM 2.0 compatibility and hardware capable of Secure
Boot through UEFI. The official objective calls out TPM and UEFI as the
hardware-requirement comparison points.
