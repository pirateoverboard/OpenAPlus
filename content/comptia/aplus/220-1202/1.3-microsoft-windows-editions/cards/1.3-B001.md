## Question

A small business needs Windows systems that can join Active Directory, use
BitLocker, and host Remote Desktop sessions. Which Windows edition tier is the
better fit: Home or Pro?

## Answer

Pro.

## Instructor Notes

Home editions are consumer-focused. Pro adds the business management and
security features named in the scenario.
