## Question

Which Windows 10 edition is aimed at high-end desktops that need enhanced
performance, high RAM support, more physical CPUs, and ReFS support?

## Answer

Windows 10 Pro for Workstations.

## Instructor Notes

Do not confuse this with Enterprise. Pro for Workstations is the
workstation-class Windows 10 edition, while Enterprise is aimed at large
organization deployment and management.
