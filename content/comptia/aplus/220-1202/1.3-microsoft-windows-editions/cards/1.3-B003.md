## Question

Which Windows edition tier is designed for large company deployments with
volume licensing and advanced centralized management?

## Answer

Enterprise.

## Instructor Notes

Enterprise is the large-organization tier. Messer's examples include volume
licensing and advanced management capabilities rather than home-user
customization.
