## Question

How does a managed work desktop usually differ from a home Windows desktop?

## Answer

A managed work desktop is usually standardized with limited customization,
while a home desktop allows broader personal customization.

## Instructor Notes

The objective's "desktop styles/user interface" item is about recognizing the
support and management difference, not memorizing a theme or wallpaper setting.
