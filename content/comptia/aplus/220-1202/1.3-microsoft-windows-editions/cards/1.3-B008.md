## Question

A technician needs a Windows 11 computer to accept inbound Remote Desktop
connections. Which edition limitation matters?

## Answer

The Remote Desktop service is available in Pro and Enterprise editions, not
Home.

## Instructor Notes

RDP clients are broadly available, but hosting an inbound Remote Desktop
session is the edition-dependent feature.
