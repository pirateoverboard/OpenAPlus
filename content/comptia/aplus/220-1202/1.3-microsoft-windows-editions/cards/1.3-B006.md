## Question

A user has Windows Home, but the business needs the computer joined to an
Active Directory domain. What edition issue should the technician recognize?

## Answer

Windows Home is not the right edition for domain joining; the system needs Pro
or a higher business edition.

## Instructor Notes

This is a common edition trap. Domain support is a business feature associated
with Pro and Enterprise, not Home.
