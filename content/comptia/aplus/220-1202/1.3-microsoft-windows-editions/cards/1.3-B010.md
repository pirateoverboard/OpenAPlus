## Question

What is `gpedit.msc` used for on a Windows computer?

## Answer

It opens the Local Group Policy Editor to manage local system and user policy
settings.

## Instructor Notes

Local Group Policy applies to one device. In Active Directory environments,
Group Policy can also be centrally managed through domain tooling.
