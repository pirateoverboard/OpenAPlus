## Prompt

Type the base Linux command that checks a filesystem for logical errors.

## Typed Answer

fsck

## Back

`fsck` means “file system check.”

## Instructor Notes

The approved notes specify an unmounted or read-only volume for checking and
repair; the base-command association is the recall target here.
