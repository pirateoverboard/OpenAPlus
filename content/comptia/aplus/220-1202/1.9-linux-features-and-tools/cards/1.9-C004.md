## Text

Linux filesystem mount definitions are listed in
{{c1::/etc/fstab}}.

## Extra

The mount process reads this configuration to make defined filesystems
available through the directory tree.

## Instructor Notes

Think “filesystem table”: the filename `fstab` points to stored filesystem
mount definitions.
