## Text

Linux DNS resolver configuration is stored in
{{c1::/etc/resolv.conf}}.

## Extra

Incorrect resolver settings can disrupt services that depend on DNS.

## Instructor Notes

`/etc/hosts` supplies local name mappings; `/etc/resolv.conf` controls resolver
details.
