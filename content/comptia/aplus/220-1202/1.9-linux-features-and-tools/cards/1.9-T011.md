## Prompt

Type the base Linux command that associates a storage device or filesystem with
a directory.

## Typed Answer

mount

## Back

`mount` makes the filesystem accessible at a mount point.

## Instructor Notes

`/etc/fstab` contains stored mount definitions; `mount` performs or displays
the active association.
