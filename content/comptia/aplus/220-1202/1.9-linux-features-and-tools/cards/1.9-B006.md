## Question

A filesystem is nearly full. Which command identifies the space consumed by a
specific directory: `df` or `du`?

## Answer

`du`.

## Instructor Notes

`du` reports usage for a file or directory. `df` reports free space for
filesystems, so it establishes capacity but does not attribute usage to one
directory.
