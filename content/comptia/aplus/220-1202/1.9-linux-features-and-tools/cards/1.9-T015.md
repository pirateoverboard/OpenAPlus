## Prompt

Type the Linux package manager named by Objective 1.9 that manages RPM packages
and replaced `yum`.

## Typed Answer

dnf

## Back

`dnf` installs, updates, and removes RPM packages.

## Instructor Notes

Choose the package manager supplied by the distribution; `apt` and `dnf` are
not assumed to coexist on every Linux system.
