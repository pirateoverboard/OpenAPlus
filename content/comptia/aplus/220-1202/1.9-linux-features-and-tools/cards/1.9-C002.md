## Text

Linux password hashes and password-aging information are stored in
{{c1::/etc/shadow}}.

## Extra

The file is distinct from the broader user-account records in `/etc/passwd`.

## Instructor Notes

Do not infer from the name `/etc/passwd` that it stores the password hashes;
the approved notes associate those hashes with `/etc/shadow`.
