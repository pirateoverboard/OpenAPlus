## Text

Local Linux hostname-to-address mappings are stored in
{{c1::/etc/hosts}}.

## Extra

A local mapping can supply a name result separately from DNS.

## Instructor Notes

Do not confuse local mappings with DNS resolver settings in
`/etc/resolv.conf`.
