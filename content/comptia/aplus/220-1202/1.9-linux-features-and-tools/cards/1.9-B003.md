## Question

A technician needs elevated rights for one command without remaining in a root
shell. Should the technician use `sudo` or `su`?

## Answer

Use `sudo`.

## Instructor Notes

`sudo` applies the requested authority to the specified command. `su` changes
the shell's user identity until that session is exited.
