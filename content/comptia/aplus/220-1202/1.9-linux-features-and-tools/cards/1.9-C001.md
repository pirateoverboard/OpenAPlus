## Text

Linux registered-user account records are stored in
{{c1::/etc/passwd}}.

## Extra

The records include account attributes such as user and group IDs, home
directories, and shells.

## Instructor Notes

Keep general account records separate from the password hashes and aging data
stored in `/etc/shadow`.
