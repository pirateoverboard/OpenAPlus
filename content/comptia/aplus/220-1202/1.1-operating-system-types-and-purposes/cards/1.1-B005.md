## Question

Which workstation OS is centered on the Chrome browser and is best matched to a
cloud-heavy, web-application workflow?

## Answer

Chrome OS.

## Instructor Notes

Chrome OS is based on the Linux kernel, but the exam distinction is its
browser-centered and cloud-dependent workflow.
