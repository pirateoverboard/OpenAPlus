## Question

Why is NTFS a poor first choice for a removable drive that must be written by
Windows, macOS, and Linux systems?

## Answer

NTFS is a Windows-focused filesystem, and non-Windows systems may have limited
or read-only NTFS support.

## Instructor Notes

NTFS is common and capable on Windows, but cross-platform write compatibility is
the trap. A scenario about easy file movement across operating systems points
away from NTFS.
