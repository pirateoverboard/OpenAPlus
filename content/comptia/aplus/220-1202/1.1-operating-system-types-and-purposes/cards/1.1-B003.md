## Question

Which workstation OS family is Unix-like, has many distributions, and is often
chosen for low cost and hardware flexibility?

## Answer

Linux.

## Instructor Notes

Linux is not one single desktop product. Support, drivers, and administration
experience can vary by distribution and hardware platform.
