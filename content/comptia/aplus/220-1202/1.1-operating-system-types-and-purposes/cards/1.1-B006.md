## Question

How do iOS, iPadOS, and Android differ by supported device platform?

## Answer

iOS runs on Apple iPhones, iPadOS runs on Apple iPad tablets, and Android runs
on many manufacturers' mobile devices.

## Instructor Notes

The point is platform recognition, not app development. iOS and iPadOS are
Apple-specific, while Android is the non-Apple mobile OS family with broad
manufacturer support.
