## Text

exFAT stands for {{c1::Extensible File Allocation Table}}.

## Extra

exFAT is commonly used for removable media that must work across multiple
operating systems.

## Instructor Notes

The acronym matters less than the use case: exFAT is the cross-platform flash
drive answer when large files are involved.
