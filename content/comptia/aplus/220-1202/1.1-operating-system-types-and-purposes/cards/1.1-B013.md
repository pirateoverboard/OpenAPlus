## Question

Which filesystem is the Apple platform choice for modern macOS, iOS, and iPadOS
storage?

## Answer

APFS.

## Instructor Notes

APFS is the Apple filesystem association. It is optimized for modern Apple
storage features, including solid-state storage and data integrity capabilities.
