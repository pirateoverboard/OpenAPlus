## Question

Which workstation OS is tied to Apple hardware and is commonly selected for an
Apple-managed desktop environment?

## Answer

macOS.

## Instructor Notes

The key distinction is platform coupling. macOS can be a strong fit inside an
Apple environment, but it is not a general-purpose OS for arbitrary PC hardware.
