## Question

A user needs a USB flash drive for files larger than 4 GB and wants to move the
drive between Windows, Linux, and macOS. Which filesystem is the best fit?

## Answer

exFAT.

## Instructor Notes

FAT32 is broadly recognized but has a 4 GB maximum file size. exFAT is the
better removable-media fit when both large files and cross-OS compatibility
matter.
