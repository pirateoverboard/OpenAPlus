## Text

APFS stands for {{c1::Apple File System}}.

## Extra

APFS is used with modern Apple operating systems, including macOS, iOS, and
iPadOS.

## Instructor Notes

APFS is the Apple filesystem association. Do not choose it for a generic
Windows/Linux/macOS flash-drive sharing scenario.
