## Question

A business needs broad desktop software support and common industry hardware
compatibility. Which workstation OS family is the most likely fit?

## Answer

Windows.

## Instructor Notes

Windows has a large software and hardware ecosystem. The tradeoff is that its
large install base and wide hardware coverage can also increase security and
integration complexity.
