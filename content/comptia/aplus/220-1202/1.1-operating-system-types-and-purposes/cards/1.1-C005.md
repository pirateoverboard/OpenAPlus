## Text

ReFS stands for {{c1::Resilient File System}}.

## Extra

ReFS is associated with Windows storage resilience and large storage needs.

## Instructor Notes

Remember the "resilient" clue: ReFS is about integrity and large Windows
storage, not broad removable-media compatibility.
