## Text

FAT32 has a maximum individual file size of about {{c1::4 GB}}.

## Extra

This limitation is why FAT32 is a poor fit for large-file removable storage.

## Instructor Notes

This is a common trap when a scenario mentions broad compatibility but also
requires files larger than 4 GB.
