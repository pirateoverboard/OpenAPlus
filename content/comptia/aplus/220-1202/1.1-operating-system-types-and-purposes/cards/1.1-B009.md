## Question

Which Windows filesystem is most associated with large storage requirements and
ongoing data integrity checks?

## Answer

ReFS.

## Instructor Notes

ReFS is not the general cross-platform answer. It is the Windows-side choice
when the requirement points to large storage and resilience rather than broad OS
compatibility.
