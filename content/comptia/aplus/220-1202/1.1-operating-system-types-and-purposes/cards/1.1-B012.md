## Question

Which Linux filesystem is designed for high performance and scalability on
large filesystems?

## Answer

XFS.

## Instructor Notes

XFS is a Linux-oriented answer for large, high-performance storage. Do not mix
it up with ReFS, which is the Windows large-storage resilience option in this
objective.
