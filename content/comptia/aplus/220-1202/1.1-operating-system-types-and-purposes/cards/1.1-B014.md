## Question

Why is an end-of-life operating system a support risk?

## Answer

The vendor may stop providing normal updates, leaving security and
compatibility problems that cannot be fixed through routine patching.

## Instructor Notes

End-of-life is a vendor lifecycle state, not a local setting. A technician may
need to recommend an upgrade or replacement instead of expecting future patches.
