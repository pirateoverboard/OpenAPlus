## Question

A Windows volume has logical file-system errors, but there is no evidence of
bad sectors. Which Check Disk option is the better first repair choice?

## Answer

`chkdsk /f`.

## Instructor Notes

The `/f` option targets logical file-system repair. Use `/r` when the task also
requires bad-sector checking and readable-data recovery.
