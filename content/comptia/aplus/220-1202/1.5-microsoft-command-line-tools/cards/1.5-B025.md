## Question

A route trace shows the path, but the technician also needs round-trip and
packet-loss statistics at each hop. Which route-analysis approach fits?

## Answer

A path analysis command such as `pathping`.

## Instructor Notes

`pathping` is useful when `tracert` alone is not enough because the technician
also needs loss and timing data along the route.
