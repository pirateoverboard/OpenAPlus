## Question

A technician needs a Windows client to process Group Policy updates now instead
of waiting for normal refresh timing. Which Group Policy command role fits this
task: update or results reporting?

## Answer

Update: use `gpupdate`.

## Instructor Notes

`gpupdate` applies policy updates. Use `gpresult` when the task is to report
which policies applied.
