## Question

A technician suspects a Windows drive has bad sectors, not just logical
file-system errors. Which Check Disk option fits that scenario?

## Answer

`chkdsk /r`.

## Instructor Notes

`/r` includes the `/f` behavior and adds bad-sector checking and readable-data
recovery, so it is broader than a logical repair-only check.
