## Question

A technician needs to verify which Group Policy settings apply to a computer
or user. Which Group Policy command role fits this task: update or results
reporting?

## Answer

Results reporting: use `gpresult`.

## Instructor Notes

`gpresult` reports policy results. It does not force a new policy refresh; that
is the job of `gpupdate`.
