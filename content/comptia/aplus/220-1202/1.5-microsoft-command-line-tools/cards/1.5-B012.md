## Question

A technician is troubleshooting a permissions difference between two Windows
sessions. Why is the detailed `whoami` output useful?

## Answer

It shows the current user context plus detailed group and privilege
information.

## Instructor Notes

Plain `whoami` identifies the current user. The detailed form is better when
the problem may depend on group membership or privileges.
