## Prompt

Which command form locates bad sectors and recovers readable information with
Check Disk?

## Typed Answer

chkdsk /r

## Back

`chkdsk /r` adds bad-sector checking and implies `/f`.

## Instructor Notes

Use this when the recall target is the bad-sector option, not only logical
file-system repair.
