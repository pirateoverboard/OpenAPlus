## Question

A technician needs command-line access to disk, partition, and volume
configuration. What risk should they keep in mind before using DiskPart?

## Answer

DiskPart can destroy data if the wrong disk, partition, or volume is selected.

## Instructor Notes

DiskPart is appropriate for disk configuration work, but the safety issue is
the main distinction from informational commands.
