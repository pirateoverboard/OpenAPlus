## Question

An organization wants users to authenticate centrally and access managed
resources from many Windows clients. Should the clients join a domain or a
workgroup?

## Answer

A domain.

## Instructor Notes

A domain centralizes authentication and device access across a business
network. Joining it also requires an eligible Windows edition and appropriate
rights.
