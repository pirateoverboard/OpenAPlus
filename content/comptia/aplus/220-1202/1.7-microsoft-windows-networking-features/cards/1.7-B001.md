## Question

Five office PCs need to share resources without centralized authentication.
Each PC will maintain its own user accounts. Should the technician configure a
domain or a workgroup?

## Answer

A workgroup.

## Instructor Notes

Workgroups fit small peer networks because account and device administration
remains on each computer. A domain is the better match when centralized
authentication and management are required.
