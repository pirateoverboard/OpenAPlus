## Question

A Windows client configured for automatic addressing receives a `169.254.x.x`
address and cannot reach the internet. What addressing fallback is the client
using?

## Answer

APIPA.

## Instructor Notes

Windows uses Automatic Private IP Addressing when it has no static address and
cannot obtain DHCP configuration. The link-local address may support nearby
communication but does not provide normal routed connectivity.
