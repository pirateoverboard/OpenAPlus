## Question

A user with a compatible desktop mail client needs to manage a local mail
profile and its data files. Which Control Panel applet should be used?

## Answer

Mail.

## Instructor Notes

The Mail applet appears when a supported mail client is installed and exposes
local account, profile, and data-file configuration.
