## Question

A PC has several speakers and microphones. Which Control Panel destination
lets the user select devices and adjust input or output levels?

## Answer

Sound.

## Instructor Notes

Sound handles audio input and output devices and their levels. Device Manager
would be used instead for an audio device's driver or hardware status.
