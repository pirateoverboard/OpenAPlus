## Question

A user wants a simple Control Panel view for adding and configuring a printer.
Which destination best matches the task?

## Answer

Devices and Printers.

## Instructor Notes

Devices and Printers offers a simplified view of connected devices. Device
Manager is the better match when the task concerns driver or hardware status.
