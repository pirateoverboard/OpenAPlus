## Question

A user wants to review whether Windows shares activity, language, or speech
data with online services. Which Windows Settings category should be opened?

## Answer

Privacy.

## Instructor Notes

Privacy contains controls for information and activity shared by applications
or services. Accounts instead manages identity and sign-in configuration.
