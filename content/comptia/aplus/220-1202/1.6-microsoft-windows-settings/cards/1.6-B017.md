## Question

A docked laptop should keep running when its built-in display is closed. Which
Power Options setting should be changed?

## Answer

Choose what closing the lid does, and set the applicable lid-close action to
keep the system running.

## Instructor Notes

The configurable lid-close action is useful at a docking station, where the
laptop may need to keep operating with external devices in use.
