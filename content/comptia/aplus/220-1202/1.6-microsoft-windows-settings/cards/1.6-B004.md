## Question

A technician wants the Control Panel destination for viewing network adapters
and changing adapter or sharing settings. Which destination should be opened?

## Answer

Network and Sharing Center.

## Instructor Notes

Network and Sharing Center collects adapter, addressing, and sharing
configuration. Internet Options instead handles legacy internet properties.
