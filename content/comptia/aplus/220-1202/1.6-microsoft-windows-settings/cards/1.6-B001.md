## Question

A technician needs to change legacy internet security zones and certificate
settings. Which Control Panel destination should the technician use?

## Answer

Internet Options.

## Instructor Notes

Internet Options groups legacy internet properties such as security, privacy,
content certificates, connections, programs, and advanced settings. It is not
the destination for configuring a network adapter itself.
