## Question

A technician needs to reach remote-access and system-protection settings from
Control Panel. Which applet provides those paths?

## Answer

System.

## Instructor Notes

The System applet also presents computer and Windows information. Network and
Sharing Center is for adapter and sharing configuration, not Remote Desktop or
system protection.
