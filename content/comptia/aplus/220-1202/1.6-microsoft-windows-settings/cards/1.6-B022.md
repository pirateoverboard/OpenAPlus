## Question

A user wants Windows Update to avoid restarting the PC during normal working
hours. Which Settings category contains the active-hours configuration?

## Answer

Update and Security.

## Instructor Notes

Update and Security manages operating-system updates. Active hours identify
the normal use period so update restarts are less disruptive.
