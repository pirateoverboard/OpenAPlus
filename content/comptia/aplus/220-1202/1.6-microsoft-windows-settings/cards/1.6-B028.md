## Question

A technician needs the Windows Settings category that shows connection status
and provides network and IP configuration. Which category should be opened?

## Answer

Network and Internet.

## Instructor Notes

Network and Internet is the modern Settings destination for connectivity and
address configuration. Network and Sharing Center is the related Control Panel
destination.
