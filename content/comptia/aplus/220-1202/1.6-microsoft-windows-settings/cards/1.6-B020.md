## Question

A user needs Windows usability changes for the display, keyboard, and mouse.
Which Control Panel destination groups these accessibility settings?

## Answer

Ease of Access.

## Instructor Notes

Ease of Access groups interaction and accessibility adjustments, including
options for users who need alternate display, pointer, keyboard, or screen-free
operation.
