## Question

A user wants to change between local and Microsoft account information and
configure a PIN or security key. Which Windows Settings category should be
opened?

## Answer

Accounts.

## Instructor Notes

Accounts manages sign-in identity, email integration, and sign-in methods.
Personalization changes appearance rather than authentication choices.
