## Question

A technician is investigating startup behavior and wants to test whether a
configurable power feature is contributing. Which Power Options feature can be
disabled during this troubleshooting?

## Answer

Fast Startup.

## Instructor Notes

Fast Startup uses hibernation and can be enabled or disabled while
troubleshooting startup behavior.
