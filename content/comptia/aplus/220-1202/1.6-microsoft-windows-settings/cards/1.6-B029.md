## Question

A user needs to configure Xbox Game Bar and related Windows gaming features.
Which Windows Settings category should be used?

## Answer

Gaming.

## Instructor Notes

Gaming groups Xbox Game Bar and related gaming-network features. It is not the
destination for graphics display scaling or general notifications.
