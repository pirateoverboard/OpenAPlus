## Question

A technician needs to change a local account's type, password, or picture in
Control Panel. Which applet should be opened?

## Answer

User Accounts.

## Instructor Notes

User Accounts manages local account details. Domain accounts are maintained in
the domain rather than by this local Control Panel applet.
