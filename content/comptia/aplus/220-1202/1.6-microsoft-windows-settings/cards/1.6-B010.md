## Question

A newly connected device is not working, and the technician needs immediate
hardware status and driver-management options. Which tool should be opened?

## Answer

Device Manager.

## Instructor Notes

Device Manager is the direct destination for hardware and driver status and
for actions such as enabling, disabling, adding, or removing a device.
