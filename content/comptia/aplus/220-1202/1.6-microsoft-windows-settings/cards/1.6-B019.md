## Question

A laptop should reduce power use by suspending individual idle USB devices.
Which advanced power feature provides this behavior?

## Answer

USB selective suspend.

## Instructor Notes

Selective suspend targets individual USB devices instead of putting the whole
computer into sleep or hibernation.
