## Question

An administrator wants to switch a laptop between predefined groups of energy
and performance behavior. Which Power Options feature should be configured?

## Answer

Power plans.

## Instructor Notes

A power plan groups power-usage settings so the system can be tuned for
different energy or performance goals.
