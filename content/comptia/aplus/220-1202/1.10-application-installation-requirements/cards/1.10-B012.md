## Question

After an application upgrade, one workstation becomes slow and some local files
are missing. Which installation-impact category do these effects represent?

## Answer

Device impact.

## Instructor Notes

These effects occur on the endpoint itself. Network access, workflow changes,
and organization-wide downtime belong to separate impact categories.
