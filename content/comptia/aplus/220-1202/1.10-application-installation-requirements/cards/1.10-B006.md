## Question

What should a technician compare when checking whether a workstation meets an
application's CPU requirement?

## Answer

Compare the installed processor and its processing speed with the application's
published CPU requirement.

## Instructor Notes

CPU demand varies by workload. Use the application's stated requirement rather
than assuming that any processor with a high clock number is compatible.
