## Question

A specialized application installs successfully but will not operate unless a
particular USB device is connected. What requirement is this device satisfying?

## Answer

An external hardware-token requirement.

## Instructor Notes

The token authorizes application use. It must remain available to the assigned
user or system, so token management affects ongoing operation as well as the
initial installation.
