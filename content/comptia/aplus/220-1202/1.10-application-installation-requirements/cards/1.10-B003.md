## Question

A 3D application requires a dedicated graphics card, but a laptop has only
integrated graphics. What requirement is unmet?

## Answer

The dedicated-graphics requirement is unmet.

## Instructor Notes

With integrated graphics, the CPU and GPU are on the same chip, and graphics
uses system memory. Dedicated, or discrete, graphics uses a separate graphics
card with its own video memory.
