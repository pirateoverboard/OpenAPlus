## Question

Before installing a graphics-intensive application, which graphics-memory
specification should a technician compare with the application's requirement?

## Answer

The graphics card's available VRAM.

## Instructor Notes

VRAM is video random-access memory used by a dedicated graphics card. Do not
substitute the computer's total system RAM for a stated VRAM requirement.
