## Question

Downtime during an application change would stop a critical process and delay
other departments that depend on its results. Which impact category is this?

## Answer

Business impact.

## Instructor Notes

Business impact captures consequences to critical processes and downstream
teams. It is broader than a single device problem or a changed user workflow.
