## Question

An application meets the workstation's CPU and RAM requirements, but its
publisher does not list the installed operating system as supported. What
requirement still blocks the installation decision?

## Answer

Application-to-OS compatibility.

## Instructor Notes

Adequate hardware does not establish software compatibility. Confirm the
supported OS and version before treating the system as ready.
