## Question

An installer fits in the available drive space, but the application will create
large project files after installation. Which storage requirement should drive
the readiness decision?

## Answer

The total space needed for the installed application and its ongoing use, not
just the installer size.

## Instructor Notes

Installation media size, installed size, and working-data growth are different.
The destination needs enough capacity for the application's practical use.
