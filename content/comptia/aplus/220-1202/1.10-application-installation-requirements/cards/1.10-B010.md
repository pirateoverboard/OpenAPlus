## Question

A technician will use a downloadable application package. Which source should
be preferred over an unrelated third-party download site?

## Answer

The software publisher's official download or a trusted centralized
application store.

## Instructor Notes

The distribution method is electronic, but the source still matters. An
unrelated third-party site adds uncertainty that a direct publisher source or
centralized application store avoids.
