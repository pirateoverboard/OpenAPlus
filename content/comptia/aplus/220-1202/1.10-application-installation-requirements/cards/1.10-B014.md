## Question

An application update changes the steps employees must follow for a
time-sensitive daily task. Which installation-impact category is this?

## Answer

Operational impact.

## Instructor Notes

Operational impact concerns how work is performed. Even a functioning
application can disrupt a workflow when its process changes.
