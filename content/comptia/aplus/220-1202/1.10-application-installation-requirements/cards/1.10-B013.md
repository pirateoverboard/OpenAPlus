## Question

A newly installed application must reach an internal service and write to a
shared folder. Which impact category requires review?

## Answer

Network impact, including service access and permissions to the share.

## Instructor Notes

Successful local installation does not prove that network dependencies are
available. Required access and share permissions must support the application's
use.
