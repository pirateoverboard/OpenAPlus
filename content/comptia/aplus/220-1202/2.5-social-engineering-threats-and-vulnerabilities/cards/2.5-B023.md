## Question

What security risk becomes critical when technology passes the point at which
its vendor provides support and security updates?

## Answer

Newly discovered vulnerabilities may remain unpatched because the vendor no
longer supplies fixes.

## Instructor Notes

The objective labels the lifecycle concern EOL. The approved notes distinguish
end of sales from the later end of service life; loss of security updates is
the decisive security issue.
