## Question

How does spoofing differ from an on-path attack?

## Answer

Spoofing falsifies an identity or source. An on-path attack intercepts and
relays communication between two parties, potentially reading or changing it.

## Instructor Notes

The techniques can work together: ARP spoofing can redirect local traffic
through an attacker and thereby establish an on-path position.
