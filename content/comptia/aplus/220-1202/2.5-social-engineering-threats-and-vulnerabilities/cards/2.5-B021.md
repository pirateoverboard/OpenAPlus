## Question

Why is an unpatched system a security vulnerability?

## Answer

It lacks available fixes, leaving known operating-system or application flaws
open to exploitation.

## Instructor Notes

Patch management includes identifying, testing, prioritizing, and deploying
updates across all systems, including easily overlooked devices.
