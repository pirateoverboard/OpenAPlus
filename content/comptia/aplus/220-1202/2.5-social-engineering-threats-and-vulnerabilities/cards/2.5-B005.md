## Question

How does whaling differ from spear phishing?

## Answer

Spear phishing targets a particular person or group; whaling is spear phishing
aimed specifically at a senior executive or other high-value leader.

## Instructor Notes

Both are targeted and may use information about the victim. The target's senior
or high-value role is what makes the attack whaling.
