## Question

What makes an attack a zero-day attack?

## Answer

It exploits a vulnerability before that vulnerability has been detected or
publicly disclosed.

## Instructor Notes

Attackers may keep an unknown flaw private so they can use it before defenders
publish details and begin remediation.
