## Question

Why can an insider threat be especially difficult to identify and contain?

## Answer

The insider may already have trusted access and knowledge of valuable systems
and normal organizational processes.

## Instructor Notes

An insider may be an employee, contractor, or recruited trusted person. The
defining advantage is legitimate access or institutional knowledge, not
advanced technical skill.
