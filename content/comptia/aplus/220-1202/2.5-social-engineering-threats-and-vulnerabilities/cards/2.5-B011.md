## Question

A malicious access point uses the same SSID and a similar captive portal as a
trusted hotspot. What threat does this describe?

## Answer

An evil twin.

## Instructor Notes

The access point imitates a legitimate network to attract users. A familiar
SSID alone does not authenticate the wireless network.
