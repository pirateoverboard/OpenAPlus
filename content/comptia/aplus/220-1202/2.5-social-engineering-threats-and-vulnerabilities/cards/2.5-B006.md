## Question

What social-engineering attack obtains sensitive information by watching a
person's screen or keyboard?

## Answer

Shoulder surfing.

## Instructor Notes

The observer may be nearby or use a camera or other distant viewing method.
Screen placement and privacy filters help reduce exposure.
