## Text

XSS expands to {{c1::Cross-Site Scripting}}.

## Extra

XSS executes attacker-supplied script in a victim's browser in the context of a
trusted site.

## Instructor Notes

The acronym uses XSS because CSS already commonly refers to Cascading Style
Sheets.
