## Question

Attackers compromise a vendor's trusted software-update process so malicious
code reaches the vendor's customers. What type of attack is this?

## Answer

A supply chain or pipeline attack.

## Instructor Notes

The attacker reaches downstream organizations through something they trust
from a supplier or provider. This differs from an insider acting from within
the target organization.
