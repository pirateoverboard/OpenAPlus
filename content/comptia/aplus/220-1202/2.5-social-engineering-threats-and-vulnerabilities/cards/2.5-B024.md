## Question

Why does bring your own device create a security challenge for an organization?

## Answer

An employee-owned device mixes personal and work use, complicating security
control, malware exposure, company-data protection, and data removal.

## Instructor Notes

The organization must protect its data while the employee owns and also uses
the device personally, including when the device is lost, sold, or replaced.
