## Question

A text message claims a package cannot be delivered until the recipient opens
a link and confirms personal information. Which phishing variant is this?

## Answer

Smishing (SMS phishing).

## Instructor Notes

The text-message channel distinguishes smishing from email phishing and
voice-based vishing.
