## Question

What distinguishes a distributed denial-of-service attack from a general
denial-of-service attack?

## Answer

A DDoS attack coordinates the disruption from many systems, while a DoS attack
does not require multiple distributed sources.

## Instructor Notes

Both target availability. DDoS commonly uses a botnet to exhaust bandwidth or
service resources from many origins at once.
