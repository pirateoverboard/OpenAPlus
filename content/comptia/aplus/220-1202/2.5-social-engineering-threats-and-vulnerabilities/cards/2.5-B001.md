## Question

What feature makes a deceptive message a phishing attack rather than merely
unsolicited email?

## Answer

It uses deception to make the recipient reveal information, follow a malicious
link, or perform another unsafe action.

## Instructor Notes

Spoofed identity or copied branding can make the lure believable, but the
human-directed attempt to obtain an action or information is the key.
