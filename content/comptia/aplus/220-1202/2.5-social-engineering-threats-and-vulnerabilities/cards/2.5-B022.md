## Question

What makes a system unprotected in the context of Objective 2.5?

## Answer

An expected security control, such as antivirus or a firewall, is missing or
disabled.

## Instructor Notes

Permanently removing a control to solve an application problem creates a new
exposure. Troubleshoot the compatibility or configuration issue instead.
