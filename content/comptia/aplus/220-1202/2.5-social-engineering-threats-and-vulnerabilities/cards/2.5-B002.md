## Question

A caller claiming to represent a bank asks a user to confirm account details.
Which phishing variant uses this voice channel?

## Answer

Vishing (voice phishing).

## Instructor Notes

Caller ID can be spoofed, so the displayed number does not authenticate the
caller.
