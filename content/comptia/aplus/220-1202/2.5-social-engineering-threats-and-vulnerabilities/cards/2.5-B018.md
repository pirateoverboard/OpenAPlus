## Question

An urgent email that appears to come from an executive directs accounting to
send payment to a new bank account. What threat should be suspected?

## Answer

Business email compromise (BEC).

## Instructor Notes

BEC abuses trust in business communications, often through spoofing,
compromised accounts, or targeted social engineering. Verify sensitive requests
through a separate trusted channel.
