## Question

An attacker claims to be from the help desk and uses technical language to
persuade an employee to disclose a code. Which social-engineering method is
central to the attack?

## Answer

Impersonation.

## Instructor Notes

The attacker pretends to hold a trusted identity or role. Authority,
familiarity, and technical detail are persuasion tools, not proof of identity.
