## Question

An unauthorized person slips through a controlled door immediately behind an
employee without the employee's consent. What attack occurred?

## Answer

Tailgating.

## Instructor Notes

The approved notes contrast tailgating with piggybacking, where the authorized
person knowingly permits the follower to enter. Neither gives the follower
independent authorization.
