## Question

Where in macOS settings would a technician configure a wired or wireless
interface's IP and DNS values?

## Answer

The Network settings area.

## Instructor Notes

Network settings cover interface-specific configuration, including automatic
or manual addressing and more detailed options. The card tests the settings
area, not a version-specific click path.
