## Question

What is the primary purpose of the macOS Dock?

## Answer

It provides quick access to applications and folders and shows which
applications are running.

## Instructor Notes

The Dock is a launch and status area rather than a file-search tool or a
multiple-desktop overview. Its placement and visibility can be customized.
