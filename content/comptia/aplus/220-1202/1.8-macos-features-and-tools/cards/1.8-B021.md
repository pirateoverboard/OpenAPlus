## Question

Which Apple feature family lets a user move work and supported capabilities
between a Mac and other Apple devices?

## Answer

Continuity.

## Instructor Notes

Continuity supports cross-device workflows such as transferring files,
forwarding communication, or using capabilities from another device. It is
coordinated through the Apple ecosystem and iCloud.
