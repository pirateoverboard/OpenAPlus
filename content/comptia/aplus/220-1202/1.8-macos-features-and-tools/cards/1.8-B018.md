## Question

A user wants supported documents and other data to remain synchronized across
their Apple devices. Which macOS-integrated service provides this function?

## Answer

iCloud.

## Instructor Notes

iCloud integrates synchronization across macOS, iOS, and iPadOS. iCloud Drive
is its cloud-file-storage component; Messages and FaceTime provide messaging
and communication services within the same ecosystem.
