## Question

What maintenance advantage does the macOS App Store provide for applications
obtained through it?

## Answer

It provides a centralized place to obtain and update those applications.

## Instructor Notes

Applications installed from other sources may use a separate update mechanism.
The App Store should not be assumed to manage every application on the Mac.
