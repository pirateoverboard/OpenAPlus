## Text

In macOS, an `.app` item is an {{c1::application bundle}}.

## Extra

It appears as one application in Finder while containing the files needed by
that application.

## Instructor Notes

This bundle design is why many self-contained applications can be removed by
moving the `.app` item to the Trash.
