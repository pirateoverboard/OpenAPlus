## Question

A technician needs to inspect securely stored passwords and certificates for a
macOS user. Which built-in feature manages this information?

## Answer

Keychain, accessed through Keychain Access.

## Instructor Notes

Keychain protects credentials, certificates, secure notes, and similar
secrets. FileVault has a different scope: it encrypts the Mac's disk rather
than serving as a credential manager.
