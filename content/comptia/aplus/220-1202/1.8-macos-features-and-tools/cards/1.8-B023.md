## Question

A company wants data on a lost Mac to remain unreadable without authorized
startup authentication. Which macOS feature should be enabled?

## Answer

FileVault full-disk encryption.

## Instructor Notes

FileVault protects data at rest across the disk. Keychain instead stores and
protects credentials, certificates, secure notes, and similar secrets within
the user's environment.
