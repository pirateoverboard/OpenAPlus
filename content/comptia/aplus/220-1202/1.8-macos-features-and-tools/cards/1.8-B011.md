## Question

Which macOS settings area lets a technician add or remove a printer or scanner
and check its status?

## Answer

Printers & Scanners.

## Instructor Notes

This area also exposes device-specific configuration and sharing options. It is
the macOS counterpart to managing these peripherals through a central settings
interface.
