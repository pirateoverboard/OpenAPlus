## Question

Which macOS settings area is used to arrange multiple monitors and adjust
resolution, brightness, or color?

## Answer

Displays.

## Instructor Notes

The stable concept is the purpose of the Displays area. Exact interface labels
and navigation paths may differ between macOS releases.
