## Question

A conferencing application cannot use the Mac's camera or microphone. Which
settings category should the technician check for per-application access?

## Answer

Privacy.

## Instructor Notes

Privacy controls limit access to sensitive data and hardware such as location,
photos, calendars, cameras, and microphones. Grant only the access required by
an approved application.
