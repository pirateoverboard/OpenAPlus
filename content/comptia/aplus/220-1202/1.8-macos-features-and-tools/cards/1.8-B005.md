## Question

Why should a company use Managed Apple IDs instead of employees' personal
Apple IDs for organization-assigned applications and content?

## Answer

Managed Apple IDs keep account and content administration under organizational
control rather than tying company resources to an employee's personal account.

## Instructor Notes

Managed Apple IDs can be administered through Apple Business Manager and
integrated with corporate identity and device-management systems. The key exam
distinction is personal ownership versus managed organizational control.
