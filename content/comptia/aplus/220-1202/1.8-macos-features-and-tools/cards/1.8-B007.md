## Question

What ongoing maintenance is necessary for antimalware software on a Mac?

## Answer

Keep the software and its threat signatures updated.

## Instructor Notes

Protection becomes less effective when detection data is stale. macOS should
not be treated as immune to malware simply because it has historically faced a
different threat volume than Windows.
