## Question

A user has NTFS permission to read a folder locally but is denied by the
folder's share permissions. What access will the user have through the network
share?

## Answer

No access; the most restrictive applicable permission wins, and deny takes
priority over allow.

## Instructor Notes

Remote access evaluates both permission layers. A permissive NTFS entry cannot
override a restrictive share result.
