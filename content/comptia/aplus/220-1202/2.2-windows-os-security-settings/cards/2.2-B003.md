## Question

A connection works only while Windows Firewall is disabled. After confirming
the firewall is involved, what should the technician do instead of leaving it
disabled?

## Answer

Re-enable the firewall and configure the narrow rule or exception required for
the connection on the appropriate network profile.

## Instructor Notes

Temporary deactivation can isolate a cause. A targeted rule restores the
needed traffic while retaining protection for unrelated traffic.
