## Question

Company laptops must keep the entire internal volume, including the operating
system and user data, unreadable if a drive is removed. Which Windows feature
should be configured?

## Answer

BitLocker.

## Instructor Notes

BitLocker protects a whole volume. A sign-in control alone would not protect
the data after the physical drive is attached to another computer.
