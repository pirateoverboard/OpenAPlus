## Question

A public-use Windows computer needs a temporary account with very limited local
access. Which built-in account type best fits?

## Answer

The Guest account.

## Instructor Notes

Guest access is deliberately limited. It is a poor fit for a named employee
who needs persistent settings or normal organizational access.
