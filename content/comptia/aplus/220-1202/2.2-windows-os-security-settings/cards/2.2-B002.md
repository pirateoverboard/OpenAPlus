## Question

Microsoft Defender is active, but its threat definitions are outdated. What
security action should a technician take?

## Answer

Check for and install the latest virus and threat protection updates.

## Instructor Notes

Real-time scanning and current definitions solve different parts of the
problem: scanning must be active, and its known-threat information must also be
kept current.
