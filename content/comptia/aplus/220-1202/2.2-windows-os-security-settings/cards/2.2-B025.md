## Question

An administrator wants to organize Active Directory users and computers by
department so policy can be applied to that hierarchy. What should be created?

## Answer

Organizational units (OUs).

## Instructor Notes

OUs provide an administrative hierarchy and policy scope. Security groups are
better suited to assigning permissions based on membership.
