## Question

A trusted application stops working while Microsoft Defender real-time
protection is active. What is a safe way to test whether Defender is involved?

## Answer

Temporarily deactivate real-time protection, perform the controlled test, and
reactivate protection immediately afterward.

## Instructor Notes

Disabling antivirus increases exposure. It is a short diagnostic step, not a
lasting compatibility fix.
