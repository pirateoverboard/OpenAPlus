## Question

An organization wants its email service hosted outside its local data center
and managed as an online productivity service. Which type of system should be
configured?

## Answer

A cloud email system.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The mail service resides in the cloud instead of on an email server maintained
in the organization's local data center. Product names can vary, but that
service-location distinction is stable.
