## Question

A sales team needs to coauthor a sequence of visual slides for a customer
briefing. Which cloud collaboration tool best fits this work?

## Answer

A cloud presentation tool.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

The required output is a slide-based briefing. Videoconferencing may deliver
the briefing remotely, but it does not replace the tool used to create the
slides.
