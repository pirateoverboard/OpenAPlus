## Question

An administrator creates a new employee in the organization's managed
directory. The account must then appear automatically in connected cloud
directories. What should be configured?

## Answer

Identity synchronization.

## Instructor Notes

### Why

Identity synchronization propagates directory changes to connected identity
systems. It reduces duplicate account maintenance, but it does not automatically
grant every licensed application.
