## Question

A new employee's synchronized cloud identity exists, but the employee cannot
use a subscription productivity application. What configuration should the
administrator verify next?

## Answer

Verify that the required cloud application license is assigned to the user.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Identity synchronization creates or updates the cloud account; license
assignment grants the user's entitlement to the subscription application. One
can succeed while the other is still missing.
