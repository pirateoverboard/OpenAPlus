## Question

Remote support staff need quick text conversations and short status updates
without scheduling a meeting. Which cloud collaboration tool best fits the
need?

## Answer

Instant messaging.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Use videoconferencing when the scenario requires a live audio-and-video
session. Short, near-real-time text exchanges point to instant messaging.
