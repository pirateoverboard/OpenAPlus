## Question

A distributed team needs a scheduled live meeting in which remote participants
can see and hear one another. Which cloud collaboration tool should be used?

## Answer

A videoconferencing tool.

## Instructor Notes

### Exam Trap

Instant messaging supports quick text exchanges, but the requirement for a live
audio-and-video meeting identifies videoconferencing.
