## Question

When a hard drive is destroyed by drilling, what must the drill pass through to
damage the data-bearing media?

## Answer

The drive platters, not merely the outer case.

## Instructor Notes

The purpose of drilling is physical media damage. A hole that affects only the
enclosure does not accomplish that goal.
