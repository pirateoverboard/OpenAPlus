## Question

A company must remove all existing data from a working drive and then reuse the
drive. Should it choose logical wiping or physical destruction?

## Answer

Logical wiping.

## Instructor Notes

Physical destruction makes the drive unusable. A whole-drive wipe addresses
the stored data while preserving a working device for recycling or
repurposing.
