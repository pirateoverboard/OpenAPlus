## Question

A magnetic hard drive must be sanitized and permanently rendered unusable by
removing its magnetic field. Which method should be used?

## Answer

Degaussing.

## Instructor Notes

Degaussing acts on magnetic storage. It is destructive to a hard drive rather
than a reuse-oriented erasing method.
