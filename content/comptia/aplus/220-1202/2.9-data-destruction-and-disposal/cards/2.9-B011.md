## Question

At the source's stated Windows scope, which standard-format option overwrites
the drive's sectors with zeros while keeping the drive usable?

## Answer

A regular format.

## Instructor Notes

A quick format clears the file table but does not overwrite the old data. The
regular option is the reuse-oriented choice when sector overwriting is
required.
