## Question

A third-party vendor destroys or recycles an organization's drives. What
document should the organization require as evidence of the completed work?

## Answer

A certificate of destruction or recycling.

## Instructor Notes

Outsourcing can provide equipment and scale, but the organization still needs
a record of the result. The certificate creates the paper trail for what
happened to the media.
