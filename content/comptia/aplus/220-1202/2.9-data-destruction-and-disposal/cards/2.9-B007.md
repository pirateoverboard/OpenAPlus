## Question

A technician overwrites one sensitive file on a drive. What important
limitation distinguishes this from a whole-drive wipe?

## Answer

The other files on the drive remain available.

## Instructor Notes

File-level overwriting has a narrow scope. It does not satisfy a requirement to
sanitize every previously stored item before the drive leaves the
organization.
