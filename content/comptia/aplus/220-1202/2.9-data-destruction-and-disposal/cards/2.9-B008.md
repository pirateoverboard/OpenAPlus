## Question

A working drive will be assigned to a new purpose, and all of its current data
must be removed. Which erasure scope matches this requirement?

## Answer

A whole-drive wipe.

## Instructor Notes

A file-level overwrite affects only selected files. Whole-drive wiping matches
the full-media scope while leaving the drive available for reuse.
