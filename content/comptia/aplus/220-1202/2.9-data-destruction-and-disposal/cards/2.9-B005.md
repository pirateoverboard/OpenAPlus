## Question

Why is degaussing not an effective destruction method for an SSD or other flash
storage?

## Answer

Flash storage does not store data with the magnetic field that degaussing
removes.

## Instructor Notes

Match the destruction method to the media technology. If flash media must be
physically destroyed, do not choose degaussing; use an approved method
compatible with nonmagnetic storage.
