## Question

An organization is retiring many drives and wants heavy machinery to break the
media into pieces so the drives cannot be reused. Which destruction method
matches this requirement?

## Answer

Shredding.

## Instructor Notes

Shredding is a physical-destruction method. It fits a permanent retirement
decision, unlike wiping or a regular format that preserves a usable drive.
