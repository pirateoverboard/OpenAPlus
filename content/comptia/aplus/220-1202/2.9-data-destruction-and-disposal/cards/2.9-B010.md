## Question

Why should a quick standard format not be treated as proof that a drive's old
data was sanitized?

## Answer

It clears file-system indexing information without overwriting the existing
data, so recovery may still be possible.

## Instructor Notes

The word *format* does not by itself establish sanitization. The overwrite
behavior distinguishes quick formatting from the regular option described
by the source.
