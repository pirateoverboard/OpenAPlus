## Question

Before selecting or outsourcing a storage-disposal method, what external
requirements must the organization identify?

## Answer

The applicable regulatory and environmental requirements.

## Instructor Notes

Local rules and policies may legally require storage drives to be destroyed.
The applicable regulatory and environmental requirements must be checked
rather than assuming one universal process.
