## Question

Why is low-level formatting not a normal end-user data-destruction choice?

## Answer

It is a factory-provided process rather than a recommended user operation.

## Instructor Notes

Do not choose low-level formatting merely because its name sounds more
thorough. For reuse, compare approved wiping and regular formatting based
on their actual overwrite behavior.
